Chapter 235 - 3: Breakthrough

The humid, dark interior of a secluded karst cave offered the three women their first true sanctuary since the absolute erasure of the Dark Valley.

Outside, the distorted remnants of the spatial fabric still hummed with a terrifying, high-frequency static, but inside, the only sounds were the slow, steady drops of mineral water hitting the stone floor and the ragged, synchronous breathing of the survivors.

Huo Yue, Ziyan, and Medusa rested against the damp cavern walls, their bodies slick with sweat and grey ash, their breathing heavily fluctuating as the immense adrenaline of the spatial erasure finally began to recede from their nervous systems.

"That was... remarkably, deeply satisfying," Huo Yue spoke, her voice echoing softly against the stalactites.

She leaned her head back against the cold stone, a serene, almost detached smile playing on her lips.

Every single ounce of the suffocating, toxic anger she had felt over the past year, the nightmares of her master’s capture, the helplessness, the burning need for retribution, had finally, completely been released with that single, casual downward sword slash.

It was as if her cultivation pathways had been thoroughly scrubbed clean by the void.

Medusa remained standing near the entrance of the cave, her arms crossed firmly over her chest as her slitted golden pupils stared out into the fractured horizon.

Her mind was still completely locked, replaying the non-Euclidean geometry of that attack over and over again.

"So that is the true, unvarnished power of a Supreme Emperor..." the Lamia Queen muttered, a rare trace of profound reverence and cold dread lacing her tone. "That thing didn’t just bypass their spatial dimensions but even treated the very concept of a Heavenly Saint’s existence as an error to be deleted. It was truly, utterly terrifying. To think that a mortal can actually possesses power of that caliber..."

To the side, Zhu Ziyan let out a weary sigh, gently patting the thick, blood-stained fur of her winged white tiger.

With a soft click of her fingers, her eyes suddenly flared with a feral, bright radiance.

The massive beast let out a low, content purr before its physical form dissolved into a swirling vortex of white motes of light, entering directly into her Tyrannical Tiger Subduing Eyes.

The ancient, inherited pupil technique contained an entire separate, hyper-spatial dimension specifically designed to house, nurture, and heal her subdued primordial beasts.

Once the tiger was safely resting, Ziyan turned her focused gaze back toward the center of the cave, where the fragile, translucent jade orb containing Liu Ruyan’s fading soul hovered silently in the air.

"Now," Huo Yue’s expression instantly shifted, the lazy satisfaction vanishing to make way for a solemn, unyielding resolve as she stood up, her wine red eyes locked onto the shivering soul of her guide. "It’s finally time to awaken my master and reforge her mortal vessel."

Huo Yue did not hesitate.

With a sweeping wave of her arm, her high-grade spatial ring flared with a brilliant crimson light, and a vast, dazzling array of incredibly rare spiritual herbs, ancient minerals, and supreme materials cascaded onto the cavern floor, instantly transforming the dark stone cave into a blinding treasury of alchemical wealth.

The sheer concentration of spiritual energy radiating from the items caused the air inside the cave to turn into a thick, sweet mist.

First, she retrieved the Nine-Life Nirvana Lotus, a legendary plant that only grew in the deepest volcanic rifts of the Central Region, its petals pulsing with a vivid, crimson lifeforce capable of anchoring a shattered soul to the mortal plane.

Beside it, she placed three stalks of the Soul-Nurturing Divine Grass, each blade shimmering with a soft silver dew that could systematically repair the deep, microscopic tears left behind by the Shadow Hall’s horrific soul-torture arrays.

To forge the physical foundation of the new body, Huo Yue brought out the Starlight Bone-Forging Marrow, a dense, iridescent liquid harvested from the remains of an ancient Behemoth, alongside a pristine chunk of Seven-Color Myriad Dao Jade, which would serve as the core skeletal framework to ensure the new body possessed an innate affinity for the laws of the universe.

She didn’t stop there as she systematically added the Nine-Transformation Yang Spirit Pill, a supreme alchemical treasure she had stolen from some random man in an auction, and a vial of Primordial Phoenix Essence Blood to ignite the dormant meridians.

"Master, endure this final trial," Huo Yue whispered.

With a sharp movement of her fingers, her four Heavenly Flames erupted from her body, forming a magnificent, multi-colored alchemical furnace made of flames in the center of the cave.

She threw the mountain of spiritual herbs and materials into the heart of the conflagration, utilizing her supreme control to liquefy the treasures into a glowing, rainbow-colored matrix of raw, divine genesis.

The translucent jade orb shattered, and Liu Ruyan’s shivering, fraying soul was gently drawn into the center of the molten liquid.

*ROAR!*

The four flames hissed as the Imperial Flame Transformation Technique operated at its absolute maximum capacity.

The liquid matrix began to rapidly wrap around the silver soul, weaving new, unbreakable meridians out of the phoenix blood and crystallizing a perfect, jade-like skeletal structure from the Myriad Dao Jade.

The cavern walls began to crack from the sheer density of the ascending life force.

*BOOM!*

The moment the new physical body completed its synthesis, the sky directly above the isolated mountain range completely changed.

The serene morning air was instantly replaced by a colossal, swirling vortex of dark, bruised-purple tribulation clouds that blanketed the territory for hundreds of miles.

The heavy, suffocating pressure of the Heavenly Tribulation descended like a physical mountain, intent on striking down this newly forged entity that dared to defy the natural cycle of death and rebirth.

"It’s a Heavenly Tribulation!" Medusa hissed, "Quick! Let’s get out of its range or we will all die!"

Medusa and Ziyan and immediately flew outside the cave, traveling miles away in an instant, however, Huo Yue remained, staring at the scene in front of her.

From the center of the alchemical fire, a majestic figure slowly stepped out, and Huo Yue couldn’t stop her eyes from tearing up.

Liu Ruyan had returned!

Her new body was flawless, a face so beautiful no one would doubt if she claimed to be a fairy from the immortal realm, with her long silver hair cascading down her pale, porcelain shoulders, and her eyes opened to reveal two sky blue pupils.

At this moment, she didn’t look like a fragile ghost anymore, now she carried the unyielding aura of a sovereign.

"Master!" Huo Yue exclaimed as she immediately rushed towards the woman and hugged her.

Liu Ruyan was still in a daze, but hearing that familiar voice and that warm hug, her features immediately softened.

"Girl, it seems this old lady now you her life."

Huo Yue shook her head, "No! Master, this is my duty as your disciple!"

Liu Ruyan chuckled, "Ah, I have made many mistakes in the past. But, taking you as a disciple, was certainly the most correct choice I had ever made."

*Rumble!*

Liu Ruyan blinked, before patting Huo Yue on the back, "Alright, we can catch up later. For now, leave, or you will be caught up in my tribulation."

Huo Yue broke the hug and nodded before a pair of crimson flame wings appeared on her back as she flew out of the cave.

At that exact moment...

*CRACK!*

The first three strikes of the tribulation lightning, colossal pillars of violet energy, shattered the roof of the cave, blasting directly down onto Liu Ruyan’s exposed head.

The impact was deafening, but the Nine-Life Nirvana Lotus dissolved within her bloodstream, instantly generating an unyielding shield of green lifeforce that absorbed the kinetic fury of the lightning, converting the raw destruction into structural stability for her new skin.

*CRACK!*

*CRACK!*

Then, the fourth, fifth, and sixth strikes descended simultaneously, transforming the mountain into a pillar of pure, chaotic electricity.

The lightning began to target her internal organs and her newly woven meridians.

Liu Ruyan let out a low, sharp grunt of pain, but the Starlight Bone-Forging Marrow and the Seven-Color Myriad Dao Jade within her bones flared into brilliance.

Her skeletal framework absorbed the heavenly lightning, using the destructive energy to permanently temper her bones into an indestructible, saint-grade material.

"Come then, oh heavens!" Liu Ruyan roared, her voice ringing out with a heavy, bell-like clarity as she looked up through the shattered ceiling.

The seventh and eighth strikes were not standard lightning; they were formed from the absolute Void Annihilation Thunder, shaped like colossal dragons that tore through the spatial barriers to devour her soul.

Far away, Huo Yue watched with her hands clenched, but the Soul-Nurturing Divine Grass and the residual energy of the Nine-Transformation Yang Spirit Pill detonated within her master’s dantian.

A brilliant, defensive matrix of silver soul-force erupted outward, meeting the lightning dragons head-on and systematically digesting their destructive laws to expand her own sea of consciousness.

Finally, the clouds turned a deep, light-absorbing black as the ninth, and final, strike prepared its descent.

It was the Sovereign Tribulation Spear, a localized execution bolt containing the absolute mandate of the world’s law to kill the anomaly.

*BOOM!*

The sky split wide open as the black spear rammed directly into Liu Ruyan’s chest.

The shockwave blew not just Huo Yue but Medusa and Ziyan back into the sky several miles away as the entire mountain range collapsing into a pile of rubble around them.

But through the thick, swirling dust of the destroyed cave, a glowing figure emerged, her power causing the very air itself to tremble.

Liu Ruyan stood tall amidst the debris, her hand casually grabbing the tip of the disintegrating black lightning spear, her skin glowing with a flawless, crystalline white radiance.

Through the profound help of the supreme spiritual herbs and materials her disciple had provided, her body had successfully withstood all nine strikes without a single meridian fracturing.

The dark tribulation clouds above instantly shattered into millions of golden particles, raining down onto her form as a divine reward from the universe.

Her aura didn’t just stabilize, but it violently exploded past the barriers of the mortal plane, establishing a permanent, unshakeable link to the earth’s core laws.

She had done it.

Liu Ruyan had successfully broken through to the absolute Earthly Saint Realm upon her very rebirth, her presence radiating a dense, world shattering authority that made her one of the premier powerhouses of the Central Region.

At that moment, Huo Yue, Medusa and Ziyan flew back and landed in front of her.

"Master, congratulations on your breakthrough!"

Liu Ruyan smiled, "Thank you, Yue. It is all thanks to your help."

Now, in the Central Region, she can already be considered a powerhouse! And it is all thanks to her disciple!

Indeed, the greatest choice she had ever made in this life, was taking Huo Yue as her disciple.
