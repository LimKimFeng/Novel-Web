Chapter 102: The Curse

Dawn Minion had failed to complete their task, their assassins disappeared, who were directly assumed dead since they didn’t return until the morning.

The assassin group of course became more cautious, but they didn’t send more assassins to this mission immediately and decided to get more information first. Unknowingly, they had actually looked down upon their target and had paid the price for it.

But this was just the start...

******

Vritra sat in one of the corners of the cave, hungrily devouring the demon meat from his inventory. His body had already recovered to the peak state after resting for a bit—both his mind and body.

BOOOOOOOOM!!

Every few seconds, the sound of loud explosions rang out that shook the whole place. BOB moved according to Vritra’s instructions, rolling at full thrust and ramming into BOB 2 with destructive force without holding back.

BOB 2 wasn’t unscathed when he previously fought with the alive BOB, and now under the effects of Forbidden and Blood Lock, the situation had completely changed. Although at first BOB 2 was still at a slight advantage, soon it began to lose ground.

Vritra just had to control BOB. All the injuries it received upon clashes were instantly healed by using its energy, which is one of the perks of a soul wraith. When its energy got dangerously low, Vritra just needed to fill it back by transferring his mana, which he could do without even touching when they were in close proximity.

"Sigh, I don’t even know what to expect anymore. I have never heard of anyone killing a legion or Myriad when there is such a large level difference. Just level near 500 killing a Myriad? Well two, considering another one is about to get added to the list.

That kind of sounds absurd, even if you had used tactics to do so," Yasmine spoke, shaking her head. A big credit of which went to his fast thinking, perfect calculations, and those terrifying abilities.

"Heh, soon we will have two such strong soul wraiths. I guess I can now go all out with my revenge. I have delayed it for far too long already," Vritra mumbled, thinking of all the people who had nearly got him killed.

From king, commander, guards, his classmates, and not to mention... the Nine Pacts—all of them would be paying the price for it.

One or so hour had passed, and BOB 2’s combat strength and every ability had been weakened by more than thirty percent, as it began to lose very badly against BOB.

"Wait, now that I think about it, why hasn’t the message popped out yet? Haven’t I already killed one of them? Shouldn’t the quest already be clear?" Vritra thought in confusion.

"Yes, the quest is completed, but it doesn’t show so yet, which means the quest will most probably be upgraded since you would be slaying both the BOBs—which just means a higher reward for us, hubby, hehe," Yasmine replied with a giggle. She still felt a little weak, but soon she would be fine.

"That’s good to know, but... sigh, this is just the second plane and already so dangerous. I wonder how many more challenges I will have to go through after this. Oh right, I still have to find the door to move to the next plane still."

Vritra stretched his arms and pondered where the door could be. He had already passed through so many caves but had not seen anything out of the ordinary.

"Ah right, what is that field thing in BOB’s status panel? And why were its stats so high?" Vritra asked curiously.

"Those stats are perfectly fine. After crossing through the thousand-level mark, the stats increase exponentially.

My stats were much higher when I had reached that level a long time ago. Anyway, that field— it is a bit hard to explain— but it is kind of a mastery in anything.

It could be elemental attributes, abilities, or anything, like your dust abilities which are comprehended once you have arrived at level 1000. This field greatly defines how strong someone will be in the later stages," Yasmine explained.

"I see, I wonder what it will be for me. Anyway, I am curious about one more thing... these quests, do all people receive them or is it kind of different with me because of any of my abilities?" he questioned.

"Yeah, others receive them too, but they are kind of rare. But you just arrive at special places, so you already got two. Normally, people wouldn’t even see one in their life," she answered calmly.

Yasmine had previously cleared a few quests, so she didn’t think too much about it—but others would have been shocked by this.

"...Uhm, do you happen to know who exactly is controlling this all? Is it that divine, or the true gods?" Vritra asked hesitantly. He had always tried not to think of this topic.

Yasmine remained silent for a few seconds, then she replied: "... I am not sure either, but even the true gods—those terrifying entities—can’t control all of this. They could give abilities, curses, and such things to people, but they can’t even control the status window.

As for this Divine, I think it should be someone on the level of those Gods." She added, but didn’t want to talk about this topic for now, since if she accidentally said anything she shouldn’t, then Vritra might be exposed to those gods—both good and evil—and might even be punished for no reason.

Vritra nodded and didn’t continue the topic.

After over ten minutes, finally, the long-awaited messages popped in front of Vritra.

++++++++++++

[You have killed a Level 1011 Ball of Brutality.]

[+EXP Earned.]

[You have Levelled Up!]

[You have Levelled Up!]

[You have Levelled Up!]

...

[All Stats Increased by 1.]

[All Stats Increased by 1.]

[All Stats Increased by 1.]

...

[You have gained 1 Allocation Point.]

[You have gained 1 Allocation Point.]

[You have gained 1 Allocation Point.]

...

++++++++++++

The moment BOB 2 had died, an invisible change occurred in the entire space as a wave passed through the entire plane. No one noticed it, not even Vritra or Yasmine, as it swept over all the people present in the plane.

Vritra’s complete focus was on the messages that kept popping one after another, without stopping for a few seconds.

++++++++++++

[Quest: Survive against and Kill both of the Balls of Brutality has been completed.]

[Reward: You have obtained two Myriad Armors, made from the Balls of Brutality, corresponding to their levels; with protection of their field abilities.]

++++++++++++

And then...

[The Cursed Hunter Activated!!]

[The Divine’s curse has been negated.]

Vritra was again filled with confusion by several things, as he muttered in surprise.

"Wait, both the BOBs were at the same level—then why was there such a big difference in their strength? No wait, before that—I received two armors? Field abilities have been added as well for protection? No no no, before all that, what is this curse? Did it get activated because I had killed them both?"

Yasmine was surprised as well. She wasn’t affected since she didn’t possess a body, but thankfully, Vritra was saved because of that title. It wasn’t a simple title—obtaining it was near impossible for most.
