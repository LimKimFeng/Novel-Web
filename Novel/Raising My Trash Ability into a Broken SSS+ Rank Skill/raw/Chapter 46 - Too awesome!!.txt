Chapter 46: Too awesome!!

Yasmine almost fell down as Vritra disappeared. She was startled as she sensed her surroundings but couldn’t locate him anywhere; his scent, aura, Vritra’s very presence had completely disappeared.

She could still feel her bond with him, which meant he was still alive, but nowhere near her. It was really like a different space had swallowed him.

"H-Husband? Are you there?" she called out. She had experienced so much just for one day, and she no longer wanted to play with him for today.

On the other hand, Vritra was even more panicked; as soon as the skill was activated, he really disappeared from the real world and entered a separate space where only emptiness existed.

He could still hear and see the real world but couldn’t touch anything. As the land under his feet disappeared, Vritra began to fall down and couldn’t hold onto anything. He saw Yasmine’s face for a moment before he disappeared into the ground and continued to plummet.

"What the hell? Yasmine?" he shouted, but his voice couldn’t be heard in the real world, and Vritra had no idea how to go up.

By the time he came to his senses, his vision was only filled with mud as he kept falling. The only choice he had was to deactivate this skill, but Vritra hesitated. He was under the land; if he deactivated it now, he was afraid he would be embedded in the mud and end up dying.

But there was no other choice. Just as he was about to deactivate it, a few messages appeared in front of him.

[Mutamorphis Activated.]

[Analysing body condition...]

[Analysing the environment and nearby threats...]

[Automatic Morphological Adaptation Triggered...]

[...]

[You have acquired the active skill: Fly.]

Finally, Vritra was able to stop and float freely in the air. He finally had a chance to observe this new skill calmly.

First, he flew upward and arrived back into the death canopy, where Yasmine was still looking for him. He floated in front of her, but she still couldn’t sense him. Vritra simply passed through her; it seemed aside from two of his senses, he really didn’t exist in the real world at all.

’Then isn’t this a great defensive and spying skill? I wonder if there is a time limit. Will the attack pass through my body as well?’ he wondered and deactivated the skill, appearing exactly in front of Yasmine.

"You are back!! Oh, you can fly now? So the skill is exactly as its name says. Were you able to hear and see the real world?" Yasmine asked once he became visible.

"Yes, in that state I can watch and see everything around me," Vritra answered.

Then the two decided to do a few experiments.

After staying for around twenty or so minutes in that state, all of Vritra’s mana ran out, and he was back to reality. Aside from the high mana consumption there was no short coming of this great skill.

Also, his fear was for naught. If his skill was deactivated in the ground, it would have simply pushed the mud away, and he would be stuck there.

"This is a great skill, but it won’t be so useful against opponents who are too strong that they could break space or those that could control the space attribute," Yasmine spoke after observing it.

"With this skill, didn’t my defense become much stronger? If I were to face Diana now, she won’t even be able to touch me." Vritra laughed, very much satisfied with Fourth Dimension.

Now it was time to see the next skill. Hopefully, it would be just as good. Hybrid... does that mean it is both active and passive? While pondering, Vritra looked at the status window again.

++++++++++++++++

[HYBRID SKILLS]:

{Shift}

(Skill Description):

When one isn’t enough, take two. When strength or agility or defence is lacking just borrow from the other, be careful what you choose. Healing, mana regeneration, mental recovery will automatically support one another, two could be added to the third.

++++++++++++++++

"Hmm, is it what I think it is?" Vritra wondered.

"I think so, give it a try," Yasmine encouraged him.

After thinking for a moment, Vritra activated the new skill.

++++++++++++++++

Strength: 1151 Agility: 672

++++++++++++++++

...

[Shift Activated.]

[Strength→ Agility]

...

++++++++++++++++

Strength: 10 Agility: 1813

++++++++++++++++

Immediately, Vritra felt extremely weak, but his reaction speed, thinking speed, raw speed—all of them had increased by several times.

Vritra moved forward and in an instant covered more than a hundred meters. He kept the skill activated for a while, and there was no consumption of any energy. This was an even more incredible skill that had directly increased Vritra’s strength greatly.

Not to mention, his healing and mana regeneration had increased. As for mental recovery, he didn’t know the effect yet, but the passive section was also great.

"So I can just add however much of the stat of one attribute to the other I want, and it also works passively, letting two regenerations support the third, that is incredible," Vritra exclaimed, since mana regeneration has increased then the Shift will also support the Fourth Dimension skill, letting it last even longer.

Now one of his great worries was finally resolved. He always felt that his agility was lacking behind, and before adding allocation points, he had to think a lot and wait for the situation, but now there was no need to worry.

"..." Yasmine was baffled by such a godly skill. She was the one to tell him that at lower level, even with high stats, it gets increasingly difficult to defeat higher-level opponents, but with such a skill, he could easily cross that limit of levels.

It is already so powerful, but once his level increases to a very high number, then once this skill is used, won’t Vritra’s poor enemies be slaughtered without any idea how they died?

Leaving his dragon wife to her thoughts, Vritra, who was overjoyed by the two skills, decided to check out the treasure maps. He focussed on the two scrolls in his mind and inserted some form of energy into them.

The two scrolls unfurled and, on their surface, thousands of lines began to flash as if they had started to search for the treasures. A few seconds passed in silence, and finally, the two maps showed extremely detailed locations of two different treasures. He read their names and opened his eyes.

Yasmine, who was back to her senses after being shocked for a while, looked at Vritra and asked:

"So, which treasures did they display?"

"The first is the Inheritance of the Scarlet Couple," Vritra answered, wondering if she would know something about it.

"Oh that..." Yasmine didn’t seem surprised. After a moment, she nodded and spoke: "That is indeed a great treasure, of the level of top ten Mythic Halos, but there is a problem."

"What problem?" Vritra asked. He knew getting such treasures wasn’t an easy task.

"The location of that inheritance is already known by the world, and hence the people that will come there will be extremely strong. When the inheritance ground opens, that place will be extremely chaotic.

Even when I was at my peak, I would think twice before going there." Yasmine warned.
