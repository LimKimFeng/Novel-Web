Vritra stared at the new system menu while feasting on the demonic meat. Almost three days had passed since the fight with those three organizations; his body had completely healed and even his life force was replenished.

He cast a long shadow over the cave’s wall, his two horns making him appear like a true devil.

It seemed the Divine Sin Arrow’s main ability was to allow him to upgrade various of his skills, and he could now even purchase skill points and luck points.

It was a great change — with enough sin points, he could upgrade all his skills and buy a lot of skill points and luck points. And when his luck reached high enough, he would be able to draw skills so easily.

Though everything seemed quite expensive, as long as he could find a big crowd, earning those sin points would be a piece of cake.

Wiping the blood from the corner of his lips, Vritra gulped the last bite and drank some water.

"Wifey, why has my level paused at 999? Surely after killing those two 2nd Legion-ranked men I should be much higher," he asked and stood up, stretching his body as cracking sounds rang out.

"It’s a huge jump to that level. A major part of your experience points has been stored, and to evolve you would first need a 1st Grade Legion Stone," Yasmine explained.

"Is that too rare to find?" Vritra walked out of the cave as all the animals around the cave ran away in panic just from the faint aura that leaked out of his body.

"Not really, but the Legion Stone is based on a person’s field. While in your case, your leveling up was too fast, so I don’t know what might be your field.

Normally, people form strong connections with a certain ability, element, or anything, but for you, it can be dust, spatial field, fire, demonic energy, or many others. So we would have to search for a 1st Grade Empty Legion Stone. It can be used for any ability, though it’s a little rare," she replied.

[Intra Shuffle Activated.]

The horns over his head twisted and soon disappeared — he couldn’t walk around humans with horns above his head after all. And while he was at it, he even changed his facial features slightly.

There was a high chance that he already might be a little popular, and evil organizations’ favorite thing is to eliminate enemies through a borrowed knife, so all the good organizations would come to hunt him if they heard even a little about him.

After he was done with his little preparation, Vritra flew into the sky and looked around, then he focused his gaze once again on the new system menu and decided to add all the allocation points to intelligence.

Now that he could just purchase the skill points, he no longer had to be very careful with them.

"Now that this is done, it is finally time to head into the Blue Kingdom. I have to find Mom, Fiona, and Diana soon and also that empty Legion Stone. I can’t remain too weak for long," Vritra mumbled to himself.

He planned to annihilate all the people who had managed to escape very soon.

Since Bobby was still perfectly fine, he wasn’t as much worried about them, but looking down at his body, Vritra sighed in regret.

His armor was broken in a single strike from Blunder. If it was still fine, then he might have been able to upgrade it too in the future — but whatever was lost was lost, and he had gained a lot too.

The two men from the Bone Blood Cult were still lying as corpses in his inventory, waiting to be turned into soul wraiths, but with his current strength, Vritra couldn’t do that task. So as soon as he reaches the Legion rank, he would have two bodyguards way stronger than himself.

Not to mention those thousands of men he had killed who would increase the strength of his soul wraiths.

Hoooo.

Taking a deep breath, Vritra focused on the two hearts in his chest as they began to beat faster and faster, pumping blood throughout his body.

THUMP THUMP THUMP

Purple veins became sharply visible over his pale skin. In the next moment, Vritra shot forward, releasing a loud explosive sonic boom. Just those hearts seemed to have increased his already high agility by several times — Vritra tore through the air at a terrifying speed.

Many low-level 1st Legion-ranked people wouldn’t be as fast as Vritra was already. There was a devastating difference between his speed now and just three days ago.

Seriously though, the two hearts were one of the biggest treasures that he had received after his transformation. None of the poisons could affect him now, nor could most internal attacks like the one from Blunder.

[Fourth Dimension Activated.]

Like a rocket, Vritra flew through the air but soon entered a different space. He didn’t plan to announce his arrival to everyone so openly — the Blue Kingdom was several times more dangerous than the Dunshire Kingdom.
