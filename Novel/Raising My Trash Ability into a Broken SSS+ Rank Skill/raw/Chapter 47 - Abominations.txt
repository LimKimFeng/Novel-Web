Chapter 47: Abominations

"Even when I was at my peak, I would think twice before going there... it is just too dangerous. The people of my level, even demon lords, might arrive there. The treasure will undoubtedly be great, yes, but so will be the danger.

Even with these new skills and your increasing strength, you wouldn’t last there for more than a few minutes, so let’s give up on that, it’s just too dangerous." Yasmine advised.

Then asked: "So what is the second treasure?"

"Heh, in the same kingdom as the Scarlet couple inheritance, the blue kingdom is where the second treasure is located as well. The 20th ranked Mythic Halo, Misty Essence, is located there in some nameless inheritance." Vritra answered.

He wasn’t discouraged since he couldn’t get the first treasure, he didn’t want to throw his life away because of overconfidence so he completely disregarded the 1st treasure map.

"Misty Essence?!! It is located in the blue kingdom? Even I have never heard of this news, then surely that thing is hidden from everyone. This is great, we will head to the Blue kingdom next.

Even though that Mythic Halo is ranked at the last, it is solely because it couldn’t deal any damage directly, but if it is used correctly then it could bring disasters." Yasmine’s emotions were like the graph of sine, constantly going up and down.

"Yes, there is still quite some time before these inheritances open and both of them will open at the same time. Our next destination is Blue kingdom." Vritra said, then he remembered another thing.

’How do I earn those Sin points to get more such skills?’ While he was pondering, he looked at the messages that he had ignored earlier.

{Sin Point Gained: Awestruck +1}

{Sin Point Gained: Shocked +1}

{Sin Point Gained: Watched +1}

...

"!!!" He saw several such messages and then looked at the lower section of his status menu and immediately understood the method to earn Sin points.

{Sin points: 11}

’So it is based on the reactions of people around me to all of my actions. Won’t that take too long to earn the Sin points—wait, if there are a lot of people then it would be no problem.’ He pondered. All of these points had come from Yasmine.

He decided to worry about this later and focus on clearing the Death Canopy. Finally, he closed his status window and stretched his arm. He had gained a lot, now it was time to clear the rest of the waves.

Just as he turned around, Vritra was reminded that he had asked the Soul Wraiths to devour each other and had forgotten to stop them. Now only six terrifying-looking demons remained in front of him.

"Sigh. I was planning to keep a few of each type. Anyway, let’s see how it turned out." Vritra mumbled. Just by appearance, all of them looked a lot stronger and deadlier. Even the single goblin who had a height of over 6 feet looked burly and buffed out.

First he looked at the levels of all his Soul Wraiths. Yasmine had told him that devouring gives the wraiths more EXP than just killing others, and the EXP required for these Soul Wraiths is several times lower than him, so leveling up is much easier for them—several times at that.

So he expected them to reach high levels, but upon looking at these Soul Wraiths, he was completely baffled and just stared at them with a dumbfounded expression.

"Yes, raising Soul Wraith is indeed as big a cheat as your other abilities. Just feed them strong souls and they will level up quickly." Yasmine said. His growth rate was increasing at a chilling rate.

Vritra smiled as his shock left. No wonder Nihil-Anima challenged the 2nd-ranked Mythic Halo and even fought one of the Demon Gods. Its army alone is so dangerous. Excited about adding four more warriors to this matchup, he looked at Yasmine and said:

"Let’s start the next wave."

"Yes, now you will also be able to use these guys to fight along with you. Your killing efficiency will increase greatly, so the number of high-level demons you kill will increase greatly. Which means more levels and stronger Soul Wraiths. Hmm hmm, indeed it’s too disturbing." Yasmine instantly called for the next wave.

Now Vritra wasn’t planning to stop until he had killed all the demons in the Death Canopy.
