After leaving the temple, Vritra bought a unique set of clothes that was quite eye-catching, and after wearing them, he arrived at the arena where the recruitment would take place. Finding the place wasn’t difficult at all.

The news of Diana’s appearance in that competition had already spread in just a couple of hours. A lot of people were hurrying to go there. As Vritra watched such a crowd heading towards the arena from the sky, he felt quite excited.

"Do my current appearance look weird?" Vritra asked. He was wearing a red dress along with a cape, with a mask that perfectly hid his face and a red wig — a typical cliché superhero costume.

Four words were written on his chest and two on his cape, saying:

-LAST NIGHT, I FUCKED-

-YOUR MOTHER-

"Yes, you look extremely weird. Why are you using a mask? Won’t your looks get you even more sin points?" Yasmine chuckled at his comical appearance. He looked too funny, and others would be easily offended just by looking at him.

"Uh... to be honest, it is quite embarrassing, especially everything I will be doing there. Sigh... getting sin points isn’t an easy task. Thankfully, the rewards are very much comforting," Vritra said, shaking his head. He calmed himself while thinking about Shift, Four Dimension, and the two treasure maps — he could obtain something even better next time.

"Hehe, you will certainly attract everyone’s attention. Let me know if you need my help," Yasmine spoke. She was looking forward to this good show.

Vritra nodded and flew towards the arena. The place was quite big, enough to accommodate more than ten thousand people. He went directly to register.

Several people were doing the work of registration, so despite the large crowd planning to participate and who hadn’t registered yet, the queues weren’t long.

[Fourth Dimension Deactivated.]

Vritra’s figure became visible. He joined one of the queues and waited patiently. He had already started to attract quite a few gazes.

{Sin Point Gained: Observed +48}

{Sin Point Gained: Surprised +57}

{Sin Point Gained: Glared at +71}

Vritra ignored all the gazes; he would only start his act after the fight started. Soon, it was his turn. The person responsible for registration passed him a form and asked him to fill it without even raising his gaze.

"Haha, I think all the people in here already hate you. This dress of yours is wonderful; it is simply a point-grabbing machine." Yasmine was already laughing so hard. Staying with Vritra was like experiencing a completely packed film.

There was action, adventure, comedy, love, taboo love, ecchi things, despair, and much more. She would never be bored while with him.

Vritra looked at the form while receiving a few dozen sin points every few seconds just by standing there. The form was pretty simple — he had to write his name, level, and address.

"What among these things will be announced on the stage?" Vritra asked.

"Your name and level," the man with dark circles under his eyes said, his voice full of irritation.

"What is the normal level of warriors participating?" Vritra asked.

"Tch, it’s the guards for the castle. The minimum level is 100." The man sounded really annoyed, but he still answered while writing something in the register.

"Hmm, is there a word limit for the name? Will the full name be announced before the fight?" Vritra asked, irritating even the people who were queuing behind him.

"You!! There is no word limit and yes, the whole name will be announced—" He finally became enraged, and while answering, he looked up and was shocked by Vritra’s appearance.

{Sin Point Gained: Enraged +39}

{Sin Point Gained: Surprised +1}

"Alright, thank you. Give me a minute." Vritra didn’t mind the angry man. As long as he was receiving these points, he quickly began to fill the form.

The registrar took several deep breaths to calm himself down. There were always a few crazy people like that. He had already been working for several hours by now, and getting angry would only make him sick.

There was no checking of the level, since if it was higher than what was written, it would be better for the castle. But if the real level was lower, then they would get beat up in the competition.

After filling the form, Vritra passed it back to the man busy doing yoga, before saying, "Make sure he calls my full name."

Then he walked away, leaving behind a small crowd of people fuming in anger.

The registrar looked down at the form, wondering what that alien meant, and after seeing the name, his face turned black and blue in rage.

"Stop, dammit, are you messing with me!! Huh, what is written on that cape? Your mother?" After reading that, the registrar went so mad that he struck his head on the table and fainted.

Vritra observed the arena from the warrior lobby. In the lobby alone, there were several hundred people, and as he glanced at the seats...

"T- There should be nearly..." Vritra roughly counted the large crowd in astonishment and was sure there were around eleven or twelve thousand people gathered there.

"Damn, just jumping in the centre of the arena will give me tens of thousands of points. Hehe, we will be rolling that Gacha again today." Vritra’s smile widened under the mask, and he was no longer bothered by his strange clothes.

It wasn’t long before the goddess also arrived in the arena and took her seat on the high platform. There was another woman who looked just as gorgeous with her. The crowd went mad with chatter and shouts as they saw the two women.

They began to bow and pray. The number of people reached more than thirteen thousand. This was going to be such a huge event that most of the people in the capital from around there had arrived in the arena.

"Shall we start?"
