Chapter 83: Forbidden

{You have acquired the Active Skill: Blood Lock.}

{Better luck next time.}

{Better luck next time.}

...

Vritra pushed one key after another, he was not even seeing the notifications, a feeling of complete defeat overtook his mind, he felt that he wouldn’t be able to obtain the Divine Sin Arrow anymore.

{Better luck next time.}

{Better luck next time.}

{You have acquired The Third Eye: Nether Eye.}

{Better luck next time.}

{Better luck next time.}

...

Soon all three hundreds keys were spent, but Vritra was still in a daze he was once again about to buy more keys with his remaining points but Yasmine called out to him in time.

"Hubby, are you alright?"

Vritra blinked and finally came to his senses, he only remembered seeing dozens of failure messages before he became dazed, he was surprised to see that less than a million points were left now.

"I used three hundred keys and still got nothing?" Vritra was about to lose all the strength in his body and fell down to the ground when Yasmine reminded him again.

"You were in kind of a trance earlier, look again I am sure you should have gotten at least one good thing even if the chances were so low."

Vritra nodded and with his heart bleeding he looked over the three hundred messages, there were so many {Better luck next time.} that he felt terrible but soon he noticed that several lines were longer than the other.

"I got f- four things?!!" Vritra was pleasantly surprised, as he called their names out loud:

"Forbidden, Blood Lock, an skill upgrade stone and Nether Eye?"

All the sadness instantly vanished, yes it is true that a lot of sin points were wasted and he still found it hurting but since the luck couldn’t be increased by allocation points so he couldn’t do anything about it for now.

Still getting four things of the level of shift and fourth dimension was more than worth it, he had worked hard for just one day and obtained these godly rewards, what was there to regret.

Throwing these useless thoughts away he focussed on his rewards and read their description carefully.

++++++++++++++++

[Forbidden]

(Skill Description):A forbidden law shall remain non existent. It allows you to seal a single core concept or attribute from a target, rendering it null and unusable.

The chosen concept may be physical, mental or abstract. Once sealed, the target suffers complete loss of that function until the effect ends or is broken, effects and duration are based on your intelligent intelligence stat.

++++++++++++++++

Vritra red the description and his eyes widened in surprise, it was quite well detailed this time around.

{Sin Point Gained: Awestruck +1}

"Whoa, what a cheat. So you can just seal any concept or attribute of the opponent at will? So as long as you can hold on, you can seal off his strength, agility, thoughts, regeneration or even something like mana to a great extent, sigh."

Yasmine shook her head as she read the description along with him, she wondered how much of a monster was her hubby planning to be.

Vritra nodded, he understood the same thing, but this skill went even deeper. Won’t he able to seal things as fear, sanity self awareness or stamina, physical strength or sight, hearing, pain or the abilities of the opponent or even natural restriction such as sealing the space around him or even gravity.

He instantly decided to give it a try, he looked around in the market and soon his eyes fell onto a beggar who was begging while pretending to be blind.

Vritra was still in his disappearing state, from dozens of meters away he locked onto the middle aged beggar and muttered.

"Forbid: Sight!!"

[Forbidden activated.]

...

"Please give me s- some money, my children hadn’t eaten anything in days. How can I work when the god sent me here with sob such a defect, please help this poor old blind beggar."

While the beggar was pleading, looking very miserable many people helped him by giving him coins. The beggar secretly glanced at his stash of coins and notes and felt delighted, it seemed tonight he would be able to drink wine and gamble again, where could he find such fools.

"!!!"

But suddenly, darkness appeared in front of his eyes, startling the beggar. For a few seconds he remained frozen then he rubbed his eyes but that didn’t do anything, he looked around in fright but it seemed he had really gone blind.

{Sin Point Gained: Frightened +1}

"H- HELP MEEEE!! I HAVE GONE BLIND, S- SOMEONE PLEASE HELP ME I CAN’T SEE ANYTHING ANYMORE, WHAT’S HAPPENING?" He screamed in horror.

But the people just sighed seeing the poor beggar, while some felt annoyed since they already gave some money to him earlier, they felt this shameless beggar wanted more.

"What are you yapping about? Weren’t you already blind?" a man spoke.

"No, I was just pretending!!" beggar replied hastily.

...

"Forbid: Gravity!!"

[Forbidden activated.]

In the next moment, the beggar’s sight returned but his body began to float in the air as he screamed in terror and flailed his arms and legs around, all the people around him were also surprised as they moved away.

"G- Ghost, he is possessed by a ghost. Call the shamans, w- wait, is that Baldy a monk? Get here and throw your holy water on the ghost."

A Baldy in the distance who was busy stuffing food in his mouth was dragged towards the beggar forcefully, while his bull ignored the pleading screams of his owner.

The concept of gravity hadn’t been completely reduced to zero but it was reduced to almost zero.

"Forbid: life."

"..."

It didn’t work, so this skill couldn’t be used to directly kill the opponent. But he could still use this skill to kill the opponent in countless ways.

...

Vritra ended his experiment, he was delighted. He could do much more than this with such a deadly and disastrous skill, though he could only seal one concept or attribute at a time and only on one person but it was still a great skill.

"And I don’t even need use the mana, it only exhaust my mental strength." Vritra mumbled to himself feeling quite joyful with one other.

Then his expectant gaze moved to the next thing.

++++++++++++++++

[Innate Skill Upgrade Stone]

(Description): A stone which could instantly increase the level/grade of your innate skill.

++++++++++++++++

Vritra read the single line, this stone could only be used with the skill Dust Manipulation. He decided to think about this stone later, it was a obviously an awesome item too, but he needed to put some thought into whether to use it now or not.

So he directly moved onto the next part, his smile widening in anticipation. Even Yasmine’s expectations had increased greatly by now, this divine sin wheel was just too unreal, each reward it provided was just too terrific each successful draw increased Vritra’s strength greatly.

"Let’s see what’s next." Vritra rubbed his palms together and read the description of the next skill.

Vritra: "..."

Yasmine: "..."

{Sin Point Gained: Dazed +1}

{Sin Point Gained: Awestruck +1}

{Sin Point Gained: Baffled +1}

"I- Isn’t that a bit too scary? What the hell are these skills? Hubby, just don’t be too hard on your enemies in the future, sigh those pitiful creatures." Yasmine spoke in shock, giving him one sin point after another.
