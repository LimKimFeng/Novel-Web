Vritra chewed on the piece of juicy and tough meat of a demon, a drop of green blood sliding down his chin as he focused over different sections of his status.

He had collected quite a lot of corpses, they were his emergency ration; he had noticed that after his initial disgust vanished, the more of the meat he ate, the more his hunger grew—twisting into a craving that demanded even more before he could finally feel satisfied.

The bloodied meat was satiating his hunger and the blood was quenching his thirst, so he had been able to go for an entire month without any rest at all; also because of the help of several passive skills he received from Mutamorphic skill.

Later he received another passive skill, Battle Tenacity, which reduced his mana and stamina consumption by more than half coupled with the mana and stamina boosts he received; he didn't feel exhausted even for a moment.

He was also quite satisfied with his level and stat, the strongest goblin that he faced were at most of level 30 or 31; and he noticed that it became increasingly difficult to level up as his level became higher but that also meant the power difference between each level also became significant.

Dust Manipulation also levelled up just on the 28th day, and now it had become a whole lot stronger yet there was so much space for growth, this skill unlike what other believed was quite broken.

Vritra also noticed that something strange had happened to the species section probably since he had been eating demon flesh and drinking demon blood constantly, but he wasn't concerned about that not that he has a choice.

Just as he finished eating the fresh meat of a goblin Knight, Vritra noticed that it was almost time for the first month to end he jumped off the corpse mountain and walked forward while using dust whips to kill all the goblins without much effort, he was much stronger than any of them.

"These goblins have dropped quite a lot of weapons, I don't even know how many I have killed; but the number should be very large. Let's see what they are going to throw at me next..." Vritra mumbled as more messages popped in front of him.

Soon the goblins that were not dead and were charging towards Vritra were pushed back until they disappeared completely from sight then the ground began to rumble, the walls shook as a distant guttural roar resounded.

The changes were much more significant than the last time, just the roar alone made his ear hurt.

"It seems something big is coming..." Vritra mumbled as two long swords made of hardened dust particles appeared in his hand, turning very sharp and durable.

Finally, he saw it. Creatures several times bigger than the goblins were rushing forward madly, carrying large hammers, axes and even stones; there were countless of them, the sight alone could dread in the heart of most.

They were towering brutes with thick green skin and bulging muscles, their faces were twisted with tusks jutting from their snarling mouths. Large pieces of ragged clothes barely covered their scarred body. Their eyes glowed with a maddening glint, and they gripped ominous looking weapons in their hands.

Instantly Vritra's body flashed and appeared several meters away from his original position as he plunged the long sword into the belly of an Orc before slicing upwards as it was torn into two; just then a tingly sensation ran across his body, telling him about the threat.

He used quick step once again to barely dodge the deadly strike from behind, a large storm of dust suddenly formed around him engulfing more than fifty orcs, blades made of dust began to swing around dangerous, while large spiders and puppets formed around Vritra not to kill but to defend and distract.

Since the current orcs were mostly at lower level than Vritra so the deathly aura that emanated out from his body froze them instantly, letting Vritra cut through them without any efforts, message after message appeared in front of Vritra, but he didn't stop for a moment as he relished in the feeling of tearing apart the giant demons.

The dust swords were instantly painted by their blood, heads of the orcs rolled as Vritra killed a least of couple of them with a single swing of his swords.

There was now an advantage, since the bodies of the orcs were large it was relatively slower so the number of attacks that landed on his body reduced but the ones that landed were much more deadly than the ones that goblins delivered.

Internal organs splattered as the swords cut open the bellies of two orcs, but he was suddenly struck by the hammer like weapon of an orc as his shoulder almost shattered.
