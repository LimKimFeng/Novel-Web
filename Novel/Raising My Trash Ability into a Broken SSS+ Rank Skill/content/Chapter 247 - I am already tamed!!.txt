Chapter 247: I am already tamed!!

Vritra lay on the bed, his mother hugging him from the right and Fiona on the left. Both were clinging to him as if he might just disappear at any moment. Fiona was already very shaken by the entire event, and upon knowing everything that had happened in the forest, Vanessa nearly fainted.

The two women wouldn’t separate from him, as they felt comfort in his warmth, claiming different sides of his body as their private property.

Diana sat to the side, glancing at the two and feeling jealous. It seemed like they were really enjoying cuddling. She really wanted to join in, but there was no space left for her.

’Sigh, I never thought him ignoring me could feel like such a punishment. Before, I was afraid of his attention on me, and now I desperately want him to even look at me...’ Diana thought, feeling miserable for herself.

The goddess knew she had to do something—somehow win his trust completely. She had already lost any thought of bringing harm to Vritra a long time ago. She longed for his touch so badly and wanted to continue what they had ended halfway the previous time.

’Haa, how should I go about it? Won’t I appear too desperate? But I am indeed desperate, so it should be fine... YES, let’s do this.’ Diana thought with strong resolution, her intense gaze fixed completely on Vritra.

Feeling her piercing gaze, Vritra knew the goddess was truly reaching her limits now. She had seemed to really look forward to the main event when they had gone into the forest, but Vritra ended the session abruptly, even though she had pretended she was really disappointed.

Compared to before, the goddess had already changed a lot.

For now, he removed her from his mind and focused on the current situation. He couldn’t even move his arms as they were surrounded by softness and warmth.

"Sigh, I wonder how your luck actually works. I can’t understand how you seem to end up at the wrong place at the wrong time and then face all sorts of dangerous entities. It is a miracle that we are still alive." Yasmine said, releasing a long, tired sigh.

’Sorry for making you go through all that, but I am innocent. Yeah, I provoked the Nine Pacts by killing their members since I didn’t like them and needed the Divine Sin Arrow. But Bone Blood Cult, the Demon Generals, and now Grieves—it was completely coincidental.’ Vritra too sighed, wondering which bastard was constantly cursing his luck.

"Anyway, I will soon be giving you a surprise." Yasmine said, her tone turning mischievous.

’What sort of surprise?’ Vritra asked curiously.

"Hehe, do I need to define what the word surprise means? You will have to wait a little longer to know it." She teased, enjoying keeping him in suspense.

’Fine, I will wait.’ Vritra said, rolling his eyes. Then he pondered: ’Since Hell and Knight have finished absorbing all that energy, then the labyrinth must have already collapsed by now. Then those three Demon Generals might really have fallen for my lie. They might have gone to the small forest to wait for me. If this went right, then I would be able to obtain new slaves.’

Then he gazed at his level that was still stuck at 1,999 and decided to use the empty 2nd Legion Stone as soon as possible. The increase in each level after reaching the 2nd Legion rank was even more difficult and needed a large amount of experience points.

And Vritra had just gotten lucky, as he had killed more than six hundred powerful beings—mindless humans and the Grieves—even the Pope, who was at the 3rd Legion rank. So he had accumulated a large amount of experience points.

Currently, his soul wraith inventory had expanded a little: Hellmancer, Dark Knight, Bob, Bobby, Ghoul, Ghost, and Psyche, his new soul wraith that possessed the abilities of the Grieves.

It even had the knowledge about the glyphs.

And right now, except for Bobby, all the soul wraiths were at least of the 2nd Legion rank. Hellmancer and Dark Knight were at the beginning levels of the 4th Legion rank, Bob was in the middle of the 2nd Legion rank, Bobby at the peak of the 1st Legion rank, Ghoul and Ghost both close to the peak of the 2nd Legion rank, and Psyche in the middle levels of the 3rd Legion rank.

It was a bit messy for now, but Vritra was planning to raise the levels of all his soul wraiths.

’Sigh, it’s been so long. When is the Misty Essence Mythic Halo going to come out? There is no point in staying in this kingdom anymore otherwise, as the danger will only increase the longer I stay here.’ Vritra thought as he relaxed between the two women who were sleeping peacefully despite it being afternoon and bright outside.

’I will do all that later. For now, I just need a good long rest.’ Vritra yawned and felt his eyelids becoming heavy. He glanced at Diana and spoke: "Why don’t you rest as well?"

"O-Oh, can I?" the goddess stood up with hope in her eyes. She wouldn’t have minded sleeping on top of him, resting her head over his chest, and enjoying embracing him.

"Yeah, go back to your room and rest all you want." Vritra barely stopped himself from laughing when her expression shifted from eager to utter disappointment. She made it so obvious.

’Don’t tell me... did he lose all interest in me already?’ the goddess felt depressed as she walked out of the room in a daze. She glanced back at the three one last time before closing the door.

Then she headed back to her room in misery.

"Hahaha, it is hard to believe it is the same goddess from some time ago. You have really trained her well." Yasmine laughed. The goddess was becoming cuter by the day.

’I am practicing. After all, I need to tame my Dragon Queen in the future.’ Vritra replied, keeping his eyes open with a little difficulty.

"What do you mean tame? I am already yours. I would just love whatever you do to me..."

While talking to her, Vritra soon fell asleep, and Yasmine decided to rest too.

When out of nowhere all the members of the temple disappeared—even the Pope—the four men from the Golden Moon became even more confident in their belief that they truly might be possessed by Grieves.

They started searching all over the place for them for several days, even inside the small forest, but found absolutely nothing. It was as if the earth had swallowed them all. Not even the traces of the fight were found—all thanks to Hellmancer.

After a long rest, Vritra woke up when it had started to turn dark. The other women were already awake and waiting for him to eat dinner together. After washing his face to throw all sleepiness away, Vritra left his room.

Soon he sat along with the three women and ate dinner. Finally, the peaceful and wholesome family time had returned, and Vritra always enjoyed it as long as it lasted.

After dinner, the women took a walk around the castle along with him and then went back to their rooms, while Vritra decided to pay the King a visit... a friendly one.
