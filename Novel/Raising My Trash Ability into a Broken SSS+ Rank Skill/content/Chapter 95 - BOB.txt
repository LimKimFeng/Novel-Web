Chapter 95: BOB

Vritra had entered the Fourth Dimension and even used Shift to increase his speed, while using Forbidden to decrease the speed of that creature. Still, only because of Yasmine’s help was he barely managing to survive against that thing.

He wasn’t sure if his Fourth Dimension would work against it or not, but even after disappearing from the real world, that large metallic ball was still able to sense his presence, which made him even more unsure. If his gamble failed, then one strike might really kill him.

Vritra was just running around aimlessly when a message popped up in front of him.

____________

[You have entered the Death Canopy.]

[A sealed space within the Divine Sin Tomb, this place only serves two purposes — extermination and a test.

An area which was used to clean off anyone who tries to intrude into the Divine’s house. Either you find the way out or die, but the way out is non-existent. The level required to even have a chance to exit this plane is at least 1000 (Legion level).]

[Survival chance: None]

[Difficulty level: Impossible]

____________

Vritra almost tripped as he read the lines, while Yasmine teleported him away just in time to avoid getting hit by that rolling creature. He wondered why he always ended up in this kind of situation.

{Sin Point Gained: Chased +1}

By the message, he got a little info. So this Divine is actually some kind of godly being who is probably dead—anyway, his own house had become his tomb. And just how these creatures are called Myriad by being at or above level 1000, humans and demons are probably called Legion who had achieved or surpassed that level.

"FUCK!! What kind of test is it if there is no way out? Damn it, even if I use all my strength, I doubt I could even scratch the surface of its body."

Vritra was once again in a hopeless situation. This time, his opponent was way too strong.

He couldn’t keep running like this forever—soon Yasmine’s energy would be depleted and she wouldn’t be able to help him, then he would truly be doomed.

____________

[Quest: Survive against and Kill one of the Balls of Brutality.]

[Reward: Myriad Armor, made from the Ball of Brutality, corresponding to its level.]

[Penalty: None]

____________

"..." Vritra was truly speechless and felt so angry that he almost wanted to slice the display screen in two. When just running away is so difficult, how could he even think of killing one?

Vritra ignored this quest for now. The only skill that could help him kill stronger beings is Blood Lock, but there was no way he could break through the Ball of Brutality’s defenses and draw even a single drop of its blood.

Wait, one of them... doesn’t that mean there’s more than one?

{Sin Point Gained: Chased +2}

Vritra felt a tingly sensation all over his body. He noticed that while he seemed to be running randomly, it was as if his direction was being controlled by that large ball behind him. It seemed that wasn’t a mindless creature.

"Damn it, there are two or probably more?!! FUCK!! BOB, WHY THE FUCK ARE YOU CHASING ME?" Vritra cursed as he saw several intersections in front of him and on both sides. It was like BOB had planned to bring him to this place from the start.

The whole place was rumbling loudly and it was almost impossible to know from which place the other BOB would pop out. Thankfully, Vritra had his Nether Eye which greatly increased his senses.

’Oh right, Nether Eye. Maybe mental attack could work against it.’

Vritra first sensed the entire space around him, his feet moving at a blurring speed, and he didn’t dare pause for a moment.

He instantly sensed the intersection with the loudest vibration. His body twisted as a dust clone appeared right beside him. Vritra kicked the clone lightly to change his direction abruptly while sending the clone flying into that intersection.

By now, he had learned to bring his body parts out of the Fourth Dimension while his entire body remained there—just now, he had done the same with his feet.

BOOOM

Another ball, looking just as large but with even more dangerous spikes, shot out of that intersection, making the clone explode instantly. Now the two balls charged at Vritra at full speed.

"FUCK FUCK FUCK, why does the situation keep getting worse? I have to think of a way immediately. Damn it BOBs, why don’t you two play with one another?"

Vritra ran towards the door from which he had entered, as he took out several potions that would increase his strength, agility, and endurance.

He hastily gulped them all and received a boost of several dozen stat points before wearing back his mask. He even pulled out a couple of rings that he had taken from the king’s inventory—they too gave him a little boost over agility.

Finally, he was able to decrease the speed at which the first BOB was catching him. He couldn’t use Forbid on the second BOB, so he had to rely on Yasmine.

...

The people from the Nine Pacts and Golden Moon had finally neutralized all the attacks that were targeted at them because of that hateful human. Almost all had survived, but most were in very bad condition.

Only the leaders were still mostly fine.

"Let’s join hands for now. We can’t let that demon escape," Number One suggested.

"Yes, it will just be to kill him. After that, we will fight fairly for the treasure of this place," the dragon-masked man replied.

After the arrangements were made, both of them charged into the gate with mad rage. They had even forgotten about the tomb for now and only wanted to kill Vritra.

Right after passing through the door, they found themselves standing in an ant-colony-like place, with countless caves and intersections. This was an even more difficult puzzle with no way out.

"How are we going to find him in this place?" the monkey-masked man asked, his voice full of rage.

"I don’t think he has found the way out just yet. He must be walking around somewhere in those caves. Let’s divide into groups and look for him," Number Two suggested. Even he couldn’t remain calm after being insulted by Vritra and made a fool of several times.

"Alright, we must kill him." Both sides agreed but just then...

"Brothersssss, so you came for me!! Thank you."

All the people were surprised to hear the detestable voice again. Because of the echo and strange rumblings, it was hard to find his location instantly.

{Sin Point Gained: Watched +11,089}

But within moments, they all saw the same person in black and white dress charging towards them at an incredible speed. His hateful hidden face flashed in front of them for a moment as he passed by—before any of them could react.

His speed was so fast that even the leaders were caught off guards and they failed to make their moves immediately.

"Thank you," were the words that everyone heard, before his figure disappeared to their left.

The leaders faces became ferocious, they weren’t going to miss this chance to kill him but just then they all heard the rumbling and explosive sound getting closer- too close.

And then they saw the monster rolling towards them, carrying along an aura of death.
