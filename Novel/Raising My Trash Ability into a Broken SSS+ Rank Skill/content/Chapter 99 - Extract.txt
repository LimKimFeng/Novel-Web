Chapter 99: Extract

Holding the axes in his hands, Vritra ran towards BOB 1 at full speed; the injured ball lay in its battered form, unable to move at all. Even for a Myriad creature, it had received too much damage, while its strength kept weakening.

The second creature paid no mind, even as it had injured its companion. It continued to charge towards Vritra as if they had found the true purpose of their life—both of them had made him run all over the place for so long.

Finally, it was the last or probably the second last step of his plan. As he approached BOB 1, Vritra raised both his hands above his head, taking the partial form of a dragon as black scales grew over his body.

He used Shift again, and momentarily his speed decreased while he poured all those points into strength. Vritra suddenly felt power filling his muscles.

...

[Shift Activated.]

[Agility → Strength]

++++++++++++++++

Agility: 10 Strength: 3555

++++++++++++++++

Releasing a thunderous roar, Vritra brought down both the axes, pointing the sharp parts downward. Even if the creature was severely injured, Vritra was not taking it easy. His hands and axes began to glow with a dark purple flame as he drew all the Evil-Veyll God Essence from his Essence Core.

He had decided to pour everything into this one attack. Previously, he had killed someone nearly level 850, but this time the bar had been raised to above level 1000.

He could feel the second creature creeping close to him at a great pace. Vritra wasted no time as he struck the injured ball with the sharp blades of the axes. Even its flesh was extremely hard, but after a slight resistance, the blades actually began to tear apart its flesh as they plunged deeper and deeper.

CRIIIIIIIEEEEEEKKKKKKKKKKKK!!!

For the first time, BOB released a loud roar as pain coursed through its body, but it was too weak to do anything. As the axes tore into its body, they finally plunged into its organs.

Then the purple flames, which were extremely destructive, wreaked havoc inside its body—cutting and crushing everything within. The large ball was being ground into tiny pieces from the inside.

Vritra didn’t stop — as the momentum from his downward strike pulled him forward, he leaned into it and rolled ahead with the force. The corpse, still skewered on both axe blades, moved with him like a grotesque extension of his own body.

It spun under his feet, dragged behind his back, then whipped forward once more. The half-shredded flesh rotated violently around him. Blood sprayed in arcs, and chunks of ruined muscle scattered across the ground, while the smouldering purple flames clung to the torn body of the creature until it took its last breath.

Then in the next moment...

++++++++++++

[You have killed a Level 1011 Ball of Brutality.]

[+EXP Earned.]

[You have Levelled Up!]

[You have Levelled Up!]

[You have Levelled Up!]

...

[All Stats Increased by 1.]

[All Stats Increased by 1.]

[All Stats Increased by 1.]

...

[You have gained 1 Allocation Point.]

[You have gained 1 Allocation Point.]

[You have gained 1 Allocation Point.]

...

++++++++++++

BOB 1 was finally dead.

He had actually managed to kill a Myriad creature, a being with over a thousand level. The power difference between 999 and 1000 is quite vast, even a warrior close to legion level can’t defeat someone who had just arrived at that level.

Vritra felt strength pouring into his body, but before he could follow up with any action, he sensed the other ball getting dangerously close to him.

So without a second thought, he threw the axes and the large corpse into his inventory, then used Shift again—and his agility increased even more because of so many level-ups.

Vritra was painted in greyish blood, but he didn’t have the time to stop and enjoy his feat. He sped forward and barely managed to dodge getting pinned by those dangerous spikes.

’Plan 1 was successful with only one step remaining now. I need to create more distance between us for that, but Yasmine is too exhausted right now.’ Vritra gritted his teeth and pulled out several bottles of mana potions.

He reached deeper into the caves and then gulped all of them hastily. A large amount of energy flowed into his body, which made his face turn red. This energy couldn’t be stored since his mana meter was already full.

Vritra paused for a moment all of a sudden, and then he used every bit of energy in his body as a vast amount of dust exploded out of him, completely filling up several hundred caves without leaving even a tiny space.

Even the audience were shocked after seeing so much dust leaking out of the caves all of a sudden. It was not enough to stop the deadly creature but could block it for a moment or two.

"..." Vritra pulled several more bottles of mana potions and his axes, to which the corpse of the BOB 1 was still stuck. He drank one bottle after another, and in the midst, he shouted:

"EXTRACT!!"

The invisible flames burst out of his body, completely enveloping the corpse. All the energy was instantly sucked dry from his body as the transformation to becoming a Soul Wraith had just begun.

If he failed at this step because of a lack of energy, then he really would end up dead at the hands of the other BOB. He drank one mana potion after another without any pause and gave all this energy to Nihil-Anima, as the corpse began to disintegrate.

This was such a bad feeling—drinking the mana potion again and again only to be emptied of all energy the next moment. He felt utterly exhausted, and even his mind was being affected, but he didn’t halt as he chugged bottle after bottle of the potions.

The extremely fast rotating ball was slowly getting closer—now it was a war of time and luck.

Vritra was shocked that this thing needed so much energy. He was so close to failure, but Nihil-Anima worked super hard as it consumed blood, essence, and soul of the dead creature, using a part of its own energy to help its owner.

After what felt like an eternity, the corpse had completely disappeared and...

++++++++++++++++

[SOUL WRAITH INVENTORY]:

Name: BOB Rank: Myriad Creature Level: 1011

++++++++++++++++

Vritra was overjoyed—plus extremely exhausted—as he sensed the presence of a terrifying being appearing in his body. Its strength was just as high as before it had died, but now it was like an undead, so it could be said to be even stronger.

Vritra looked at the stats of the Myriad creature in a haste. His combat strength had just been increased to a whole other level.

++++++++++++++++

Race: Ball of Brutality

Level: 1011

Vitality: 100%

Field: SPACE

Stats:

• Strength: 12,059

• Agility: 13,538

• Endurance: 16,458

• Intelligence: 10,842

• Mana: 9,397

++++++++++++++++

’Whoa, so high... It’s like almost ten times my stats. Its stats shouldn’t be so high—after all, with each level up our attributes increase by just one, aside from the allocation points. And what is that "Field"...?’ Vritra wondered, but he had no time for a chit-chat, as he heard the explosive sound from behind.
