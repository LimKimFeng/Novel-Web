Chapter 357: Reward (3)

"...Eh?"

Li Feng froze.

He blinked once.

Then again.

He stared at the screen, rubbed his eyes, and looked once more... just to confirm his mind wasn’t playing tricks on him.

But the result remained unchanged.

[Rank 6 Divine Pregnancy Pill!]

"...Is this some kind of fate-altering pill or something?"

A flicker of hope still lingered in his heart, refusing to die completely.

Perhaps it wasn’t what it looked like.

Perhaps there was some deeper, misunderstood function.

Slowly, he opened the description.

[Name: Divine Pregnancy Pill]

[Rank: 6]

[Description: As long as any woman at or below the Soul Formation Realm consumes this pill, she is guaranteed to conceive a healthy child after union between man and woman.]

[Note: This pill is bound to the host so only the host can impregnate any woman who consumes it. Furthermore, the child born will inherit the combined talents of both parents. It will not be weaker than either side, and may even surpass them, possessing a chance of awakening an even stronger talent!]

[Remark: A divine pill from ancient times, now lost to history after its creator was struck down by heavenly tribulation for crafting such a heaven-defying creation.]

"Dammit! It really is a pregnancy pill!"

Li Feng jumped to his feet in anger.

It was indeed a miraculous pill worthy of being called a divine pill, but...

"What the hell was that guy thinking, creating something like this?"

Li Feng grumbled.

After all, refining a pill of this caliber required immense skill.

The person who made it was no ordinary cultivator... he had to be a true powerhouse.

But then, a thought crossed Li Feng’s mind.

The stronger one’s cultivation became, the harder it was to produce descendants.

Li Feng already knew this from personal experience.

He had been with countless women and indulged himself with their bodies time and time again, yet not a single one had gotten pregnant.

Whoever created this pill must have been someone truly desperate for a child.

Li Feng let out a long sigh and sat back down on the bed, staring at the notification.

"...I’m still too young to be a father."

Even if he combined the years from both his lives, he would already be in his fifties.

Yet, deep down, he still felt young at heart.

There were still so many things he hadn’t done, so much of the world left to explore... and, of course, countless women he had yet to meet.

It wasn’t that he didn’t want children.

Otherwise, he wouldn’t have recklessly released his seed inside every woman he’d been with.

If any of them had gotten pregnant, he would have simply treated it as an accident and provided support.

But this pill was completely different.

Using it meant he would be deliberately choosing to become a father.

He would have to take full responsibility, knowing with certainty that his partner would conceive his child.

As much of a pervert as he was, there was a huge difference between an accidental pregnancy and a guaranteed one.

Li Feng leaned back on the bed, staring at the ceiling as silence fell over the room.

He shook his head.

"I’m overthinking this."

After all, having the pill didn’t mean he had to use it right now.

He could simply keep it and use it when he truly wanted a child in the future.

He sat up and looked at the system panel.

Even though he had complained about... this pill, it would probably cause bloodshed if its existence were ever revealed.

"In a world where bloodlines decide everything... this thing is basically cheating fate itself."

A guaranteed healthy and genius child from a powerhouse would mean another generation of guaranteed safety for that faction.

If a Soul Formation cultivator was the mother, it basically meant guaranteeing another Soul Formation Realm cultivator... one who might even surpass her.

If those powerful factions knew about this pill, they would definitely pay any price to obtain it.

Though he wasn’t entirely sure what the Soul Formation realm is at, he knew it was definitely a higher realm than Nascent Soul.

After thinking for a while, Li Feng realized it wasn’t as bad as he had first thought.

This pill essentially guaranteed him at least one descendant.

As for who he should give it to, he simply shrugged.

After all, he had no plans to give it to anyone anytime soon.

With these thoughts in mind, Li Feng let out a small smile.

In the end, it wasn’t as bad as he had first thought.

His gaze then shifted to the last reward he had received from clearing Yue Lan’s first calamity.

In addition to the 20,000 Favor Points and ten free Gacha pulls, there was one final reward and it was...

[x1 Wishing Card]

"Wishing Card? What is that?"

Li Feng tilted his head in confusion and checked its description.

[Name: Wishing Card]

[Rank: ???]

[Description: With this card, the Host is able to summon their future self from an indeterminate point in time, depending on the Host’s luck! The duration of the summoning largely depends on how far into the future the summoned self originates from!]

"...Eh? I can summon myself?"

Li Feng blinked, unable to stop the thought that the system really did have all sorts of interesting things.

An intrigued look soon appeared on his face.

After all, he was quite curious about what his future self would be like.

Even though he had caught a glimpse, that vision seemed to belong only to Yue Lan’s future.

What he truly wanted to see was his own.

Maybe he could even ask about decisions he might come to regret... or the embarrassing moments he should avoid in the future.

But one point made him tilt his head again.

"But... indeterminate time? What do you mean?"

[Ding!]

[It means the future Host you can summon is completely random! It could be a version of you from one hour in the future, a hundred years from now, or even further!]

Li Feng gave the system a strange look.

After all, why would he want to summon a version of himself from just one hour in the future? To say hi?

With a sigh, he shook his head.

"Well, whatever. The huge amount of Favor Points and the ten free Gacha pulls are already more than enough."

Li Feng was quite satisfied with his rewards.

He then decided to take a closer look at the new puppet blueprint, as well as the updated dual cultivation section and the Dark Source.

Just as his eyes were widening at the new effects of the Sovereign Harmonious Guiding Art, the door clicked open and Yue Lan walked back in.

Yue Lan was smiling softly, carrying a tray of hot tea.

"Li Feng, come try some mind-calming tea I just got from the elder."
