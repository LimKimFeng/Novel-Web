Chapter 356: Reward (2)

"20,000 Favor Points and ten gacha pulls..."

Li Feng gulped, his heart trembling with excitement.

Just the 20,000 Favor Points alone meant he could add another 200 stars!

He currently had 100 stars and that would bring him to a total of 300 if he use all of that to upgrade.

He couldn’t even imagine how strong he would become.

But then a wry smile crossed his face as he shook his head.

He had already learned his lesson about upgrading too many stars at once.

If he rashly added 200 stars in one go, the overwhelming vitality born from each new star would likely turn him into a complete... sex offender.

Sure, he enjoyed that kind of thing, but only when he was still in control and conscious.

"Hehe... I definitely need to find some partners for this," he muttered and smile lewdly.

His mind immediately drifted to Yue Lan, who had been staying by his side this whole time.

He still hadn’t properly reunited with her in that way after the secret realm had ended.

Remembering how she had awkwardly tried to seduce him yesterday just to cheer him up, Li Feng shook his head with a gentle smile.

"It seems I really let her down. No, I can’t waste her efforts!"

Li Feng decided he need to have a... proper celebration with Yue Lan after this.

And he also decided he would upgrade his stars gradually, one by one, only when he had a willing partner to help handle the surging lust.

He didn’t want to experience that frustrating, unbearable desire with nowhere to vent ever again.

With that thought in mind, he moved on to the second reward.

To be honest, he thought he wouldn’t need to use the gacha again anytime soon as each pull cost 1,000 Favor Points which was far too expensive for him.

He would rather use the favor point to focus on strengthening himself.

But now that he had ten free pulls, nervousness and anticipation bubbled up inside him.

After all, some of his strongest foundations, like the Starforge Body, had come from the gacha.

Li Feng suddenly cast a cleansing spell on himself, knelt down on the floor, and bowed toward the heavens.

"Please, Gacha God... grant me luck this time!"

After wiping his hands dramatically, he took a deep breath and tremblingly activated the pulls.

[Ding!]

[-10 Gacha Pulls!]

The familiar screen appeared with ten pale cards floating before him.

With a shaking hand, he flipped the first card.

And then,

Golden light erupted!

[Ding!]

[War God’s Type IX: Sky Titan Puppet Blueprint acquired!]

Li Feng’s eyes widened at the first pull.

Even though he wasn’t sure about the details of this puppet, the name alone sounded incredibly powerful.

If the first pull was already this monstrous...

Then what about the second?

He didn’t even bother checking the details as excitement surged through him.

Without hesitation, he hurriedly flipped the second card while his luck was still hot.

And another burst of golden light erupted!

[Ding!]

[Sovereign Harmonious Guiding Art Technique – Part II acquired!]

His eyes bulged.

This was actually another part of his incomplete dual cultivation technique!

Even though the current version was labeled incomplete, Li Feng already felt it was incredibly powerful and now...

His breathing grew heavier.

With widened, reddened eyes, he hurriedly flipped the third card and golden light exploded once more!

[Ding!]

[Dark Source acquired!]

"Eh?"

Li Feng’s burning gaze froze.

After all, he had just lost Goldie’s Gold Source... and now he had received another Source?

The coincidence left him speechless.

The surge of excitement within him cooled slightly as he stared at the Dark Source with complicated emotions.

Still, with a soft sigh, he pushed the thought aside for later and continued flipping the remaining cards while his streak was still hot.

However, reality quickly slapped him in the face.

Fourth draw: [Cracked Spirit Stone]

Fifth draw: [Old Kitchen Knife]

Sixth draw: [Widow Han’s Chicken Soup Recipe]

Seventh draw: [A Nice Rice Bowl]

Eighth draw: [A Forgotten, Spoiled Steamed Bun]

Ninth draw: [A Copper Coin]

Li Feng’s face darkened completely by the end.

"The hell?! Did you rob someone’s kitchen?! And who the hell is Widow Han?!" he shouted, grabbing the system panel in frustration.

It had to be known that each pull cost 1,000 Favor Points.

To get a spoiled steamed bun in return for that amount... his heart nearly bled at the thought.

Still, he tried to calm himself.

At least he had gotten three good rewards, and that was enough to ease the pain.

Plus, it served as a harsh reminder that the gacha was far too random.

He had briefly considered spending another 10,000 Favor Points for more pulls, but now he thought better of it.

...Better to save his points to strengthen himself and wait for future free pulls from the system.

With a heavy sigh, he turned to the final card.

He didn’t dare raise his hopes too high as... the higher the hope, the greater the disappointment.

Closing his eyes, he shakily flipped the last card.

And then—

To his surprise...

A brilliant radiance, even more intense than before, exploded outward.

Li Feng’s eyes snapped open in shock at the dazzling card.

Slowly... the contents revealed themselves.

[Ding!]

[Congratulations, Host! You have obtained a Rank 6 Pill!]

His heart nearly leapt out of his chest.

The pill he had used to revive Yue Lan... was only Rank 4.

And now, he had obtained a pill an entire two level higher.

A surge of heat rushed through his chest, his pulse quickening as countless possibilities flashed through his mind.

A Rank 6 pill... something capable of altering fate and life itself now rested within his grasp.

For a brief moment, ambition burned brightly within him as he couldn’t help but wonder what kind of heaven-defying miracle pill he had obtained this time.

But then—

His gaze fell upon the name.

And everything... stopped.

[Rank 6 Divine Pregnancy Pill!]

"...Eh?"
