...Until they arrived.

They had already tried everything.

They had even knocked on every stone in the chamber, but nothing happened.

There were no doors, no hidden passages, and no sign of any kind of exit.

They had even attempted to dig a hole, but strangely enough, the rock surrounding the space seemed extremely special.

"Hmm... But what if no one comes? What if no help arrives?"

"Y-You Azure Moon Sect people are all like this! I don’t want to die... hik... hik..."

And now, having two new companions who were just as emotionless and detached as Yue Lan and Liu Yanyue didn’t make her feel any better.

They clearly had no idea how to comfort anyone at all!

She had imagined a grand adventure, finding treasures, or perhaps even discovering rare ingredients for a Foundation Establishment Pill before the secret realm closed.

Because she had been lucky enough or perhaps unlucky enough to become trapped inside this stupid temple.
