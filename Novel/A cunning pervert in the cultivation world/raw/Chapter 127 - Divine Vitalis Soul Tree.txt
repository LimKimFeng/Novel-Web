Chapter 127: Divine Vitalis Soul Tree

Li Feng, completely oblivious to the outside world,... didn’t notice that another gaze had joined him, watching the shifting soil below as if something was about to sprout out.

Crack...

Crack...

Soon, a tiny sapling pushed its way out of the soil.

"Ohh" Li Feng let out in amazement, as he noticed it wasn’t like any other ordinary green sprout.

The moment it broke through, a faint ethereal glow rippled across its surface—soft golden light flowing like liquid starlight along its delicate stem.

Two small leaves unfurled, translucent like crystal jade, each vein inside shimmering faintly, pulsing in a slow, heartbeat-like rhythm.

"Woah..." Li Feng leaned closer, eyes brightening. "Is this... normal?"

The little sapling swayed gently.

It felt almost alive, not merely growing but awakening.

HUMMM-...

Then, a soft hum resonated through the wasteland, subtle yet ancient, like a distant echo calling from another realm.

Li Feng blinked.

He looked around.

"...Did it just make a sound?"

He then knelt lower, staring in fascination as the sapling’s glow grew slightly brighter, casting pale shadows across the barren ground.

Unseen by him, outside the brick—

The rift in the sky pulsed once as the being inside the void caught sight of the newly born sprout.

And the enormous presence beyond the void... shifted ever so slightly, as though acknowledging the sprout’s birth.

"...."

Then it paused.

As if thinking.

As if deciding.

And a moment later... it finally chose to bestow a tiny "gift"

A single mote of light peeled away from its massive silhouette and began to fall from the void.

... A fragment of something that should not exist in any mortal realm.

The invisible speck drifted downward, crossing the void like a falling star in reverse...silent, weightless, yet carrying the pressure of countless worlds.

It slipped through the rift.

Passed through the sky.

Passed through the forest.

Passed effortlessly through the Divine Brick surface into the pocket space...

... And gently descended onto the glowing sapling.

Inside, Li Feng felt that something was off, but before he could pinpoint it.

Just then, a notification suddenly popped up in front of him.

"Blessed...? By who?"

Looked right.

Then looked up at the empty sky of the pocket space.

No one.

Not even a fart.

Just him... and a glowing baby tree.

Crack...

Crack...

As if on steroids, the tree began to grow at an unbelievable speed.

Li Feng, noticing this, hurriedly stepped back.

Then he continue staring dumbfounded as the sapling that had been barely knee-high moments ago shot upward rising, stretching, and expanding...until it towered over him.

Soon, as if its growth spurt had finally ended, it stopped.

And Li Feng stood below it, mouth wide open as he stared at the massive tree before him.

Where once a tiny sprout had wiggled out of the soil like a shy newborn a moment ago...

...now stood a tree that looked as if it belonged in the garden of an ancient immortal.

It had shimmering golden leaves that bathed the entire area in a soft, pale gold glow.

Its trunk wasn’t merely brown like ordinary wood.

Rather, streaks of radiant gold ran through the bark like living veins pulsing faintly with power.

A gentle hum of soul energy spread from the tree, brushing against Li Feng’s spirit and flooding the pocket space with warmth.

The air seem to became easier to breathe.

His mind felt clearer.

His soul felt... lighter.

Almost comforted.

Almost embraced.

Even the spiritual energy inside the pocket space seemed to be rising, slowly but noticeably.

And he could even vaguely sense that the Divine Brick itself seemed quite pleased.

Staring at the divine, nowtowering tree before him, Li Feng couldn’t help but swallow hard.

"Damn... you grew up too fast."

Hungry.

Lonely.

Excited.

Happy.

"...So that’s a yes."

"Alright, alright. Come here, Little—"
