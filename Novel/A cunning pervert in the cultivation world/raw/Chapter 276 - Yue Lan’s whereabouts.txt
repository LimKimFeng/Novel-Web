Chapter 276: Yue Lan’s whereabouts

Around Li Feng’s cave, which had already turned into a small gathering spot.

The atmosphere felt unexpectedly lively.

With so many beautiful fairies present, it seemed as though they brightened the night itself as their melodic chatter and gentle laughter weaving through the air like music.

Crackle...

Crackle...

Li Feng sat calmly on a rock in front of a bonfire, with Jian Ruyi staying close by his side while Ning Jianli stood quietly behind him.

Both women were calm and silent, like blade still in their sheath.

Li Feng looked across the fire at Shen Jia, who had just finished explaining the situation.

He couldn’t help but feel happy and smile at the news.

"So you’re saying my senior sister is with your senior sister ?"

Shen Jia nodded.

"...Yes, but I’m not sure about their exact situation. That is why I came here looking for help."

As she spoke, she couldn’t help glancing at the two powerful disciples from the Heavenly Sword Sect who were acting almost like Li Feng personal guards.

The whole scene still felt weird to her.

Despite her skepticism, many of the disciples present had vouched for Li Feng so she reluctantly accepted that he might be the one capable of helping her.

Li Feng didn’t notice her doubtful looks or he just simply didn’t care.

He rubbed his chin, falling into deep thought.

According to Shen Jia’s story:

She had managed to regroup with Liu Yanyue, the blind swordswoman.

A few days later, they encountered Yue Lan and decided to team up after running into a demonic disciple along the way.

Then Yue Lan mentioned that she had discovered a strange temple. Liu Yanyue immediately grew interested and asked Yue Lan to take them there.

The three of them went together.

But the moment they stepped inside, Shen Jia saw Liu Yanyue and Yue Lan vanish mysteriously, as if they had been teleported away.

Shen Jia tried to investigate but found no clues.

With no other options, she spent several days searching for help until she met Ye Shuang, who was exploring the area.

Ye Shuang then introduced her to her "super strong and powerful senior brother" which was why she had come here.

’Vanish, huh...’

Li Feng didn’t believe in ghosts- ah, wait, ghosts were actually real in this world...

Still, he didn’t buy this as some inexplicable mystery.

There had to be a logical reason behind it.

Judging from Shen Jia words and description about the place, Li Feng suspected it was most likely the work of some kind of formation.

’... A teleportation formation is at least a Rank 3 formation. It seems that temple is far from ordinary.’

No matter what, he had to go see it in person to draw a proper conclusion.

With his Rank 2 Formation Master talent, he was confident he could at least uncover some clues.

As no matter how slim the chance, he would never abandon Yue Lan to her death.

But another problem soon arose.

He glanced at his petite wife beside him and couldn’t help but sigh inwardly.

’I was planning to hunt down that powerful demonic cultivator who can wield sword intent but... it seem like that will have to wait.’

Li Feng felt a twinge of pity for any righteous disciples who might cross paths with that demonic monster, but he wasn’t some selfless hero.

Yue Lan was key to his future growth and his first women in this world.

Even if she weren’t his favor target, her well being would always rank high among his priorities.

’And it seem I’ll have Goldie stay behind to protect this camp while I’m gone.’

In that brief moment of silence, Li Feng had already mapped out the entire plan before leaving.

Then he smiled and turned to Shen Jia.

"I understand. Do you still know the location of that temple?"

Shen Jia nodded.

"Yes. I left marks along the way here. It should be one or two days travel from this spot."

Li Feng nodded, then looked up at the dark night sky.

"Hmm. Then we’ll set out tomorrow morning."

He planned to set up protective formations around the area tonight, arrange the puppets, and have Goldie guard his fairies.

....Once he rescued Yue Lan, he could return and enjoy the feast he had been looking forward to.

Shen Jia nodded slowly in agreement.

Truthfully, she was exhausted after days of searching for help and traveling here.

"Okay. We’ll leave tomorrow morning then."

Just as she started to stand, Li Feng casually tossed a small pill bottle toward her.

She caught it and looked down.

"A pill bottle ?"

"You should take one. You look quite worn out."

Shen Jia fell silent for a moment, staring at the shameless man in front of her.

Then she slowly opened her mouth.

"...Thank you."

She wasn’t someone petty enough to not understand how to show gratitude, when someone willing to help her.

Besides, she really did need some pills to recover quickly for tomorrow’s journey.

Li Feng grinned at the awkward woman.

"Haha, no worries. We fellow disciples should always look out for one another."

After a short chat, the group began to disperse, each returning to their own tasks.

Looking at the many fairies gathered around which are all his for the picking freely.

Li Feng couldn’t help but sigh inwardly.

It seem that he didn’t have enough time right now to play with all of them yet.

He knew that even if he casually pulled any random one of them into his cave, she wouldn’t resist.

Most of them would even cooperate eagerly, spreading their legs for him without hesitation.

"I guess I should set up the formations tonight," Li Feng muttered as he stood up.

But then Jian Ruyi, who had been silently listening the entire time, rose up with him.

Seeing this, Li Feng reached over, gently patted her head and smiled.

"You can rest, you know. I was quite rough with you earlier."

He still vividly remembered how wild and unrestrained he had become during their "honeymoon."

Yet, surprisingly, despite her small and slender frame, Jian Ruyi had managed to keep up with his intense mortal indulgences...as expected of a Heavenly Sword Sect disciple.

"It’s okay. I will accompany you."

"Oh? With the formations?"

Jian Ruyi shook her head, then spoke solemnly.

"I will accompany and follow you for the rest of our time in this secret realm."

Li Feng blinked at the serious declaration, then burst into laughter and didn’t reject her.

"Haha, as expected of my cute wife."

His hand slid down from her head and landed on her perky rear and he squeeze those soft flesh.

Leaning in close, he whispered against her ear.

"...Then I hope to see my bunny wife make an appearance every now and then."

Jian Ruyi’s face remained stern on the surface, but a faint flush crept up her cheeks.

Li Feng couldn’t help thinking how utterly adorable she looked when he manage to tease her like this.

Meanwhile, Ning Jianli who had been standing behind him and quietly listening wore a strange expression as she stared at Li Feng.

"Hm? What’s wrong?"

Li Feng noticed and turned to ask her.

Ning Jianli hesitated for a moment before finally deciding to speak.

"...Senior Brother Li, are you also a formation master ?"
