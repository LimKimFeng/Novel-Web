Chapter 347: The First Calamity.

Li Feng calmly released his hands and there wasn’t even a trace of Yan Moxuan left.

After all, he had erased the man’s entire existence to the point not even a single drop of blood remained.

"Let’s see if you can revive from that."

Li Feng smiled coldly and slowly turned his attention to the False Cultivator.

"How cruel. Your fellow rat is dead, and you’re still acting like nothing happened?"

[...]

The False Cultivator remained silent, simply staring at Li Feng.

Meanwhile, Li Feng didn’t let his guard down for even a second.

...After all, this guy was the real threat.

Now that he was in the Ruler of the Stars state, he could sense it clearly...

This was the calamity he needed to defeat in order to resolve Yue Lan’s fate.

And unlike Yan Moxuan, the False Cultivator was wrapped in...something that he couldn’t manipulate or twist with the "Law" the way he had with Yan Moxuan.

But Li Feng just smiled.

....He also preferred using his own hands to personally tearing this thing apart.

Suddenly, the False Cultivator moved slightly.

[...I didn’t expect... someone of your caliber... to even exist here...]

SPLASH!

SPLASH!

Li Feng glanced around.

The black water had now flooded the entire space.

Instead of a floor, he felt as if he were standing on top of a vast, pitch-black ocean.

The False Cultivator’s aura grew stronger and stronger, as if he were being empowered by the black sea itself.

He spread his arms wide, as if welcoming Li Feng into his domain.

[...It seems you’re misunderstanding something. I was never—]

Before he could finish,

A fist flew straight at his face.

BANG!

The False Cultivator head exploded into black slugde as Li Feng appeared in front of him and smiled coldly.

"Blah blah blah, I don’t give a damn!"

Then he gripped the False Cultivator’s remaining neck with terrifying strength, his large palm completely enveloping it.

[...!]

Raising his other fist high, Li Feng swung it down like a falling star, his face filled with wild excitement.

"I only care that I get to beat the shit out of you... you BASTARD!!!"

BOOOOMMM!!

Instead of a simple punch, it sounded like a star exploding.

And a terrifying shockwave shook the entire tower.

SPLASH!

SPLASH!

Waves of black water surged violently.

Goldie was already on one knee, struggling to keep its balance from the massive shockwave.

And the Divine Brick was flying erratically, tossed about by the force.

Meanwhile, Li Feng wasn’t done.

He was just about to tear the thing’s body apart.

Suddenly—

SPURT!

SPURT!

SPURT!

Countless jets of black liquid exploded from the dark ocean, piercing through the air toward him like venomous spears.

Li Feng’s eyes narrowed.

In his Ruler of the Stars state, his perception had expanded to an almost god-like level.

He could see the trajectory of every jet, feel the malicious laws woven into the black liquid, and sense the devouring force trying to latch onto his very existence.

"Heh."

But he just smirk and didn’t dodge.

Instead, he tightened his grip on the False Cultivator’s neck even harder, fingers sinking deeper as if trying to crush the very concept of the creature.

BOOM!

A brilliant silver starlight erupted from Li Feng’s body as countless tiny stars manifested around him, spinning rapidly like a protective galaxy.

SPLASH!

SPLASH!

SPLASH!

The black jets slammed into the starlight barrier and exploded into corrosive mist, but none managed to penetrate.

The False Cultivator’s headless body twitched violently in his grasp.

Then a distorted, gurgling voice echoed directly inside Li Feng’s mind:

[...You... cannot kill... what is already... beyond death...]

Li Feng grinned savagely, his eyes burning with battle intent.

He didn’t bother replying as he raised his free hand, and the stars circling around him condensed into a single blazing silver spear of pure stellar energy.

Without hesitation, he thrust it straight down toward the False Cultivator’s chest.

But the moment the spear of silver light was about to pierce—

The entire black ocean beneath them erupted.

WHOOOOSH—!!!

Tens of thousands of black tentacles, thick as ancient pillars, shot upward from the dark water.

They wrapped around Li Feng’s arms, legs, waist, and even coiled around the silver spear, trying to drag him down into the depths.

At the same time, the False Cultivator’s damaged head began regenerating at a visible rate, black liquid bubbling and reforming a featureless face.

[...This is my domain... My calamity... You are merely a... food...]

Li Feng felt a terrifying suction force coming from the black ocean.

It wasn’t just physical... it was pulling at his soul, his cultivation base, and even his memories.

And the more he struggled, the stronger the pull became.

"Like hell I am!"

Li Feng roared.

The Ruler of the Stars state flared to its limit.

His body grew even larger, muscles bulging with cosmic power as the stars lit up across his skin like a living galaxy.

He then twisted violently.

CRACK—!

CRACK—!

Dozens of black tentacles snapped like weak ropes.

With a furious shout, he slammed the False Cultivator’s body downward, using it like a hammer to smash into the ocean surface.

BOOOOOOMMM!!!

A massive crater formed in the black water, and for a brief moment, the devouring force weakened.

Li Feng didn’t waste the chance.

He channeled every ounce of stellar energy into his right fist.

The silver light became so intense it looked like a newborn sun had appeared inside the tower.

"Falling Star Fist!"

He punched downward with everything he had.

The False Cultivator, still held in his left hand, could only widen its featureless face in shock.

Then the fist connected.

And the impact was cataclysmic.

BOOOOOMMMMM!!!

A pillar of silver light pierced straight through the black ocean, creating a massive hole that reached all the way to the bottom of the domain.

Shockwaves ripped outward, vaporizing countless tentacles and black water into harmless mist.

The False Cultivator’s body was nearly obliterated.

...only a small core of writhing black essence remained in Li Feng’s crushing grip.

Yet even now, the creature’s voice echoed weakly:

[...You... still... misunderstand... I am not... the calamity...]

Li Feng’s expression turned icy.

He didn’t expect this thing to still be alive after that.

’It seems what that mysterious voice said is true... this thing is really immortal. It seem like I can’t kill him like this...’

He glance at the ice cocoon and smile slightly.

’Then I just need to buy a bit more times... and draw this thing true body out.’

Then he brought the black core close to his face, staring at it with killing intent that could freeze the stars themselves.

"Then what are you?"

The black core pulsed once...twice...

Then, in a voice that sounded almost... amused.

[...I am... merely the shadow... that was cast... when the real calamity... opened its eyes...]

At that cryptic words, Li Feng didn’t bother listening anymore.

He crushed the core.

TING!

Then he smiled coldly.

"How about you show yourself for real now?"

At his words,

... Suddenly, the entire black ocean began to boil violently.
