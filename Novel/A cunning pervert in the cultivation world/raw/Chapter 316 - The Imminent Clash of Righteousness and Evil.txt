Chapter 316: The Imminent Clash of Righteousness and Evil

In his rage, he slaughtered all of them.

By the time he met up with Ye Mo, Yan Moxuan felt as though he had killed more Fire Yang Sect and Devil Yin Sect disciples than righteous disciples in this secret realm.

After a long moment of silence between them, Yan Moxuan finally looked away and murmured a single word.

"...Useless trash."

Hearing those words, Ye Mo lowered his head slightly, clenching his fists beneath his sleeves.

’Calm down... I just need to endure this...’

He reminded himself again and again.

He could not afford to clash with Yan Moxuan here.... not when he had already lost two lives.

If he died this time, it would mean his true death.

Besides, even in Yan Moxuan weakened state, Ye Mo knew he was still no match for him.

For now, he just had to wait until the secret realm closed.

Then he could return to the sect and ask his master for resources to help reconstruct a new life.

But then a beautiful voice suddenly broke through the tense atmosphere.

"Ahh!! Brother Yan!! I found you! This secret realm is so weird!!"

Yao Xin came running toward them with tears in her eyes.

Ye Mo and Yan Moxuan turned to look at the petite yet voluptuous woman and couldn’t help but notice something strange.

"What happened to your life force?" Yan Moxuan frowned and asked.

Right now, Yao Xin looked unnaturally pale, as if she were gravely ill or had just suffered a severe injury.

Her usual lively aura was dim and fragile.

When Yao Xin heard the question, her expression twisted as though she was traumatized by the memory.

"I-I died, that’s why!"

She then began to explain her situation.

How she had encountered a strange but powerful puppet, how she had died, and how she had managed to resurrect herself using a secret technique.

However, because the technique wasn’t perfect, her blood essence was still extremely weak.

...Of course, she hid the fact that she had already used up all the Rank 3 talismans her grandfather had given her.

After all, even though they were all from the demonic path, and that was also precisely why she could never reveal her weakness.

"We need to get out of this stupid place!" Yao Xin shouted.

She really didn’t want to lose her life or encounter that puppet again.

Meanwhile, Yan Moxuan’s frown deepened the more he listened.

First, there was a monstrous body-tempering disciple from the righteous path who could supposedly rival him, according to Ye Mo’s words.

Now there was a strange puppet capable of killing Yao Xin.

He knew just how resourceful Yao Xin was, so killing her was no easy feat.

Even he wasn’t sure how deep her reserves truly ran.

Yet a random puppet had suddenly appeared out of nowhere and manage to killed her...

Yan Moxuan suddenly felt a headache coming on.

Not because he felt pressured, but because he still couldn’t find his target or achieve his simple goals.

Suddenly, his eyes glowed red as his emotions slowly spiraled out of control.

Ye Mo and Yao Xin noticed this and instinctively took a guarded step back.

Then,

An intense, sharp killing intent radiated from Yan Moxuan.

Even the air around him began to turn faintly red as strange red qi leaked outward.

"Haa... Haa..."

But after a few deep breaths, Yan Moxuan finally calmed down and looked at them with cold composure.

Just as he was about to say something, he suddenly looked up toward the distant sky.

Ye Mo and Yao Xin followed his gaze.

In the distance, a brilliant blue light descended from the heavens like a falling star, accompanied by the majestic silhouette of a massive blue phoenix woven from wisps of spiritual light.

The scene was breathtakingly beautiful, radiating an extraordinary aura that all three of them could clearly feel.

"..."

Without a word, Yan Moxuan vanished, rushing toward where the light was gradually descending.

Ye Mo and Yao Xin exchanged a quick glance, their eyes gleaming with greed and excitement before they activated their movement techniques and followed closely behind.

To cultivators like them, such an extraordinary phenomenon represented a heaven-sent opportunity to soar to greater heights.

Even if it was dangerous, they still chose to investigate.

Soon, almost every disciple scattered throughout the secret realm noticed the falling blue light and the soaring phoenix.

Recognizing it as a rare opportunity, disciples from both the demonic and righteous paths began heading toward the same location.

Somewhere within the forest...

A heroic and strikingly beautiful woman with a high ponytail casually wiped the blood from her sword.

At her feet lay a headless corpse, and judging by the robes, it seemed to be a disciple of the Fire Yang Sect.

"Hmm... this is the tenth demonic disciple I’ve encountered," she murmured, flicking her wrist to swing the sword in a sharp arc before sheathing it smoothly.

"It seems an entire group of them managed to sneak into this secret realm."

She let out a soft sigh. "And I still haven’t found that seed of calamity...’

Just then, an old voice echoed within her mind.

"Hoho... do not worry, my disciple. The opportunity I spoke of... has arrived."

Wei Zhenxin frowned slightly as confusion flashing across her eyes.

She was just about to ask for clarification when... she noticed it.

A brilliant light descending from the sky.

Her gaze sharpened instantly as she sensed that ancient aura.

At the same time, the joyful voice of Immortal Jiuming rang out clearly in her sea of consciousness,

"That is the greatest opportunity within this realm... Go now, you will meet that woman there. Destroy the seed of calamity, and then seize that opportunity!"

Hearing those words, Wei Zhenxin’s eyes brightened with determination.

A resolute smile bloomed on her face as she nodded firmly.

"Yes, Master!"

In the next instant, she vanished like a shadow, her figure blending into the forest as she rushed toward the falling light with incredible speed.

While the entire secret realm was thrown into turmoil...

At a distance away from where the mysterious temple had collapsed—

Crack...

The ground began to shift, as though something were pushing from beneath.

And soon,

Crack...

....a seemingly unremarkable brick covered in vine could be seen poking out.
