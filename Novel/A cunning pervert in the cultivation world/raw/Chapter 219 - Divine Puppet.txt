Chapter 219: Divine Puppet

Li Feng looked at the status and couldn’t help but sigh.

It was already impressive that it had surpassed the limits of the original blueprint, yet he still felt a tinge of regret.

If only he had a better blueprint... and more materials, he might have been able to craft a Rank 3 puppet using the divine wood.

But he had already checked with the System, and for some reason, complete blueprints became insanely expensive starting from Rank 2 puppets.

A Rank 2 puppet blueprint alone cost at least 1,000 Favor Points, and he only had a thousand points to his name.

Even if he did have 1,000 Favor Points to spare, he would rather use them to upgrade his star than waste them on a blueprint.

And it wasn’t just puppet blueprints.

Everything starting from Rank 2, whether pills or formations became absurdly expensive, as if all Rank 1 items had been sold at a beginner-friendly discount.

As for Rank 3 resource? He couldn’t access it yet.

Apparently, he needed to reach the Foundation Establishment realm before it would even become available.

Li Feng also began to notice a pattern with the system shop.

And that the resources he could access through the System Shop were always limited to items one realm above his current cultivation.

The only exception was that Immortal-grade Dual Cultivation technique, whose rank he still wasn’t entirely sure about.

"...Maybe it’s a bug?"

Li Feng smiled like a seasoned scammer and chose to keep this "bug" to himself.

Who knew if the System might suddenly take it back and just refund it if he kept asking about it?

That would make him vomit a mouthful of regretful blood.

He then finally looked at the other nine finished puppets and smiled.

Now that the first one had succeeded, there shouldn’t be any problems with the rest.

After all, he had crafted them all to be precisely the same.

Soon, he began gently inserting the remaining nurtured souls.

Crack.

Crack.

Crack.

After a while, ten Rank 2 puppets knelt before him as he smiled in satisfaction.

He couldn’t help but grin at the sight of possessing ten Foundation Establishment–level puppets that he could command at will.

It should be known that a Foundation Establishment cultivator was already considered a family head–level figure in many cities....a big shot capable of controlling hundreds of others.

Even though the puppets strength might not be truly equal to that of a real Early-Stage Foundation Establishment cultivator since the puppets couldn’t utilize complex techniques or learn new ones—

...especially since all of these puppets were like newborns baby, lacking experience and still far from their true potential.

Li Feng smirked at the thought, already having a plan in mind to help these new puppets gain experience more quickly.

"What’s better than letting a senior guide the juniors?"

He planned to let Goldie act as the leader of this small group for now, allowing it to guide the puppets and help them learn through real experience.

He then finally looked toward Goldie, who was currently suspended in the air.

Countless vine-like branches wrapped tightly around its new bulky body like thick cables stuck into it back.

Golden light flowed through those branches like cables as Little Ling helped shape Goldie’s new body.

That’s right, he had also reconstructed Goldie’s body.

Even though Goldie’s previous body had become quite tough after being constantly tempered by its golden essence, but its base and foundation still couldn’t compare to Divine Wood.

Moreover, the Golden Source that served as Goldie’s core was extremely special and didn’t seem to work well with conventional puppet construction methods.

Li Feng could have created a vessel for Goldie, but it seemed that the Golden Source preferred to create, connect, and construct its inner vessel by itself.

Even from below, Li Feng’s eyes could perceive the intricate complexity currently being constructed inside Goldie’s new body, thanks to his omniscient view as the master of the Divine Brick.

He could see the threads of energy intertwining with uncanny precision, each pulse carving channels that resonated with the essence of life itself.

Inside, the Golden Source wove and carved its own internal architecture, as golden like liquid slowly spiraling like a living star and forming pathways that defied conventional understanding.

It seemed that Goldie’s Golden Source knew exactly what it was doing, which was why Li Feng only created the outer vessel, leaving the construction of the inner structure entirely to the Core itself.

And with Little Ling’s help in adjusting the spiritual qi and shaping her branches—

Slowly... a monstrous puppet was being born.

Li Feng crossed his arms as he looked at Goldie’s suspended body.

It was similar to the other wooden puppets, since he had based them all on the same blueprint, except that Goldie’s body was far taller, more massive, and more imposing.

Suddenly, Goldie’s hollow eyes flared to life, glowing like a machine powering on.

Crack!

Crack!

Crack!

One by one, the vine-like branches snapped like taut cables, and Goldie’s enormous body slammed onto the ground.

BOOM!

Leaves scattered in every direction from the impact.

Before Li Feng, Goldie’s shadow stretched upward, slowly growing taller and taller until it loomed over him, surpassing even his own height.

HUMMMM—

A powerful pressure emanated from its wooden body as golden light pulsed through it like flowing blood before gradually fading away.

Li Feng looked up at Goldie, who now appeared even more menacing.

Even he could feel the oppressive force emanating from it.

Yet, Li Feng smiled.

After all.....Goldie was like family to him.

[Ding!]

[Puppet Detected!]

[Name: Goldie]

[Type: Divine Puppet]

[Level: Early Rank 3 Puppet (Equivalent to Early Stage of Golden Core)]

[Abilities: [Spiritual Absorption], [Adaptation], [Growth], [Golden Essence], [Divine Power: "Gold"], [Wood Manipulation], [Regeneration], [Intelligence]]

[Remark: A one-of-a-kind Divine Puppet, forged from the combination of a Golden Core and the Divine Wood of a Divine Vitalis Soul Tree.]

[Capable of self-construction, spiritual absorption, and rapid adaptation.]

[Possesses immense growth potential, unparalleled resilience, and a unique affinity with Divine Power: "Gold".]

[Considered a living vessel rather than a mere puppet, with intelligence and awareness approaching that of a true cultivator.]

Upon seeing that god-like status, Li Feng blinked, rubbed his eyes, before he muttered the same words he had said when he first created Goldie.

"...Damn...Goldie is even stronger than me."
