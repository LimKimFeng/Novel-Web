The early morning mist filled the air as Li Feng followed Goldie in a single direction.

"...A swamp ?"

Before him lay a huge swamp.

He can sense a faint smell of rot drifted from its depths.

With mud bubbles popped quietly, and the surface was covered with patches of black moss.

Li Feng crouched slightly , scanning the area. " So this is where that thing’s hiding...." he muttered.

Goldie remained still beside him , its golden-lit eyes fixed on the murky water ahead.

But Li Feng’s instincts told him something was off.

Still, he didn’t act immediately and he turned his head toward Goldie.

"Goldie , go around the swamp and wait. I’ll go try to kill it, but if it tries to escape, make sure to stop it. "
