Chapter 34: New skill (01)

Ethan had to fight hard to resist the urge to start studying that technique right away.

Reluctantly, he put the book back on the table and then pulled out the gacha tickets he had saved up.

First, the seven free tickets.

He didn’t expect much from them, so he just tore them open casually.

[You used: 7x Free Gacha Tickets]

[Spinning...]

As expected, he didn’t win much of real use.

Aside from an F-grade blacksmith’s forge, which he would certainly put to good use, the rest was just a few miscellaneous tools and a single E-grade sword.

"Not bad..." Ethan smiled.

At least compared to last time, when he spun nearly a hundred free tickets, the result had been quite satisfactory.

Shaking his head, Ethan took out the other twenty-seven gacha tickets he had in his possession, twenty common and seven premium.

First, he tore open the common ones, saving the premium ones for last.

[You used: 20x Common Gacha Tickets]

[Spinning...]

[Congratulations! You received: 1x Mana Core (Rank E)]

[Congratulations! You received: 5x Minor Stamina Potion (Rank E)]

[Congratulations! You received: 1x White Steel Pickaxe (Rank F)]

[Congratulations! You received...] ...

A flood of notifications flooded Ethan’s mind, but he focused only on the first two.

Five stamina potions and a mana core.

The mana core wasn’t very useful to him, but it was perfect for Alice, who was a mage.

The stamina potions, however, were wonderful for Ethan.

This type of potion was less rare than healing potions, of which Ethan already had two, both low-rank.

But despite being less rare, it was still very useful, bringing with it the ability to instantly regenerate the stamina of an exhausted warrior.

If used in large quantities, it would cause great harm to the body, but when used in moderation, it was an incredible and reliable potion.

With a sigh, Ethan took a look at the other items he had gained.

As always, hardly impressive.

Even less impressive than what he had gained from the common gacha tickets the system had rewarded him with after his integration.

Tools, two sets of leather armor, and even a recipe for roast chicken.

Not exactly one of Ethan’s priorities at that moment.

With a bitter smile, Ethan removed only the chicken recipe from his inventory, which he would give to Alice.

"At least you have to give me something useful, right?" Ethan pulled out the seven premium tickets.

The system had no mercy.

He knew full well that if the system was in a bad mood, there was a chance he’d get seven pieces of grass, one after another.

[I have far less autonomy than you think.]

Ethan ignored that response, maintaining a suspicious expression as he tore up the seven premium tickets.

[You used: 7x Premium Gacha Tickets]

[Spinning...]

[Congratulations! You received: 1x Wolf Tooth Greatsword (Rank D)]

[Congratulations! You received: 1x Intermediate Alchemy Workshop Blueprint (Rank C)]

Right after the second item, an intense golden light engulfed Ethan’s office, his eyes widening.

An A-Rank item!

[Congratulations! You received: 5x Long Teleportation Scroll (Rank A)]

[Congratulations! You received: 1x Refined Cold Steel Ingot (Rank C)]

[Congratulations! You received: 1x Dawn Bow (Rank D)]

[Congratulations! You have received: 5x 70% XP Bonus Potion (Rank B)]

After the Rank A item, Ethan really wasn’t expecting anything much better than that.

But when he saw the seventh item, his lips curved from ear to ear, a huge smile taking over his face.

Alaric was fucked. So fucked.

Ethan controlled his emotions, taking a look at the three items that caught his attention the most.

[Name: 70% XP Bonus Potion

Description: A potion capable of temporarily increasing all XP gained by 70%. Each bottle can be divided into twenty doses; the effect can be amplified or diluted depending on the user’s power level.

Effect: +70% XP

Duration: 7 days.]

That item was, without a doubt, the one that surprised Ethan the most.

Five vials that could be divided into twenty doses each, a total of over seven hundred days with a 70% XP bonus.

Even if the effect diminished starting from the second stage, it was still absurd.

[Item: Intermediate Alchemy Workshop Design

Description: A design for creating an alchemy workshop. A master of formations is required.

Rank: C]

[Item: Long-Range Teleportation Scroll

Description: An item that, when torn, allows the user to teleport to a desired area within a radius of two thousand kilometers. Rank: A]

They were very useful items, but their utility would only have real value in the future.

Ethan simply kept them stored away for now.

Finally free, he picked up the book that was on his desk, The Principles of the Burning Blood Body Enhancement Skill.

After a moment’s hesitation, Ethan opened the book, his eyes falling on the first page.

Skill books in this world were not like those in games, even with the arrival of the Gaia System.

To learn a skill, it was necessary, first and foremost, to understand the intent of its creator.

Whether it was an internal skill, focused on circulating mana internally for physical enhancement or healing.

Or an external skill, like Aura Slash, the only skill Ethan had learned that could be used to launch a long-range external aura attack.

He didn’t use it much in the past, since it consumed a lot of aura and his talent didn’t help much. But now, with his superior aura and talent, he could already use it with a little more freedom.

Extending a single finger, Ethan moved his aura, forming a small line, and then flicked his finger.

An aura slash appeared, flying through the air and striking the wall in front of him, leaving only a small mark.

That tiny strike had already consumed five aura points.

If he used his sword to create a powerful strike, he would need at least a hundred.

With a soft sigh, Ethan simply began reading the book, focusing on his new ability.

With those two abilities and the seventh item he had earned with the premium gacha tickets, he was certain of one thing.

Alaric was completely fucked in his hands.
