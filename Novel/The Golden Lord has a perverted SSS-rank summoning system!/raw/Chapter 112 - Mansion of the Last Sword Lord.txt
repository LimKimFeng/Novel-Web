Chapter 112: Mansion of the Last Sword Lord

Goldenveil’s guards gathered all the loot inside the chamber behind the altar, where the emerald coffin rested.

The coffin itself was an artifact capable of slowing the aging process and even preserving the dead.

It was of no use to Ethan, but he knew something like this would sell for a fortune to noble families obsessed with immortality.

Being Rank S, it shouldn’t be worth less than a few tens of thousands of gold coins.

The rest of the loot consisted mainly of materials extracted from beasts, including a single bottle of third-stage beast blood and a few hundred bottles of second-stage beast blood.

Ethan would give the third-stage bottle to John to craft an even higher-quality green fairy wine.

This, of course, wouldn’t be a two-month project. The blood of a third-stage beast had to be used with much greater care and he’d give John the entire winter to work on it.

Other than that, there were weapons and armor of good quality. More than enough to equip all of Goldenveil’s second-stage warriors with gear suitable even for third-stage warriors.

In the end, Ethan just sighed and motioned for the guards to prepare everything for transport and seal the mine, preventing it from being overrun by beasts again.

There was still much to do. He needed to build a steel mill to refine the metal, train miners, and prepare those who would work on the ore.

The system would handle much of that, but his plans would still take at least a few months to come to fruition.

Most likely, only by the beginning of next winter would he actually be able to extract ore on a large scale.

"There’s nothing to be done..."

Ethan shook his head and, with the guard at the ready, set off for Goldenveil.

By the time they arrived, the sun was already setting on the horizon, painting the sky in fading shades of amber.

Curious eyes followed the returning guard through the village, and upon realizing there were no casualties, sighs of relief spread among the villagers.

The women prepared hearty and delicious meals, bringing them to the guard barracks with cheerful expressions.

[Name: Mansion of the Last Sword Lord

Description: A mansion design used in the construction of the residence of the last Imperial Sword Lord, created as a way to honor and pay tribute to the old warrior in his retirement.

Effect: +20% aura and mana density within a five-kilometer radius. +10 prestige.
