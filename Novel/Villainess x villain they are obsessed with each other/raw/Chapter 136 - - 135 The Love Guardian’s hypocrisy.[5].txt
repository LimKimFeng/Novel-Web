Chapter 136 -: 135 The Love Guardian’s hypocrisy.[5]

"Babe, did you hear that?" Rosalina walked over to Julius with slow steps.

Both of them had the same cold look in their eyes.

Their faces showed pure disgust.

They could not even imagine the other person being touched or looked at by someone else.

The very thought felt like their whole body was burning alive.

Rosalina nudged Julius’s cloak with her elbow and asked in a mocking voice, "Isn’t it just prostitution with extra steps?"

"Exactly," Julius nodded his head hard and crossed his arms over his chest. His voice was full of sarcasm.

"It’s just plain prostitution." He turned his sharp gaze toward the Love Guardian and frowned deeply.

"Oi, are you sure you’re the Love Guardian? Or are you just a broker from the red light district?"

The Love Guardian’s heavy armor shook with anger when he heard the insult.

"How dare you slander such pure love!" he snapped.

His glowing red eyes burned with fury. "Someone like you who has never experienced real love will never underst...."

Julius raised his hand and cut him off in the middle of the sentence.

"If this is what you call pure love," Julius paused and looked straight at Crypian and Alia, then spat on the ground, "then thanks, but we don’t need it."

His mouth twisted with disgust, as if the words left a bad smell.

’What kind of man is he?’ Julius thought while staring at Crypian.

’He just agreed to let Alia sleep with as many men as she wants. They are worse than animals that get fucked by different mates every season.’

Julius continued in a loud, mocking voice,

"If this is real love, then I would rather gouge out the eyes of anyone who even dares to look at my Ro..." He stopped suddenly and corrected himself, "I mean... my babe!"

Rosalina’s eyes lit up with happiness.

She walked closer and slipped her hand gently along his arm.

"Really, babe?" she asked with a sweet giggle. Her voice was full of joy. "I would do the same, hehe."

"Enough!" the Love Guardian roared. He could not take their constant criticism anymore.

"You evil fiends! What would you know about love?!"

He pointed a massive armored finger at Crypian and Alia. "Look at them! What do you see?"

Crypian and Alia stood there silently, holding back their burning anger.

Their insides felt like they were on fire from all the insults, but they stayed quiet.

They knew they had no chance of winning unless the Love Guardian stayed on their side.

Rosalina rubbed her chin slowly, pretending to think hard.

Then she answered in an innocent, sweet voice that dripped with sarcasm, "A male prostitute and a female prostitute?"

"You bastards!!!" the Love Guardian snarled. His deep voice shook with rage.

"That’s it! I am not asking you anything else!"

The moment those words left his mouth, both Crypian and Alia let out loud sighs of relief.

Crypian stepped forward with a small, hopeful smile. "Does that mean you will accept us as your masters?"

The Love Guardian nodded his heavy helmet. "Yes, I have decided. They have lost this test. Rest assured, there won’t be any pair with purer love than yours." He took one heavy step toward them.

But before he got closer, he turned his glowing red eyes toward Julius and Rosalina and shot them a sharp, angry glare.

"If you two want to live, then buzz off!" His voice grew louder with every word.

He felt like if he looked at them even one more second, he might explode and kill them himself.

Crypian and Alia stared at the Love Guardian.

Their hearts started beating faster and faster with excitement.

’Finally,’ Alia thought with a deep sigh of relief.

’Once this Love Guardian acknowledges us as his masters...’ She glanced sideways at the two masked figures.

’I will pay you back ten times for that humiliation!’

Crypian thought the exact same thing. His blood was still boiling from all the insults.

’Once this Love Guardian acknowledges us, my first command will be to kill them!’ He imagined it clearly.

’If I don’t turn you two into prostitutes, then I’m not Crypian Thorne!’

The Love Guardian slowly knelt down in front of Crypian and Alia.

His massive armored body made the ground shake a little. He spoke in a proud, serious voice:

"You have passed the trial! You two are admirable. Your love..."

"Prostitution!" Rosalina chimed in loudly, cutting him off with a sweet, innocent voice.

She even clapped her hands once like she was helping him finish the sentence.

Snap!

That single word made the Love Guardian completely lose it.

His eyes blazed like hot fire inside his helmet.

He whipped his head around toward Julius and Rosalina so fast that his armor creaked.

"This is the last warning!" he roared, pointing one thick finger at them. "Fuck off!"

The Love Guardian was filled with killing intent.

Right now, he wanted nothing more than to crush these two crazy bastards into pieces.

But he could not do it. This was a very important moment, so he forced himself to swallow his anger and stay calm.

But the two madmen were not giving him any peace.

"Lmao!" Rosalina burst out laughing loudly. She used the word Julius always said.

"Babe, did you see what kind of shit-ass idiot he is?"

"Right, right," Julius nodded his head heavily, agreeing with her.

A big smirk sat under his mask.

Rosalina stepped forward with confident steps.

She raised both her hands high in the air and laughed happily.

"Did you really think we came here just to get your stupid acknowledgement?"

She looked up at the sky like a true villainess and shouted, "This is the day I get my revenge!"

Julius stood there in a daze. He admired how graceful she looked while making her threat.

"That’s what we call a true villainess!" he thought proudly.

He reached into his cloak and pulled out a small artifact that looked like a modern camera.

Julius knelt down on one knee and started clicking photos like crazy.

He moved around Rosalina, taking pictures from the front, from the side, and from behind, capturing every dramatic pose she made.

The Love Guardian, Crypian, and Alia just stared at them with their mouths open, completely dumbfounded.

Rosalina kept going, her voice full of joy and sarcasm.

"If you thought you would survive this, then you are sorely mistaken, my dear!"

She turned her masked face toward the three of them. Suddenly, Boom!

A heavy wave of bloodlust burst out from her body.

It filled the air and wrapped around the Love Guardian, Crypian, and Alia like thick, dark smoke.

The pressure made it hard for them to breathe.

"Today is the day you die, Crypian!!!" she shouted, her voice ringing loud and clear.
