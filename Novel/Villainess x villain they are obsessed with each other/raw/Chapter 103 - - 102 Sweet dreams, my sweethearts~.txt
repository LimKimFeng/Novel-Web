Chapter 103 -: 102 Sweet dreams, my sweethearts~

Julius drove the cart back to his mansion.

As soon as the guards at the gate saw the cart, they quickly opened it for him.

He drove inside and stopped right in front of the main door.

Julius jumped down from the cart and stretched his arms high.

"Such a long day..." he muttered softly.

He walked over to the door of the cart and opened it with a quiet clink.

Inside, Rosalina was curled up on the seat.

She had fallen asleep while waiting for him. A soft, fond smile appeared on Julius’s face.

"She must be really tired," he whispered.

Her body was covered in bruises and small injuries.

Dried blood stained her dress. The pretty necklace she had worn for their date was broken.

Her eyes and cheeks looked swollen from crying.

Julius reached in slowly and carefully slid his hands under her.

He lifted her gently into his arms, trying his best not to wake her up.

As he picked her up, her hands slipped down and rested against him.

A small frown appeared on her sleeping face. She curled up even more, like she was hugging a soft pillow.

Julius gently brushed the strands of hair away from her face. Then he carried her into the mansion.

The lights in the hall were still on, but the place was quiet and empty.

It was very late at night, so most of the servants had already gone to sleep.

Julius did not keep any guards at the mansion. As long as Alfred was around, he did not need them.

"Rosel must have fallen asleep too," Julius said quietly.

His eyes looked up the stairs. Rosel’s room was on the upper floor.

The name "Rosel Augustus Hayes" was written nicely on the door, and the room door was decorated with dolls and other cute things.

Julius chuckled softly when he saw it.

He thought for a moment. ’Should I take Rosalina to Rosel’s room?’

There were other rooms in the mansion, but most of them were not used.

Only two rooms were comfortable to sleep in, his own room and Rosel’s room.

But he could not take Rosalina to his room.

Even though he could be crazy sometimes, he still respected certain boundaries.

After thinking it over, Julius let out a small sigh and walked up the stairs.

He stopped in front of Rosel’s room and reached out his hand and gently pushed the door open.

"Creak..."

The door slid open, and soft light from the hallway fell into the room.

The light shone on Rosel.

She was fast asleep, spread out across the bed with her arms and legs in every direction.

The bed was covered with dolls, and even her nightdress had cute dolls on it.

’She really loves dolls.’

Julius thought as he walked slowly and quietly into the room, careful not to wake her.

He tiptoed all the way to the bed.

When he reached the bed, he bent down and gently placed Rosalina beside Rosel.

’It really looks like a family,’ Julius thought.

A soft chuckle escaped his lips. Rosel called him Papa, and Rosalina her Mama.

Now that Rosalina had confessed her feelings to him, the warm feeling felt even stronger.

He looked at both of them for a moment with a gentle smile.

"I can’t wait to sleep with them one day," Julius muttered softly.

A happy, hopeful smile appeared on his lips.

He bent down and kissed Rosel’s forehead first, then Rosalina’s.

"Good dreams, my sweet little princess and empress," he whispered.

He straightened up, gave them one last loving look, and pulled the blanket gently over both of them.

"See you tomorrow," he said quietly.

He turned around to leave, but just then, a small, strong hand grabbed his.

Julius raised his eyebrow. "Hmm?"

But before he could understand what was happening.

With one swift motion, the hand pulled him hard toward the bed.

Julius lost his balance and fell forward. His body landed softly on the mattress with a gentle thud.

Before he could make sense of anything, Rosalina’s legs quickly wrapped around his.

One leg locked around his lower leg from below, and the other leg came from the upper side, trapping his legs completely so he could not move them.

At the same time, her slender arm reached out and wrapped around his chest.

She grabbed his shirt tightly and pulled him closer, holding him in a strong, sleepy hug.

Julius blinked in surprise.

"What just happened...?" he whispered, his voice soft and confused.

He was now lying on the bed, completely caught in Rosalina’s playful hold.

Her body pressed against his side, warm and cozy.

She was still half-asleep, but her grip was surprisingly strong, as if she did not want him to leave.

Julius lay on the bed and stared at the ceiling.

He could feel Rosalina’s warm breath gently brushing against his neck.

Her face was very close to his, and she was holding him tightly, just like a big pillow.

"Umh..." Rosalina made a soft sleeping sound and moved again.

She shifted closer and squeezed him even tighter.

Now her head rested comfortably on his chest.

She looked very peaceful and happy, like she had found the perfect spot to sleep.

Julius tried to gently move out of her grip, but Rosalina held on strongly.

He was surprised, she looked so slim and delicate, yet her arms were surprisingly strong.

"Damn... she is strong," Julius thought with a small smile.

He stopped trying to move and just lay there quietly.

The room was peaceful. On his other side, Rosel was still sleeping soundly with her legs spread around.

Rosalina’s soft breathing and warm body made everything feel cozy and calm.

’I didn’t think I would be sleeping with them so soon,’ Julius thought.

His eyes moved to the open door. Soft light was still coming in from the hallway.

’Now that she has grabbed me like this, what can I do?’ he muttered softly to himself.

Deep down, he couldn’t help but feel excited to sleep in the same bed with her.

He slowly stretched his right hand and snapped his fingers.

A gentle gust of wind appeared and pushed the door shut with a soft click.

Once the door closed, the room became completely dark.

Julius slowly let out a calm breath and closed his eyes.

"Sweet dreams, my sweethearts~" he whispered with a soft smile.
