Chapter 133 -: 132 The Love Guardian’s hypocrisy.[2]

Alia and Crypian fell into a deep ponder as they thought the question over.

For them, it was quite easy actually. Alia nodded to herself and spoke up.

"Love is about loving someone without expecting anything in return."

The Love Guardian leaned forward slightly. His deep voice echoed with curiosity.

"Loving someone without expecting anything in return? Explain."

Alia’s voice grew soft but full of conviction.

"Yes. For example, Crypian is admired and loved by many. I know the others are sincere too."

"So I can’t expect him to look only at me while ignoring the others. At least... this is what I believe."

She said it with strong conviction shining in her eyes.

"Love is about freedom."

Crypian glanced at her from the side. Her words touched him deeply.

He had been trying hard to keep some distance from the other girls because he didn’t want to break anyone’s heart.

But as Alia spoke, he realized there was no need to choose at all. After all, he could have them all.

The Love Guardian nodded slowly to himself, looking satisfied.

"How admirable," he muttered softly. "Giving the other person freedom..."

A nostalgic look appeared on his armored face.

It looked like he was really touched by the answer, which was in the end like prostitution.

If Julius or Rosalina were here they would call this prostitution!

What stupid description was that? Giving the other person freedom to do whatever they wanted?

And was that Love Guardian or whatever, did he have a screw loose? Why did he look so touched?

These would have been the words Julius and Rosalina would have used as they both had a completely 360 description from what Alia said. Anyways...

The Love Guardian nodded to himself as he wiped the corner of his eyes though his eyes couldn’t be seen.

Still, he wiped it.

"Excellent answer!" he said finally, managing to speak.

His tone was slightly emotional. "Now let’s move on to the next question."

Alia and Crypian let out a sigh of relief as they smiled at each other.

The Love Guardian paused for a bit.

He looked at Crypian and Alia with his glowing red eyes, then spoke softly but firmly.

"If the person you love chooses someone else..."

He paused and looked at the two of them carefully.

Alia already understood what the full question would be, and she knew her answer too.

Still, she stayed quiet. She turned her head slightly toward Crypian.

Her eyes softened with calmness and tenderness as she waited for the Love Guardian to finish.

The Love Guardian’s eyes shone with a bright red light inside his helmet.

He continued in his deep voice:

"...would you still love that person?"

After the Love Guardian finished, Alia and Crypian fell silent for a moment.

The red magic circle around them glowed quietly.

Then Alia stepped forward again. Her voice came out firm and clear.

"I would still love that person... and wait!" she said.

"Love only happens once. That’s why no matter how hard you try, you can’t forget that person."

She said it while completely ignoring all the logic in the world.

Earlier she had said she didn’t mind Crypian having as many girls as possible.

What did that mean now? No one knew.

It was a bit confusing, but one thing was sure, The Love Guardian was retarded.

The Love Guardian wiped the corner of his eye again, even though no one could see any tears.

"Such devotion!" he muttered.

His deep voice turned a bit shaky with emotion.

Crypian stared at Alia with warm eyes. A soft, grateful feeling filled his chest.

He didn’t say anything, but inside he felt even closer to her.

’Such a kind girl...’ Crypian thought with softness in his eyes.

He couldn’t explain what he felt in his heart towards Alia right now.

Her devotion was enough to melt even the hardest metal.

It was really admirable to see someone like her in real life these days.

The Love Guardian cleared his thoughts and turned his voice heavy again.

"Now the last question!" he said while lifting his chin in pride.

"You both have answered the two questions honestly, and the answers were absolutely the truth. Now, if you manage to answer the last question correctly, I shall accept you as my masters!"

Alia and Crypian nodded at each other.

Their faces were soft and full of belief in one another.

They turned towards the Love Guardian together.

"Please continue," they said at the same time.

"Very well," the Love Guardian spoke in his deep, powerful voice. He raised his huge red sword slightly for emphasis.

"The last question is..."

He paused dramatically, his red eyes glowing brighter inside the helmet.

"...Would you stay with the person you love the most, even if it means harming them?"

The moment those words left his mouth, Crypian didn’t hesitate for even a second.

He stepped forward immediately and answered with a strong, clear voice full of resolve.

"No!" he said firmly. "If staying with the person causes them harm, then it’s better if you do not stay with them!"

Alia nodded right away and stepped forward too. She looked straight at Crypian with calm, loving eyes.

"I would do the same," she said softly but with complete certainty. "I would love him... but I will keep my distance!"

They both showed strong resolve on their faces.

Their eyes were clear and determined as they stood side by side inside the glowing red magic circle.

"Sniff... sniff... sniff!"

The Love Guardian couldn’t hold it any longer.

He started sniffling loudly, his massive armored shoulders shaking a little.

"Such pure love!" he cried out, his deep voice cracking with emotion.

Tears were now clearly flowing inside his helmet. "Sniff... There is no one more worthy of becoming my masters than you two!"

He sniffled again, wiped his eyes with one armored hand, and slowly walked down from the stone platform while still holding his huge red sword.

He stopped right in front of Crypian and Alia.

Then, with a loud metallic clank, the mighty Love Guardian knelt down on one knee before them.

"You have passed the trial and answered all three questions perfectly!" he declared, his voice thick with happy tears.

"From today onwards, I shall be your guardian."

He paused and looked up at the two of them, the young love birds standing together.

His red eyes shone with pride and warmth.

"I shall call you my..."

Just as he was about to fully accept them as his masters and finish the sentence, a sudden loud voice echoed from the entrance of the trial room.

"Oi, Love Guardian!"
