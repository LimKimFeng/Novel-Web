"Catch them! Catch them! Don’t kill them! Capture them alive!"

The pursuers shouted loudly as they chased Julius and Rosalina without stopping.

They were dead set on grabbing the queen.

Rosalina frowned softly and looked back.

"But how will you kill them?" she asked, her voice a little worried.

The horse galloped deeper and deeper into the thick forest.

Tall pine trees stood all around them, their branches heavy with bright white snow.

The air felt cold and fresh, and the trees looked beautiful in the soft winter light.

Julius turned his head slightly and studied the trees.

These pines were a bit different from the ones he knew on Earth.

At the very top of each tree, there was a strange box-shaped cluster of needles and cones.

As he was observing the trees, suddenly, a memory clicked in his mind.

It wasn’t from his own life on Earth, it came from this world’s Julius.

When he was a little boy, his family had gone on a trip to the hills.

Back then, young Julius had been excited to play with fire.

He had lit a small spark near one of these special pine trees... and it had exploded like a bomb!

Even though the trees looked peaceful and covered in thick snow, one tiny flame could make the whole thing burst into a fiery blast.

These trees were known as the explosive pines. They were....a fire path resources and could be used in making mid grade fire path treasures.

A wicked idea popped into his head the moment he realised it.

Julius let out a creepy little laugh.

His grin stretched wide under the helmet, turning into a mischievous, almost evil smile.

"Whoa!" Rosalina stared at him and froze for a second.

"What’s with that creepy smile?" she muttered. It looked like he was planning something really evil.

"Oi!" she called out, poking his armored side.

His thoughts snapped back. "Uhh... you called?" Julius asked, still looking at her through the small eye slits of his helmet.

"Yeah..." Rosalina nodded, her brows pulling together in a cute little frown.

"What were you thinking? You were smiling so creepily!"

"Hehehe..." Julius laughed again in that playful, wicked way. He looked at her with warm, loving eyes.

"No matter what body you’re in, and no matter where we are... you will always be mine."

"Even if you change bodies, ugly or beautiful, you still belong to me. That’s what I believe."

"Those guys said they would hand you over to the prince... They looked at what is mine. They must die!!"

Julius muttered the last part with dark excitement.

He reached one hand into a small pocket hidden in his golden armor.

His fingers closed around a simple lighter, the kind every knight carried for emergencies.

"I will kill them all!" he declared, his voice full of fun, wicked energy.

They rushed forward like hungry wolves, completely unaware of the danger hiding in the beautiful pine trees around them.

"There they are!"

"Huh?"
