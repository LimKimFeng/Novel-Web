"Ciel."

Grant your wish. Grant your wish.

GRANT YOUR WISH.

A perfect child...golden hair...Beautiful face....Elegant aura. A child so pretty that entire kingdoms would probably collapse from jealousy.

Crypian Thorne had once been her best option. But compared to Uriel? That man now looked like a random potato standing beside the sun.
