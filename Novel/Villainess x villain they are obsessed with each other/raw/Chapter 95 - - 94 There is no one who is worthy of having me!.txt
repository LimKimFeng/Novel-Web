Chapter 95 -: 94 There is no one who is worthy of having me!

"Your father sent you the details four months ago, didn’t he?"

The First Elder clasped his hands together. "You received the letter from Nickel, right?"

"I received it," Rosalina snapped, her voice sharp.

"Good," the Elder continued. "Our family has always wanted to take over the entire continent of Auranel from those Hayes dogs."

"We have been talking with the Thorne family for a long time. If it weren’t for your useless mother, we would have already secured a strong alliance by now."

Rosalina’s hands clenched tightly. Veins popped up on the back of her hands.

She gritted her teeth hard. Talking about her mother like that made her blood boil. ’One day I will make all of you regret this,’ she thought angrily.

The First Elder kept speaking, his voice turning serious.

"For the past few years, we have been discussing this deal. The patriarch of the Thorne family has proposed a marriage alliance between you and his youngest son, Crypian Thorne. In return, he will give us the full strength of the Thorne family."

He looked straight into Rosalina’s eyes.

"Your father expects you to be useful to the clan. So you better not refuse." His tone was a clear threat.

’Things have turned out perfectly,’ Nickel thought with satisfaction.

’If the villainess marries the protagonist, it will be much easier. Even if she doesn’t agree right away, it won’t matter.’ He glanced at Rosalina.

She looked calm on the outside, but he knew she hated the Thornes.

It might take longer to brainwash her completely.

Still, after investigating everything, he had come up with this smart plan. Why kill her when he could use her instead?

The Thorne clan had a powerful brainwashing spell.

With it, they could control Rosalina easily.

And with her strong talent, almost equal to the main villain’s, they could definitely kill Julius later.

"So you better agree," Nickel said, a smirk fixed on his face. "Or else..."

"I refuse!" Rosalina cut him off before he could finish.

She crossed her legs and lifted her chin high, looking extremely arrogant. Her eyes were icy cold.

"There is no one worthy enough to have me accept him!"

She looked down at everyone in the room like they were beneath her.

"And now that you already know what I was doing with the Hayes heir, I will admit it honestly."

She paused for a moment, letting her words sink in.

Everyone stared at her with cold eyes, even the maids.

"Only Julius is worthy of having me. No one else," she declared.

Then she turned to the maids and snapped, "You peasants, keep your eyes down or I will gouge them out!"

She looked back at the First Elder.

She had planned to endure everything until she was strong enough to kill them all, but this was too much.

Leaving Julius? Impossible. There was no way she could do that.

Rosalina met the Elder’s eyes and said firmly, "I would rather die than be with someone else!"

She let out a cold snort and raised her middle finger at them. "Go eat shit, you motherfuckers!"

The First Elder stared at Rosalina in silence.

Nickel stared too, a bit surprised.

He hadn’t expected her to be so bold or to already be in love with the villain.

Still, it wasn’t that shocking, considering their relationship in the story.

"Gahgahgahgah..." The First Elder laughed in a creepy way as he slowly stood up.

His back was slightly hunched, making him look shorter than he really was.

"I knew you would say that!" he said, walking toward her.

"After all, your pride won’t let you accept something you think is unworthy."

He stepped closer.

Rosalina looked up at him, her eyes still burning with the same intensity.

She stared right back without fear. "Hmph!" she let out a cold snort.

"What can you do? Kill me? Then go ahead and do it!"

"But remember one thing!" She looked at both of them, a smirk forming on her face.

"Don’t think you can escape. It’s only a matter of time until he grows strong enough..."

She wrinkled her nose in disgust. "...strong enough to crush you like the pests you are!"

The First Elder circled around her slowly, then stopped right in front of her.

"Gahgahgah... Who said we will kill you?"

"No, which fool would kill a weapon like you?" His voice was thick with greed.

"We won’t kill you. After all, you will become the greatest weapon for us, both with your body and your soul!"

"You!!" Rosalina snapped.

She was about to pull out her sword, right at that moment, the First Elder kicked her hard in the stomach.

Bam!

Before she could react, the powerful kick sent her flying across the room.

Thud!

Rosalina crashed into the wall and fell to the ground.

"Guh!" She spat out a mouthful of blood onto the floor.

"I want to see," Nickel stepped forward with a wicked grin stretching across his lips, "where all your arrogance goes now!"

He spat her next words like venom.

"When everyone uses you in every way possible, you evil bitch!"

"Ugh..." Rosalina groaned, trying hard to control her breathing.

"Fuck!" she cursed under her breath. ’At this rate...’

She couldn’t finish the thought before another kick landed hard on her right shoulder.

Bam!

This kick came from Nickel.

"How does it feel to get crushed by the ant you looked down on your whole life, huh? Bitch, do you feel it now?"

Rosalina clutched her shoulder tightly. The kick had fractured her arm.

"Ugh..." She gritted her teeth as more blood trickled from her mouth.

"Just... wait for the day...!" she growled weakly.

She looked up at them, her eyes burning with pure indignation and hatred.

"This is nothing!" she growled through gritted teeth.

She tried to push herself up from the floor, her body shaking. "If you think that’s enough to break me, then—"

Bam!

Another brutal kick slammed into her stomach before she could finish.

The force knocked the air out of her lungs.

"Shut up, bitch!" Nickel snarled. "From now on, you will be nothing but a slave!"
