Chapter 96 -: 95 Brainwashing spell?

The moment Nickel had transmigrated into this world, the memories of the original Nickel flooded his mind.

Those memories were full of pain and inferiority.

Everyone had looked down on him after Rosalina was born.

Before she came, he was the most talented child in the family and everyone loved him.

But everything changed when Rosalina arrived.

Rosalina Valentina was a once-in-a-generation genius.

She was born with legendary Rose-petal eyes. From that day, all the attention shifted from him to her.

Then came her awakening.

When she awakened her core, she showed S-rank talent.

Her power grew fast, and in just a few years she had completely surpassed him.

One day she challenged him to a fight.

At that time, her mother had already been killed by the Thornes.

She defeated him with just one spell.

He knelt on the ground in shock as she looked down at him and said one word:

"Trash!"

"How does it feel, you bitch?!"

Nickel snarled as he kept kicking Rosalina hard in the stomach again and again.

"Getting beaten by this trash, does it feel good? Tell me, you trash!!"

He wasn’t the real Nickel, but he felt all of the original Nickel’s pain and hatred.

On top of that, he had always hated Rosalina from the moment the book was released.

She was the most hated character in the story, so beating her now felt satisfying.

"Cough! Cough! Cough!"

Rosalina coughed out dark blood. Her face had turned pale.

Both her hands were broken, and several ribs were cracked.

"You went on a date with that fucker?" Nickel growled.

"I will make sure you kill him with your own hands!"

He raised his foot to kick her again, but the First Elder stopped him.

"Enough!" the Elder said as he sat back down on the sofa.

"We need her alive for the brainwashing."

He pulled out a cigarette, put it in his mouth, and lit it with a clink.

He took a deep puff and blew out the smoke.

"We need her to help us dominate all three floating continents. Don’t use too much force, or her core might get damaged."

"Hmph!" Nickel spat on Rosalina before turning around and walking back to the sofa.

He took a glass, poured some wine into it, and drank deeply.

"Bloody bitch!" he snorted, taking another big sip.

Rosalina dragged her limp body slowly toward the wall.

"Cough! Cough!" She coughed out another mouthful of blood.

Sharp pain shot through every part of her body.

Her left eye was half-closed from the beating.

Bruises covered her face and body.

No one would believe this was the same cheerful Rosalina from this morning, the one who had been so excited for her date with Julius.

’Is this how my life ends?’ she thought.

Bitterness filled her heart. She had so many things she still wanted to do.

Just this morning she had dreamed of confessing her feelings to Julius, dating him in secret, taking revenge on her enemies, and then marrying him so they could live a happy life together.

But everything had fallen apart in one night.

Now she was beaten half to death.

They were keeping her alive only to sell her off and use her as a weapon against Julius.

’I couldn’t even tell him how much I love him...’ Rosalina bit her lip hard to hold back her tears.

She would never see the look on his face when she finally confessed.

And what hurt even more was that he would never know how deeply she loved him.

He would definitely take revenge once he found out what happened.

She was sure of that. But he would never know her true feelings.

’I’m sorry, Mom...’ Fresh tears rolled down her bruised cheeks.

’I couldn’t avenge you. I promised I would kill them all...’

The tears fell uncontrollably now. She had worked so hard for revenge, but in the end she was dying here. ’I’m sorry...’

Rosalina’s tears kept rolling down her cheeks.

She thought about Julius, and the pain in her eyes grew even deeper.

His face floated clearly in her mind. "If the world is empty, I will fill it for you."

That was what he had said to her. And she had truly believed him.

But she never got the chance to tell him what she could do for him.

She would have done anything for Julius... but now that bright future was gone.

’They will sell me after brainwashing me to that son of a bitch...’ Her hatred for the Thorne family burned even hotter.

’However, I refuse!’

"There is no one who has the right to own me except Julius!"

She thought fiercely. "I would rather die than become a slave!"

No matter what they did, her pride would never allow her to live like that.

She would rather die than be controlled by someone else.

She took a deep breath to calm herself, then let it out as a long sigh.

Suddenly, she shouted at the top of her voice,

"You sons of bitches!"

The moment she shouted, everyone in the hall turned to look at her.

Nickel also looked over with a smirk.

"It seems she still has some life left, huh? Wasn’t the beating enough?"

He spoke in a mocking voice, staring at her with irritation and hatred.

But Rosalina’s expression didn’t change.

She stayed calm and kept her usual arrogant look.

"It seems her arrogance hasn’t faded..." The First Elder clicked his tongue and stood up.

"She needs another beating!" He started walking toward her.

However, Rosalina let out a cold snort.

"Hmph! You pieces of shit! If you think you are enough to break my arrogance, then go eat shit!"

She raised her hand and showed them her middle finger. "You motherfuckers!"

Right after she said that, she moved her tongue across her teeth.

’This is goodbye... Julius...’ she thought.

She closed her eyes and was about to bite down hard on her tongue.

The First Elder and Nickel’s eyes widened in shock. They rushed forward to stop her.

But at that exact moment...

Boom!

The heavy door burst open with a loud crash.

Rosalina froze, her teeth just touching her tongue.

All the servants froze too. Even Nickel and the First Elder turned their heads toward the door in surprise.

A calm, slightly playful voice echoed through the hall.

"My, my... quite the crowd here..."
