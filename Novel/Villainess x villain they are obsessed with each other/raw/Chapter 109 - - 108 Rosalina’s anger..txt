Chapter 109 -: 108 Rosalina’s anger.

"Why did you make him so handsome?!"

Up on the top floor of the student council president’s office, Rosalina grabbed Julius by the collar.

Her eyes were shooting fire with rage.

Julius quickly held her hands and spoke in a wronged voice..

"Rose, listen to me... It’s not what you think. I had my reasons for making him so handsome. Please listen..."

Just a few moments ago, Rosalina had been sitting happily on his lap.

They were enjoying their time together while watching the scene below.

But the moment she saw Uriel’s face and Cecilia getting so close to him, a huge wave of jealousy hit her.

She jumped up and grabbed him by the collar without thinking.

"Speak! If you leave out even one thing, get ready for a beating!"

She snapped, still holding his collar tightly.

Julius let out a big sigh of relief. At least she was ready to listen now.

He quickly explained, "I made him so handsome on purpose... so he could seduce Cecilia...."

"Bam!"

Before he could finish, Rosalina’s fist flew straight into his face and sent him flying across the room.

"You son of a bitch!!" she shouted, her face turning bright red with anger.

Julius crashed into the wall. A big swollen mark appeared on his cheek.

"Rose... let me explain..."

But Rosalina was too angry to listen.

Steam seemed to come out of her ears and face.

Her eyes had turned red. She cracked both her fists loudly.

"You can explain... after the beating!"

She said through gritted teeth and marched toward him.

For the next ten minutes, the room was filled with loud sounds:

"Bam! Boom! Bam! Guh!"

"Nooo! Please! I didn’t..."Bam!"

"I’m sorry.....I won’t do it again....ahhh...please spare me!! No, no, not this I will dieeee..."Bam!!"

After ten full minutes, Julius was sitting on the floor with his legs folded under him.

His whole face looked swollen and messed up.

Rosalina was gently rubbing medicine on the wounds she had just given him.

"I needed him to be more beautiful than anyone else," Julius said with tears in his eyes, "because he has to seduce the eldest princess."

"She is obsessed with beauty. Like I told you before, I sent the avatar for two big goals. I want him to control the empire using his special eyes and status."

He continued, "There are two groups that hold real power, the Church and the royal family."

"So he needs to make Cecilia completely obsessed with him. That way, he can control her... and through her, control the power."

Rosalina interrupted while still putting medicine on his face, "But couldn’t you just do a coup?"

"The Church has a lot of influence on the people. You could just frame the royal family and...."

"No," Julius cut her off quickly. "If I did a coup, I would have to share power with the people."

"And people are divided. They always go wherever they get more benefits. What I need is "absolute power"."

Rosalina hummed softly as she thought about his words.

Now that Julius had explained everything, it actually made sense.

But then she paused. Her eyes landed on the big swollen bump on his cheek, the one she had just made herself a few minutes ago.

She tilted her head and continued, "You said he has a different consciousness and his own memories, right?"

A haughty expression appeared on her face.

She lifted her chin proudly and gave a wink. "So what if he falls for me? After all, I’m very beautiful."

"You think I’m an idiot?" Julius cut in before she could say anything more.

He looked at her with a tired expression.

Rosalina blinked in surprise.

Julius continued, "My mom once did something similar. She created an avatar, changed its face and everything, just to prank my father."

"At first, Father played along and pretended not to know. But as time passed, the avatar’s feelings for Father grew stronger and stronger."

"In the end, the avatar refused to listen to Mom at all. She even tried to kill Mom so she could have Father all to herself."

Rosalina leaned in closer, her eyes wide with interest. "Then?"

Julius let out a long sigh. "Well... Mom killed her. Poor second mom died..." He clicked his tongue.

"I know she was actually very kind. But the avatar envied mom. She saw how beautiful her life was with her husband and son, so she decided to steal it all for herself."

He paused for a moment, then smiled confidently at Rosalina.

"But I’m not that stupid," Julius said. "I erased all the memories of you and my feelings for you from Uriel’s mind."

"And best of all, I can kill him with just a single thought. So even if he starts thinking about betraying me, it won’t matter. I can end him anytime I want."

"So, do you understand now?" Julius asked while gently rubbing his swollen cheeks.

"We are different. Uriel is just an avatar. His only job is to control the Holy Empire for me. Nothing more."

"Hmph!" He let out a soft snort and looked at her with a wronged face.

"I said let me explain and explain, but you still beat me up anyway."

"Now now, don’t be angry, darling," Rosalina said sweetly.

She pulled his head gently and rested it on her soft shoulder.

She started patting his back like a mother calming a child.

"You know how things are these days. People treat women like doll collections. Just take that mother fucker Crypian Thorne for example..."

She paused for a moment, trying to remember the right words.

Her fingers kept softly stroking his hair.

"He already has around six women," she continued.

"They all have different hair colors and different types. It’s like... what you said..."

"Pokemon collection?" Julius finished her sentence for her.

He chuckled softly even though his face still hurt.

"And of different types too, like water type, grass type, fire type, and even ground type. In the future, he will become the Pokemon Master."

He laughed a little more as he remembered all the details he had shared with her earlier.

"And not to mention, he even has a legendary Pokemon from the four stationary continents. Sigh... some people die of thirst while others..."

"What?" Rosalina snapped suddenly.

She stopped patting his back and pulled away a little to look at his face.

Her eyes narrowed.

"Nothing, dear..." Julius said quickly, giving her an innocent smile.

He quickly rested his head back on her shoulder before she could get angry again.

Rosalina stared at him for a few seconds, then let out a small huff.

She started patting his back once more, but this time a bit harder.

"You better not be hiding anything from me, Julius. I already gave you one beating today. I can easily give you another."

Julius winced but kept smiling. "Of course not, my love. I told you everything already."

"Uriel has no memories of you or my real feelings. He is just a tool. A very handsome tool, but still just a tool."

Rosalina hummed softly, satisfied with his answer.

She hugged his head closer to her chest and whispered, "Good boy. Just remember, if that avatar ever looks at me the wrong way, I will beat both of you."

Julius closed his eyes and enjoyed her warmth.

Even with a swollen face and sore cheeks, being in her arms made everything feel better.

"Yes, dear..."
