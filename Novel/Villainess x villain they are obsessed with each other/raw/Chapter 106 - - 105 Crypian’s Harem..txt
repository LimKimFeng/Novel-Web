Chapter 106 -: 105 Crypian’s Harem.

While Julius and Rosalina were settling in after everything that happened yesterday, meanwhile, Crypian Thorne and Alia Ruth were at the training grounds.

They were dueling each other fiercely.

Sweat covered their faces, and both looked like they were about to collapse from tiredness.

"That’s it for today!"

The Dean’s loud voice suddenly rang out just as they were about to start another round.

He stepped forward, stroking his messy beard with a big grin.

"Hmm, you two are doing way better than I expected!"

Both Crypian and Alia quickly bowed to the Dean.

"Thank you for your guidance," they said together, looking very grateful.

"Hahaha!" The Dean laughed warmly and patted both of them lightly on the head.

"Don’t mention it. I’m just taking care of my students."

He smiled gently at them.

Looking at these two hardworking kids reminded him of his own younger days.

Back then, he wasn’t very talented either.

He was just stubborn and worked harder than everyone else.

But he knew hard work alone wasn’t enough.

If his old teacher hadn’t believed in him, he wouldn’t be standing here today as Dean.

So now, he wanted to be that same kind of teacher, someone who had faith in his students.

"Haaa..." The Dean let out a long sigh as old memories flooded back.

Then he waved his hand and said in a cheerful voice.

"Alright, that’s enough for now. Go rest a little. Today is a very important day, after all!"

He shooed them away with a playful smile, like a grandpa sending his grandkids off to eat snacks.

Crypian and Alia walked out of the training grounds side by side.

After they stepped outside, Alia kept stealing quick glances at him from the corner of her eye.

She looked like she wanted to say something but couldn’t get the words out.

Her face slowly turned redder and redder with every second she spent next to him.

’I didn’t think someone from such a big family could be this hardworking,’ she thought, sneaking another sideways look at him.

Since the day she met him, Crypian had always been humble and kind.

He didn’t talk much, but he was very gentle. And most importantly...

"Crypian," she finally spoke, breaking her own thoughts.

"Yes?" Crypian answered without stopping his walk.

Alia fiddled nervously with a strand of her hair.

Her cheeks burned pink as she said softly, "I... I wanted to thank you. If it weren’t for you that day... I don’t know what would have happened."

Just a few days ago, Zain Thorne had been chasing after her for a while.

He used something to make her fall unconscious and took her to an unknown place.

It would have become a nightmare if Crypian hadn’t saved her.

"And since that day..." Alia felt a strange warm tingling in her heart every time she was near him.

She took a deep breath and asked in a shaky, shy voice, "If you are okay with it... can we... can we go out?"

Her face turned completely red, like a ripe tomato.

Crypian stopped walking and turned to look at her.

A soft chuckle escaped his lips. "Sure," he said with a small smile, "but why are you so nervous?"

Alia’s nervousness grew even more. She quickly looked away, her face burning red.

She was clearly in love with him.

If Julius was here, he would have been smiling from ear to ear, giving his blessings so these two could get all lovey-dovey soon.

That way, he could torture Crypian later.

"Well... I... you see..." Alia began, stammering like a lovesick girl. "I... lo...."

Just as she was about to say the next word—

"Crypian!"

A loud voice called from behind them.

Four girls walked towards them.

The moment they saw Crypian, their eyes started shining brightly like stars.

All four of them looked incredibly beautiful.

So beautiful that each one could easily win a beauty contest for their whole country.

And every single one of them looked very attracted to Crypian.

"Did you finish your training?" one girl asked with a sweet smile.

She had beautiful violet eyes and long black hair that flowed down her back.

Her smile was bright like the full moon and could make any boy’s heart skip a beat.

Her name was Diana Violet.

She came from the continent of Vahana, which was shaped like a bird, and it was also the home of the Throne family.

Another girl stepped closer, looking shy.

"I... I brought lunch..." she said softly.

Even though she was shy, she looked like an angel with her white hair and bright blue eyes.

The pink blush on her cheeks made her look even prettier.

She was Kitone, she was from the eastern part of the Vahana continent where the first ever democracy was established.....she was the daughter of the current Premier.

"Hey! I told you today was my turn!"

A girl with fiery red hair and green eyes spoke up. She held out a basket and smiled playfully.

"Would you like to eat with me?" Her name was Ciel Scarlett...she was from the Holy empire, the only daughter of the mighty Scarlett family.

The last girl stayed a little behind the others.

She had green hair and green eyes.

She seemed very shy and tried to hide, but she kept sneaking quick glances at Crypian every few seconds.....

She was the most influential among these girls, her name was Sylviana Fairyborn...she was the eldest daughter of the Fairyborn empire, which was one of the main leading forces of the Vahana continent.

If Julius was here, even he would be shocked.

How many girls had Crypian already gathered? It looked like a whole collection!

They were all different types, like grass type, water type, fire type... *ahem*...he would definitely say something like that...

Crypian didn’t say anything at first. He just smiled softly.

A warm feeling spread inside his chest.

All these girls were looking out for him, and none of them wanted anything in return.

He had helped each of them in different situations, but they still cared about him like this.

Coming to this academy felt like the greatest luck of his life.

In his family, everyone only thought about themselves. But here, he finally felt truly warm and welcome.

"Guys," he called out with a gentle smile.

The moment he spoke, all four girls turned to look at him at once.

"Want to eat something together?" he asked.

"Yeah!" Diana agreed right away, her violet eyes sparkling.

"If you say so..." the others replied, nodding shyly or excitedly.

Crypian chuckled again. Seeing them like this really made him happy.

Though he was not sure about their feelings.

They all seemed to like him a lot, but right now he saw them only as good friends.

’Still... nothing is certain yet.’

"Okay, let’s go then," he said with a small laugh.
