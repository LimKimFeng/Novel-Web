Chapter 81 -: 80 Rosalina thinks about confessing?[1]

As Julius continued to recover at his mansion, things were very different in the Student Council President’s room at the academy.

Inside the fancy room, Rosalina sat at her desk looking completely lifeless.

On the outside she looked perfect as always, but inside she felt empty and sad.

"Julius... sob... my poor Julius..." she quietly cried to herself.

Her eyes looked hollow, and even though she had covered the tear marks with heavy makeup, the sadness was clear.

It all started five days ago. Julius had left the auditorium to "steal" whatever he needed.

During her victory speech, Rosalina suddenly heard the shocking news, some thieves had broken in and brutally tortured Julius when he tried to stop them.

She knew it was all an act.

But when she rushed to the infirmary and saw him lying there covered in painful wounds from head to toe, she nearly broke down.

She knew the truth: that crazy guy had done all of it to himself just to throw off suspicion.

"Haa..." Rosalina let out a tired sigh and rested her head on the table.

"I can’t even go see him right now... I wish I could. But until I fully secure my position as the heir, I can’t risk it."

She missed him so much.

The moment she thought about his stupid, confident grin, her heart ached even more.

Just then "poof!"

A small puff of white smoke appeared, and a cute angel version of Rosalina popped out in front of her.

"Then go and see him," the angel Rosalina said cheerfully as she landed lightly on the desk.

Rosalina lifted her head and looked at her angel self with a frown.

"But how? That son of a bitch Nickel is roaming around this city. I’m sure he would notice if I went there."

She looked frustrated. "Sometimes I really think I should just give up everything and run away to be with him."

"But you are well aware..." "poof!"

Before she could finish, another puff of smoke appeared.

This time, a completely different Rosalina popped out.

She had an unmatched powerful aura, wore a luxurious dark dress, and hid half her face behind an elegant fan.

Only her sharp, cold eyes could be seen. This was the infamous villainess Rosalina.

"You can’t!" the villainess version said firmly.

"You must not do that. Only by staying in this position can we plan our revenge properly. Without the clan’s power, it will be almost impossible."

The angel Rosalina nodded and crossed her arms.

"I think she is right," she said. Then she added with a gentle smile, "However, it isn’t necessary that you have to hide everything from Julius."

"What do you mean?" both the real Rosalina and the villainess version asked at the same time, turning toward the angel with curious eyes.

The angel Rosalina leaned forward and uncrossed her arms. She looked super serious as she spoke.

"You know how much he trusts us, right?"

"Yeah," the villainess Rosalina nodded.

"He told us everything, from the very start of his plans all the way to the end." She urged the angel to keep going.

"Then how about we trust him too?" the angel said with a bright smile.

The moment those words left her mouth...

"Poof~ Poof~ Poof~ Poof~ Poof~ Poof~ Poof~"

One after another, loud puffs of colorful smoke filled the room.

From the smoke, many different versions of Rosalina suddenly appeared!

A cool detective Rosalina popped out holding an unlit cigarette.

Then came the arrogant Rosalina with her nose in the air, the obsessive Rosalina with sparkling heart eyes, the devilish Rosalina with little horns and her beloved trident, the merchant Rosalina counting gold coins, and many more.

Soon the entire student council president’s room was crowded with all kinds of Rosalinas, all staring at the angel version with big curious eyes.

The angel Rosalina looked around in surprise.

"Whoa... everyone?" She saw all the curious faces and felt touched.

Her eyes grew a little watery as she opened her mouth to say thank you.

Before she could even say a single word of gratitude, all the Rosalinas spoke together in perfect unison, their voices full of excitement:

"What do you mean?!"

"Ahem..." The angel Rosalina coughed lightly to get everyone’s attention again and quickly got back to her topic.

"My apologies," the angel Rosalina said with a small smile.

She turned toward the main Rosalina and continued, "So what I was saying is... now that we all know he definitely loves us," she emphasized the word "us" while glancing at a few versions who had rather wild attitudes, "how about we tell him how we feel?"

"Gasp!"

"What do you mean?!"

"Wouldn’t that just expose us?"

"Right? I’m sure those assholes would definitely find out!"

Gasps of surprise and disagreement filled the room.

However, the main Rosalina raised one hand and said firmly, "Shh! Let her finish first."

For the first time, all the Rosalinas listened and quietly calmed down.

The angel Rosalina gave the main Rosalina a grateful look before continuing.

"What I’m suggesting is..." She paused for dramatic effect, making everyone lean in closer.

"How about we tell him about our real circumstances?"

"I know," she quickly raised both hands to stop any arguments, "that means we will be showing our weaknesses. But think about it, it’s Julius who told us all about his plans first."

"He even told us about the avatar he sent to the church as the Saint candidate. So how about we tell him everything?"

She explained with shining eyes, "In public, we will still behave like enemies."

She paused again, looking at all the excited faces around her. "But in private... we will behave like a real couple."

Her words stopped there.

Her cheeks turned a soft pink as she added shyly, "...and we can do all the things normal couples do."

"What do you guys think?" the angel Rosalina asked with a bright smile.

"I think it would be a win-win! If we just tell him how we feel, we won’t have to work so hard hiding our emotions anymore. At the same time, we can love him as much as our hearts want."

She turned toward the devil Rosalina and the obsessive Rosalina, clearly emphasizing her next words while looking straight at them.

"Think about it... we can even kiss him on the lips... lips... lips..." She repeated the word playfully, making her voice echo like an exciting after-effect.

"Gulp!"

"Gulp!"

"Gulp!"

A few loud gulps echoed in the room as many of the Rosalinas suddenly imagined it.

Their faces turned red and their eyes went wide with excitement.

The angel didn’t stop there.

She leaned forward with a mischievous grin and added, "And who knows... maybe we can even move ahead... ahead... ahead... ahead..."
