Chapter 86 -: 85 Reaching the heaven with effort.

"Rose, listen to me!" Julius called out as he rushed after Rosalina.

The moment she saw Crypian, her anger had exploded, and it was growing stronger by the second.

Julius quickly pulled her out of the classroom before she could do anything reckless.

If she attacked Crypian now and somehow killed him, he would regress back in time with all his memories.

Julius wouldn’t remember anything, but Crypian would. That would cause a huge hassle.

Rosalina walked ahead with her hands clenched tightly.

Veins popped out all over her arms and neck from how hard she was gripping.

Julius hurried forward and gently grabbed her hand.

"Come with me," he said softly, leading her toward the academy gate.

She was still burning with rage.

Keeping her like this wasn’t good, she needed something to release all that anger on.

’I didn’t think she would be this angry...’ Julius thought.

He knew she hated the Thorne name, but he didn’t expect her to lose control so completely.

He took her outside the academy and headed toward a small mountain about one kilometer away.

It looked like a triangle covered in trees, just like the mountain behind Nobita’s school.

Without warning, Julius picked her up in his arms and flew toward the mountain.

Rosalina didn’t resist at all. It was clear she also wanted to vent her anger badly.

After a short flight, they reached the peak. Julius gently set her down.

"Go and release your anger," he said, taking a few steps back.

He sat comfortably under a big oak tree, leaning against its trunk.

Rosalina didn’t say a word. She simply walked deeper into the forest.

Julius watched her go and let out a long sigh. "Haa..."

"Though I know he can regress, I don’t know how many times he can do it," he muttered to himself.

If Crypian’s regressions were infinite, the only way to stop him would be to destroy that cheating system.

There had even been times when Crypian regressed without dying.

Keeping him alive forever wasn’t an option, he wasn’t immortal.

"Boom! Boom! Boom!"

Loud explosions suddenly echoed from the forest.

Rosalina had started her rampage.

Large trees began falling one after another, making it look like a small landslide.

Julius clasped his hands behind his neck and leaned back casually.

"I also need to make sure she doesn’t mess things up. If she accidentally kills him and he regresses, it will become really hard to deal with."

This was Crypian’s first time at the academy, if he hadn’t already regressed twice before.

In his very first life, he was treated like a hunting dog by his family and wasn’t even allowed to attend the academy.

One day, his brother, the new patriarch, decided he was useless and had him killed.

But what his brother didn’t know was that Crypian had regressed.

That was his 13th regression, and his longest one yet.

When he came back, an overpowered system appeared and gave him rapid growth and powerful cheat skills.

Regression was just one of them.

Now he wanted revenge.

Just like every typical novel, he came to the academy to gather his harem and grow stronger.

Julius sighed again.

As time passed, the explosions and falling trees slowly became fewer.

Finally, Rosalina walked out of the forest.

Her face and white uniform were soaked in sweat, clinging tightly to her body.

Her cheeks were flushed red from exhaustion.

"Gulp!"

Julius gulped hard as he looked at her. ’It’s really hard to believe someone like her would be mine...’

Julius stood up from under the oak tree and walked toward her with a warm smile.

"Are you feeling a bit relieved now?"

"Mmm," Rosalina gave a small nod as she stopped in front of him.

She looked a little better after releasing her anger through the rampage, but her mood was still sour.

Julius stared at her for a moment. ’She doesn’t seem to be in a good mood yet,’ he thought.

"Want something to eat?" he asked gently.

"Mmm." She nodded simply, still not saying much.

"Alright," Julius smiled brightly. He reached out and grabbed her hand, ready to pull her along when...

"I-I can fly..." she managed to say.

Her already flushed cheeks turned an even deeper shade of red.

"Okay," Julius gave a short nod, trying not to laugh at how cute she looked.

He took off into the air, and Rosalina flew right beside him. Together, they headed toward the main city.

Meanwhile, back at the academy!

Crypian walked quietly along the corridor toward the Dean’s office. A glowing blue screen floated in front of him.

Status Window

Race: Human

Name: Crypian Thorne

Age:14

Stats:

Cultivation: Arcane Core Stage of Bodily Ascension. Early stage of Compression of Mental Ascension.

IQ: 25

Mana: 30% compressed mana

Skills:

- Regression [Details: Takes you back to the safest point depending on how much danger the user is in. No cooldown time.]

- Invincibility [Gives the user 1 minute of immortality. Nothing can kill the user during this time. Cooldown: 1 day.]

- Heroic Charm [Gives the hero the ability to charm others, especially those of the opposite sex.]

- Super Memorization [The user can memorize any infinite theorems with just a glance.]

- Theft [The user can steal any spell that has been used against him at least once. Cooldown: 7 days.]

Crypian stared at the screen as he walked.

"It’s been a year since I regressed, but my growth is still too slow compared to those monsters," he muttered.

He thought back to the Star Class.

Even in his previous life, Julius Augustus Hayes and Rosalina Valentina were famous.

They were still young when he died at age 20, but those two were already among the strongest in the three floating continents.

They were like the sky he could never reach.

He could only watch from below as they took all the glory, while he slaved away for his brother.

As he walked, people, especially the girls, kept staring at him with dreamy eyes.

Crypian didn’t pay much attention to it anymore.

He stopped in front of a door. Bold letters above it read: Dean Orin Belebright.

He paused for a moment, then raised his hand.

"Knock. Knock. Knock."

"Who?" a voice came from inside the room.

"I’m Crypian Thorne, sir. You asked me to meet you," he replied calmly.

"Oh, Crypian! Come in," the dean’s excited voice echoed.

Crypian stared at the door for a second and let out a quiet sigh. "Haa..."

’But this time, it will be different,’ he thought with determination.

’I will achieve my goals and reach the heavens with my own efforts!’
