Chapter 84 -: 83 The Harem would be for me![Bonus - ]

Time passed quickly.

The academy finished repairing all the damage from the monster attack and soon returned to its normal, lively routine.

It had been fifteen days since the incident.

Julius had fully recovered and started attending classes again.

Today, he was sitting in the Student Council President’s room, working as Rosalina’s secretary.

’That trash hero will arrive soon,’ he thought while happily biting into a cold, sweet ice cream.

In front of him, Rosalina was also scooping her own ice cream.

She looked as calm and elegant as always, but there was a new spark of energy in her eyes.

’Just a few more days...’ she thought, her heart beating faster.

’Once I find the perfect opportunity, I will confess.’

She tried hard to stay cold on the outside. ’It will be worth it when I propose and see the look of disbelief on his face.’

"So, Rose," Julius broke the silence.

He took another big bite of his ice cream and spoke while chewing.

"I was thinking... how about that date we talked about?"

He grinned. "Even though I lost the duel, you promised to go on a date with me to repay all my help. How about we go tomorrow?"

Rosalina frowned, pretending to be annoyed. "You didn’t forget about that?"

But inside, she was overjoyed. ’Haha, just wait a little longer. Once I confess, we will go on a date every single day!’

She grinned from ear to ear in her mind.

"How can I forget, Rose?" Julius said with a smile.

He took one last bite of his ice cream and stood up.

He was about to wipe his sticky hand on his uniform when Rosalina quickly held out her handkerchief.

"Here," she said softly.

Julius took the handkerchief, brought it close to his nose, and smelled it with a satisfied look.

’My Rose’s scent.’

Instead of wiping his hand, he neatly folded it and put it in his pocket.

Then he casually wiped his hand on his uniform.

"So, how about it?" he continued.

"We just need to pick a nice location. If you say the word, I’ll arrange everything."

Rosalina stood up and walked toward the door. She let out a small sigh.

"Now that I’ve already promised..." She turned her head slightly. Her cheeks turned a light shade of red. "I will keep the promise."

"Yes!" Julius punched the air in victory.

"Tomorrow at 9 in the morning!" he said excitedly as he followed her out of the room.

They walked out of the Student Council President’s room together.

The moment they stepped into the hallway, the atmosphere around them completely changed, but both Julius and Rosalina were too lost in their own thoughts to notice.

’What should I wear tomorrow?’ Rosalina wondered.

’How about that blue dress I wore on my birthday? No, it was a bit plain... Should I wear the red one instead?’

Meanwhile, Julius nodded to himself as he planned happily. ’Tomorrow, I’ll first take her to a nice restaurant... Hmm, yes...’

As they walked down the corridor, sudden whispers reached their ears.

Most of them came from excited girls.

"Did you see him? He was so handsome!" one girl said shyly while playing with her hair.

"Right? Some girls even fainted when they saw him!" another replied with sparkling eyes.

"I heard even the seniors are interested in him!"

These whispers floated around them. Julius frowned slightly.

’Did my avatar finally arrive at the academy?’ he thought.

From the way the girls were talking, it sounded like a very handsome guy had shown up.

He couldn’t help but think of his avatar, after all, he had made it extremely beautiful on purpose.

"I heard he was assigned to the Star Class..."

The moment Julius heard those words, his eyes widened a little. ’Could it be...?’

An image of the person he hated the most flashed in his mind.

He quickened his steps and walked hurriedly toward the Star Class.

All this hype, girls losing consciousness, seniors getting interested, it could only be for one person.

Julius’s eyes shone with excitement and annoyance.

Despite being the greatest genius, having a face that could attract any woman, and being the heir of one of the strongest ancient families in the three floating continents, he had never received this kind of attention.

No woman except Seraphina had ever shown real interest in him.

’And all this buildup could only be for one person...’

He reached out and gently grabbed Rosalina by the wrist, pulling her along as he walked faster.

Rosalina, who was still daydreaming about her dress, suddenly snapped back to reality.

"Hey..." Her eyes wavered and her face turned bright red like a tomato.

Julius dragged Rosalina along by the wrist.

For some reason, she felt very shy as she followed him.

She looked up at his face and noticed his expression had become complicated, a mix of excitement, hatred, anger, wickedness, and strong anticipation all rolled into one.

Rosalina stared at him with a small frown.

’What could be the reason...?’ she wondered as she was pulled along.

She glanced around the hallway.

Almost everyone, especially the girls, seemed to be in a daydream.

Their faces were flushed pink and their eyes looked distant.

’Weird...’ she thought.

’Why do these girls look like lovesick fools?’

She knew that expression very well, it was the same one she made whenever she thought about Julius.

This made her frown even harder.

’Are they eyeing my Julius after his brave sacrifice for the academy?’

But she quickly shook her head. No girl was even looking at him.

She kept wondering what was going on, though Julius already knew the answer.

’Look at these girls... straight out of those smut novels,’ he thought in disgust.

But his disgust wasn’t aimed at the girls, it was aimed at one person.

’The hero’s charm!’ It wasn’t any special ability a villain like him could have.

No matter how handsome Julius was, he didn’t care about that.

This "hero’s charm" was given to every protagonist in harem novels.

No matter how ordinary the guy looked, every girl, especially the exceptionally beautiful ones, would fall head over heels for him.

"Hmph!" Julius let out a cold snort.

’Even my avatar won’t be able to compete...’ he thought as the door to the Star Class finally came into view.

’But don’t worry, Mister Hero... this time will be a bit different,’ he muttered under his breath.

His eyes turned cold and sharp. ’You will gather your harem... but not for you!’

He reached the door. The words "Star Class" were written clearly above it.

’The harem would be for me...’

He pushed the door open hard. ’...to kill!’
