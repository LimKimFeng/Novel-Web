Students could be seen moving about in small groups, some chatting excitedly while others walked briskly with focused expressions.

"What time is it?" William asked casually as he tried to break the thick awkwardness that hung between them.

Seraphine startled slightly, then quickly flicked her spatial ring.

A green-colored pocket watch appeared in her palm with a soft shimmer.

She opened it and stared at the ticking hands for a second longer than necessary and then color drained from her face almost instantly.

"We are late," she said, her voice sounding urgent, her expression was clearly screaming, shit, I messed up.

"Then let’s go," William replied without hesitation.

"Wha....?" was all she managed to let out in those few seconds..

For a brief moment, some even thought another cult attack had begun.

The flash ended directly in front of a large classroom door with a signboard marked S1.

"Are you alright?"
