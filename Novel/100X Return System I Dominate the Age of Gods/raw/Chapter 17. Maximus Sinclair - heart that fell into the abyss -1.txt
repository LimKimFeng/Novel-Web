"Young master, catch me if you can," the girl teased while glancing back at the white-haired boy chasing her.

"Stop it, Lia, I’m tired already," the boy replied, deliberately slowing his pace and pretending to gasp for breath.

In truth, Maximus Sinclair’s stamina was far beyond that of an ordinary person his age.

For a noble like him, loving a commoner girl was considered taboo.

"Young master, stop teasing me!" Lia pouted, though she didn’t pull her hand away.

Seeing how passionately she praised the sweets, Maximus became curious. He peeked over the counter and noticed a blue-haired young man working diligently, scooping brown-colored desserts into small containers with practiced movements.

Maximus walked up to the counter confidently. "I would like to buy everything you have in this shop."

Maximus shook his head calmly. "Leave it to me, Lia."
