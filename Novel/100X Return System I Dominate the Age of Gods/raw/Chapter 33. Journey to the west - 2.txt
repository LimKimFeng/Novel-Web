Branches snapped violently, ancient trees shook, and frightened birds fled into the sky in chaotic swarms.

A deafening roar followed.

ROAAARRRR!!

The roar echoed through the dense forest somewhere deep within the Aris continent, carrying an aura of fear so thick that even hardened beasts froze mid-motion.

A pack of C-rank Silver Lions burst from between the trees, their massive silver-furred bodies moving at full speed, claws digging into the soil as they ran for their lives.

These beasts were apex predators in their territory, nothing feared them especially when they were together with their packs.

Yet now, terror filled their golden eyes.

Behind them, a monstrous metallic silhouette crushed everything in its path. Trees were uprooted, rocks shattered, and the forest floor caved inward as the massive vehicle advanced without slowing down.

A thunderous blast erupted.

**BOOM!**

An explosion swallowed half the clearing as compressed energy tore through the fleeing beasts.

Several Silver Lions were reduced to mangled corpses in an instant, blood and fur scattering through the air before falling back onto the forest floor.

Inside the vehicle, Will sat calmly, his posture was relaxed as he observed the scene through multiple monitors lining the interior walls while sipping into some juice with sunglasses on.
