Chapter 251

"This... what is going on here?"

The crowd was filled with horror, yet their throats were seized by the moonlight. They felt waves of suppression, unable to even catch their breath.

Meanwhile, Cedric, Tobias, Alistair, Malakai, and the others behind Alex felt a thick intent of karma and causality.

It was as if their own realms and comprehension, enveloped by the moonlight, received astonishing breakthroughs.

Tens of thousands of people in the venue stood entirely motionless, bathed in the moonlight. Time seemed to slow down, freezing at this very moment.

However, after about half a minute, the moment of termination finally arrived.

A beam of moonlight descended from the sky like a spear of lunar brilliance, piercing directly through the fierce gales and cloud mist, completely penetrating the Tiger Ancestor’s body.

After being pierced by the moonlight, the Tiger Ancestor’s miserable screams came to an abrupt halt. His eyes were filled with thick terror, his mouth opening and closing slightly, yet unable to let out the slightest sound.

The next second, his massive burly body dissolved into a pool of bloody water and vanished into ash and smoke. His peak Supreme Realm cultivation completely dissipated, evaporated by the cloud mist and blown away into nothingness by the gales.

The Tiger Ancestor was dead.

After annihilating the Tiger Ancestor, the gales instantly subsided and the cloud mist gradually scattered. The dark clouds in mid-air closed back up and slowly drifted apart. The moon vanished, reverting back to the evening glow of the setting sun.

Where the Tiger Ancestor had just been, only crystallized blood particles drifted in the air.

Everyone present felt a thick sense of terror and shock arise in their hearts.

"The Tiger Ancestor was actually..." Albert watched completely dumbfounded, his entire body trembling violently.

"Heavens, how could Alex possibly possess such terrifying power?!"

"Mother Empress, let’s run quickly!"

"Run?" Nova’s face was pale.

She looked at the tens of thousands of experts from the three great Saint Lineage families, then looked at Alex in mid-air, blazing like the sun at high noon.

She gave a tragic smile, shaking her head as if mocking herself. "Albert, do you think we can still run away?"

...

Compared to the others, the most panicked and terrified were naturally the Crane Ancestor and the Wolf Ancestor.

As half-step Saint mighty figures, the souls of the Three Heavenly Flame Ancestors had always been attached to the Profound Phoenix Stele, secretly guarding the Heavenly Flame Dynasty.

Possessing the absolute top-tier cultivation of the current era and tens of thousands of years of lifespan, they should have been immortal and indestructible before their lifespans were exhausted.

But today, the Tiger Ancestor, the strongest among the three, was easily wiped out by Alex right in front of everyone’s faces.

As fellow members of the Three Ancestors, the Crane Ancestor and the Wolf Ancestor clearly felt an oppressive sensation of impending doom.

"Crane Ancestor, did you see? The power of the Vault of Divine Three Firmaments is indeed as terrifying as the legends say." The Wolf Ancestor narrowed his eyes, speaking using sound transmission.

"If we wait for him to continue attacking, you and I will absolutely die as well."

"If we wish to survive, we can only go all out in a desperate struggle. You and I must join forces to annihilate Alex and destroy the three great artifacts. Only then can we stop this rebellion!"

"Good, let’s do it!" The Crane Ancestor nodded, and seething murderous intent permeated both their bodies.

The next second, the Wolf Ancestor’s eyes glared wide, taking the lead to attack.

"Myriad Wolf Rend!"

The Wolf Ancestor opened his bloody maw and spat out a thick black mist.

Taking himself as the origin, he polluted the surrounding dozen or so miles into something pitch-black as ink.

Accompanied by fierce wolf howls echoing one after another, from within the mist galloped thousands of pitch-black Demonic Fog Wolves with blood-red eyes. Stepping on the void, they sprinted wildly, viciously pouncing toward Alex.

This was the strongest divine ability of the Wolf Ancestor. Every single Demonic Fog Wolf was equivalent to a full-force strike from a peak Supreme Realm powerhouse, sufficient to destroy heaven and earth.

At the exact same moment, the Crane Ancestor also struck.

"Spirit Wing Annihilation!"

From his body drifted a dense white light, condensing in mid-air into a massive, purely white giant crane.

The giant crane spread its wings, casting a shadow that covered the sky and obscured the sun.

As its wings flapped, feather after feather condensed from golden light shot out. These feathers appeared light and floaty, unable to withstand the wind, but in reality every single feather was the Crane Ancestor’s ultimate killing move.

Tens of thousands of demonic wolves and tens of thousands of feathers interweaved as they blasted toward Alex.

The formidable coercive pressure made the very air between heaven and earth grow thin.

However, Alex and the Three Firmaments Supreme Beings stood with their arms crossed, remaining absolutely motionless.

"Saint Lord, quickly defend!" Cedric and the others revealed expressions of panic, shouting anxiously.

But the next second, their hoarse shouts were drowned out in an ear-shattering explosion.

The demonic wolves and feathers converged together, using Alex’s position as the intersection point

The instant they touched, a heaven-destroying, earth-shattering explosion was set off in mid-air.

The black of the demonic wolves and the white of the feathers fused into one, condensing into a half-black, half-white spherical orb in the shape of Yin-Yang.

Alex and the Three Firmaments Supreme Beings were all trapped within the sphere, fundamentally unable to break free. The intertwining of every demonic wolf and every white feather was a spiritual energy explosion sufficient to destroy a small town.

Tens of thousands of demonic wolves and tens of thousands of white feathers continuously surged into the sphere.

This meant that Alex, inside the sphere, had to endure the might of hundreds of explosions every single second.

The shockwaves splashed everywhere. Every drop of spiritual energy blasted a one-meter deep crater into the ground.

And if unfortunately touched by the spiritual energy, whether they were from the Heavenly Flame Imperial Family or the three great worldly clans, they would instantly evaporate and be annihilated without the slightest power to resist.
