Chapter 58

"What... what a powerful phenomenon!" One youth swallowed hard, speaking in absolute horror.

"Which sacred senior is breaking through their realm?"

"Could it be... the First Elder?"

"No." Lucas blankly shook his head.

"My father has been busy forging the Heavenly Clan Protection Formation recently. He simply hasn’t had the time to cultivate."

"Furthermore, even if he were breaking through to the ninth level of the Mahayana Realm, it’s impossible to trigger such a massive phenomenon."

"Then... could it be the Second Elder?"

"Impossible." Joanna shook her head.

"My father is currently responsible for gathering intelligence in the Heavenly Flame Dynasty."

"Moreover, he has been trapped at the fourth level of the Mahayana Realm for many years without the slightest bit of progress."

"With such a terrifyingly powerful aura, it cannot be him."

"Then... could it be the Third Elder?"

"Are you sick in the head?" Erik said dryly.

"My Master, his esteemed elder, is standing right over there."

"If he was breaking through right in front of your face, would you not be able to tell?"

"Then the Fourth Elder..."

"The Fourth Elder is busy refining pills every single day. Where would she find the time to cultivate?"

"Then... who could it be?!"

The crowd was abuzz with speculation. Entirely unsure of who exactly had drawn out this tyrannical, overbearing phenomenon.

A moment later, the pure white light of the pillar slowly broke apart and dissipated. It transformed into patches of glowing white clouds, floating high up in the firmament. An extra touch of poetic, picturesque beauty added to the already clear, azure sky.

A beautiful silhouette emerged from the farthest cloud.

Elegant, graceful posture. She stepped upon the clouds unhurriedly, walking toward them.

This woman was draped in a white robe, her entire being radiating an ethereal, immortal aura. Her black hair fluttered gently in the wind, fully displaying an otherworldly, transcendent beauty.

It was only as the woman drew closer that the Hale family disciples finally recognized her.

They stared blankly for a long time before someone muttered weakly.

"Isn’t... isn’t that Elara?"

"What?!"

The entire crowd of over a thousand people instantly fell into deathly silence.

In the blink of an eye, the woman’s figure approached closer. It was undeniably Elara, her graceful, flowing posture was mesmerizing. With every elegant step she took, a verdant green lotus bloomed beneath her feet.

Just like that, blooming green lotuses with every step. She slowly walked toward them.

"Holy shit, it really is Elara!"

"Was Elara... chosen by a descending Immortal?!"

"When I saw her yesterday, she was merely at the second level of the Body Tempering Realm!"

"This aura... what level of the Foundation Establishment Realm is this?"

"No, this is fucking Core Formation Realm!"

The crowd erupted into a wave of unbelievable, horrified gasps. They were even more shocked than when Julian had made his grand entrance.

Compared to Julian riding a glowing Mystic Owl and descending from the sky, Elara’s current method of appearance, stepping on blooming lotuses in the void, had a prestige factor that was immeasurably higher.

Among the screaming, exclaiming crowd, only two people had faces ashen with terror.

Oria and Qira.

"How... how could Elara’s cultivation possibly skyrocket so much overnight?!"

"As expected, the Saint Lord was scheming against us! He deliberately gave her special, intensive tutoring!"

"But... Elara broke through three major realms in just twenty hours."

"Even the Saint Lord doesn’t have such heaven-defying, omnipotent abilities, right?!"

In a flash, Elara traversed dozens of floating clouds and arrived before them. Stepping upon the green lotuses, she slowly descended from mid-air to the ground.

She walked up to Alex, knelt on both knees, and said softly, "Elara greets the Saint Lord."

"Elara, rise quickly." Alex waved his hand, chuckling warmly.

"It seems your harvest over these past twenty hours has not been small."

"Come, tell everyone. What are the results of your breakthrough?"

"As you command." Elara turned to face the crowd and smiled faintly.

"Elder brothers and sisters."

"Elara was blessed with the grace of the Saint Lord’s guidance and cultivated in seclusion last night."

"I finally managed to not disappoint the Saint Lord’s high expectations. I have broken through from the second level of the Body Tempering Realm to the second level of the Core Formation Realm."

Hiss...

The crowd couldn’t help but suck in a sharp breath of cold air.

Core Formation Realm. The Core Formation Realm! How exactly did this little girl manage to do it?!

Once a cultivator’s cultivation reached the Core Formation Realm, they could transform their Dantian into a Core, drawing upon the tribulations of heaven and earth to drive origin energy. One had officially stepped into the ranks of true powerhouses.

Lucas, Joanna, and the others all wore extremely complicated expressions.

They had worked themselves to the bone, sweating blood for an entire month, just to finally scrape their way into the Foundation Establishment Realm.

Yet Elara only took a single night to transform from a Body Tempering Realm mob into a Core Formation powerhouse.

Comparing people to people will only make you want to die.

Comparing goods to goods will only make you want to throw them away.

This level of heaven-defying aptitude, they couldn’t even feel envious. It was simply beyond reach.

When Elara first appeared, Julian had been grinding his teeth, face full of bitter unwillingness.

He firmly believed this woman had snatched the fortuitous encounter that rightfully belonged to him. More importantly, she had snatched away his opportunity to welcome Isolde back.

But upon hearing that Elara had broken through from the Body Tempering Realm to the Core Formation Realm in a mere twenty hours, Julian’s tightly clenched fists slowly relaxed.

The resentment in his heart dissipated significantly. He let out a long sigh.

She already broke through to the Core Formation Realm.

What else is there to say?

A Core Formation Realm powerhouse could crush his measly Foundation Establishment Realm self to death with a single finger. He should just keep cultivating slowly and wait for future opportunities.

"Elara, at today’s inspection assembly, you are the undisputed champion!" Alex praised loudly.

"In just a single night, breaking through from the second level of the Body Tempering Realm to the second level of the Core Formation Realm."

"A full three major realms! It can truly be called unprecedented and unrepeatable!"

"I hope that all the disciples of my Hale family can be like you. Unfazed by favor or disgrace, accumulating strength silently to erupt with profound might!"
