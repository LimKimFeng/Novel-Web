Chapter 95

It seemed what Aurelia said earlier was indeed correct.

Over the past few days of interaction, Nyx had been completely obedient and incredibly gentle toward Knox.

Even if she occasionally revealed a flash of killing intent or wildness, it was merely to hunt down some random spiritual or demonic beasts. Acting more like she was just playing around.

However, at this very moment, standing within the territory of the Hale family’s holy land and sensing the aura of Alex’s demonic arts... Nyx instantly seemed to become a completely different person. Her entire aura abruptly changed.

Just this terrifying, ghastly appearance alone was enough to shatter someone’s heart in fear.

Alex, today is the day of your demise, you old shit!

However, just as Knox was gleefully calculating in his heart, fantasizing about the glorious scene of Nyx ripping open Alex’s chest... the very next second, his smile froze completely on his face.

Nyx’s figure charged into the distance. Before she even disappeared from his line of sight... he saw a middle-aged man clad in black robes, wielding a trident, emerge from the dark clouds in mid-air.

Oscar Hale.

Oscar stared at Nyx below. An excited, piercing light burst from his eyes.

"Finally ambushed you!"

"Eat my trident!"

He flickered directly, descending from the sky and fiercely thrusting his trident downward.

"Let’s see you do evil!"

"Let’s see you eat people!"

The razor-sharp blade showed no mercy. It viciously pierced straight through the back of Nyx’s neck and embedded deeply into the ground.

Nyx’s eyes were blood-red. She let out a hysterical, shrill, miserable scream.

She struggled with all her might, unleashing the terrifying power of the fifth level of the Supreme Realm. The shockwave directly terrified Oscar so badly that his entire body trembled. His hands and feet involuntarily went numb.

"Quick... someone come quick!"

"Coming!"

The moment Oscar’s furious shout fell, two clanking bronze chains flew out from the dense forest on both sides.

At the ends of the bronze chains were scorpion-tail-like spikes. They pierced directly into Nyx’s calves, penetrating straight through.

"Hmph. Struck by my Divine Scorpion Soul-Fixing Needles, and you still want to escape?"

Peter unhurriedly walked out from the shadows. The bronze chains wrapped tightly around his thick, burly arms. He laughed coldly.

"Obediently come back with me to claim the reward!"

Oscar’s expression changed. He gritted his teeth.

"Peter, why are you here? Where are the people I stationed?!"

"Hmph. So you’re the only one allowed to set up an ambush here, and I’m not?" Peter sneered.

"As for the people you stationed... I knocked them all out and let them have a good, long sleep."

"You!"

Oscar’s eyes nearly split open in rage. His molars grinding together loudly.

He originally thought he could capture this monster all by himself and claim the entire reward. Who would have expected Peter Hale, this jinx, to crash the party halfway?

Now, at least half the credit would be snatched away by this fat pig.

Thirty Heaven and Earth-tier cultivation methods. Three hundred Heaven and Earth-tier spiritual herbs. Three hundred thousand high-grade spirit stones.

Cut directly in half.

His heart ached.

...

While Oscar was secretly agonizing, and Peter was feeling immensely smug... a sudden, ferocious force jerked the bronze chains, yanking Peter completely off guard and sending him crashing heavily to the ground.

"What?!"

The expressions of both men changed. They looked closely.

Nyx was using her jade-like hands, equipped with razor-sharp nails, to prop herself up against the ground. Trembling violently all over, she forcefully stood up from the dirt.

Her eyes were crimson as blood. She continued to let out hoarse, continuous roars.

"Purge the demons!"

"Purge the demons!"

Peter said in sheer terror.

"Is... is this thing really a monster?!"

"My Divine Scorpion Soul-Fixing Needles are coated with tranquilizing and hypnotic power. Even a Mahayana Realm Demonic Elephant would be instantly knocked out!"

"This fifth level Supreme female monster... isn’t she a bit too insanely overpowered?!"

Oscar was also shocked and terrified. He gripped his trident tightly with both hands, desperately wanting to pin Nyx back into the ground. But competing in pure brute strength, he was fundamentally no match for her.

Watching Nyx slowly break free inch by inch, about to snap their restraints...

From the woods behind them, a resonant, hearty laugh suddenly echoed.

"Hahahaha, two virtuous younger brothers, do not panic! This old man has arrived!"

Peter and Oscar turned their heads. None other than Martin Hale, riding a mottled white little donkey, clop-clopping out from the depths of the forest.

Holding a massive gourd in his hands, Martin shouted loudly.

"Immortal-Binding Date Pit Nails!"

Tiny black dots flew out of the gourd. They looked exactly like date pits. In reality, the top of each tiny black dot flashed with a chilling, razor-sharp gleam.

Over a hundred Date Pit Nails accurately pierced into every single meridian and acupoint all over Nyx’s body, forming numerous small black dots. These dots connected in pairs, forming thin black lines that crisscrossed and overlapped.

In almost a single instant, hundreds of black lines ensnared Nyx. She obediently collapsed back onto the ground. Unable to move a single inch.

...

Oscar let out a long breath of relief. He stared at Martin with an unfriendly expression.

"Brother Martin, it is so late, yet you are not sleeping. What are you doing here?"

"Heheh, haven’t you two virtuous brothers also stayed awake?"

Martin stood with his hands behind his back. Grinning cheekily.

"In order to capture this female demon, this old man even used his own ultimate treasure, the Immortal-Binding Date Pit Nails."

"Unless I will it, absolutely no one can pull these nails out."

"When the Saint Lord sees them, he will definitely recognize them!"

Seeing Martin Hale’s utterly confident appearance, Oscar and Peter exchanged a glance. They could only smile helplessly.

This old fox was truly cunning.

He didn’t act at first. But the moment he did, he snatched the absolute largest piece of the cake.

However, if Martin hadn’t intervened in time, this monster might have truly escaped.

Therefore, although Oscar and Peter were displeased in their hearts, they could only obediently accept their fate.
