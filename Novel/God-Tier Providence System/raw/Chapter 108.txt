Chapter 108

But why did Alex still insist on giving it to her?

Because Alex would rather have no merits and no faults than make a mistake.

The vast majority of these family juniors were mere cannon fodder whose names weren’t even mentioned in the original novel. Right now, they all appeared incredibly loyal and obedient. But he couldn’t possibly guess what each of them was actually thinking deep down.

What if one of them harbored crooked thoughts, obtained this Seven Orifices Divine Heart, and became a second Isolde? Wouldn’t that be equivalent to cutting off his own flesh to feed a white-eyed wolf?

Those four branch family Saint Lords were also four treacherous backstabbers. Every single one of them harboring massive ambitions.

In the entire family, only the Four Great Elders were explicitly recorded as being absolutely loyal to him. All loyal retainers who died for his sake.

Therefore, Alex would rather give the Seven Orifices Divine Heart to Lara than hand it over to other untrustworthy juniors.

In Lara’s hands, perhaps the Seven Orifices Divine Heart couldn’t exert its maximum power.

But at the very least, it would definitely be used for his own faction.

That was enough.

"Oh right, aside from the Seven Orifices Divine Heart, you may also freely make one wish." Alex stood with his hands behind his back, smiling faintly.

"As long as it is within my power, I will definitely satisfy it."

"This..." Lara was somewhat overwhelmed by the favor. She lowered her head, pondering for a moment before saying earnestly, "Saint Lord, to be able to serve the Saint Lord is already this subordinate’s greatest fortune in this life."

"Aside from growing old in the Hale family and devoting my absolute loyalty to the Saint Lord, I have no other wishes."

"If the Saint Lord insists that this subordinate make a wish..."

"...Then this subordinate only wishes for the Saint Lord’s cultivation fortunes to be prosperous, for the Hale family’s descendants to be filled with dragons and phoenixes, and for our foundation to remain evergreen!"

Hearing these words, the crowd was instantly stunned.

Alex clapped his hands and said loudly, "Excellent!"

"The Fourth Elder’s words show unparalleled loyalty and righteousness!"

"Applause!"

At Alex’s command, the crowd instantly burst into thunderous applause. While applauding, they also felt exceptional admiration for Lara’s emotional intelligence.

Goodness, no wonder she can be the Fourth Elder. The good impression left in the Saint Lord’s heart from these few short sentences is worth far more than a single wish.

Among the crowd, only Julian was trembling with rage. Nearly grinding his molars to dust.

What a waste! A reckless waste of heavenly opportunity!

Last month, after Elara took the crown, she also said something about hoping the Saint Lord lives a long life, peaceful and free of illness. Completely wasting a wish for nothing.

This time, Lara got the chance to make a wish, and actually also spouted a bunch of useless nonsense.

If you guys don’t want to use your wishes, then give them to me! I’ll buy them! My poor little Isolde, still having to wander aimlessly outside without a fixed place to live...

...

"My junior disciples, you do not need to be too discouraged." Alex stood with his hands behind his back and smiled faintly.

"In this second Examination Assembly, your performances have been so outstanding. Everyone will receive a reward."

"Next month, we will still hold the third Examination Assembly."

"What I can tell you is that the rewards for the third Examination will be even more generous than this second one."

"Furthermore, for the person who claims the crown at the Examination Assembly, I will still unconditionally satisfy one wish for them."

Hearing these words, a gleam of hope once again surfaced in Julian’s despairing eyes. He secretly gritted his teeth, clenched his fists, and made a firm resolution in his heart.

Isolde, wait for me!

Wait for me just one more month!

In next month’s Examination Assembly, I will absolutely snatch the champion’s position and bring you back to the family!

Immediately, Alex distributed rewards to the rest of the juniors respectively. He spent his painstakingly saved Providence Points in the Providence Mall to redeem the immortal pills, spiritual artifacts, and medicinal herbs that would be most beneficial to their cultivation.

The juniors were naturally deeply grateful. Each of them voicing their excited thanks.

...

Just as Alex was collectively pouring motivational chicken soup into the crowd, inspiring them to continue cultivating diligently...

A cold wind swept past. Adrian rushed over from afar, saying excitedly, "Saint Lord, I have acquired some massive news!"

"Oh?" Alex narrowed his eyes and asked deeply.

"What news? Speak."

Adrian forcefully suppressed his excitement and said loudly, "Last night, above the Azure Dragon Valley, the forbidden area of the Divine Blade Sect, the wind and clouds suddenly changed colors, the stars shifted, and a phenomenon of Ten Thousand Swords Returning to their Ancestor occurred!"

"After seeing this phenomenon, the Ten Elders of the Divine Blade Sect emerged one after another."

"They snapped swords to form formations in their respective cave dwellings and summoned all disciples. Sealing the mountain and forbidding anyone from leaving."

"Right now, various factions are spreading rumors, claiming that the Divine Blade Sect’s three-thousand-year-old legend has already been fulfilled."

"According to the legend, the Vault of Divine Blade Heart left behind by the founding patriarch of the Divine Blade Sect is about to appear in the world!"

"What?!" Hearing these words, Alex, Tobias, and Lara were all shocked.

The three-thousand-year-old legend of the Divine Blade Sect could be considered one of the most famous anecdotes in the entire Heavenly Flame Dynasty.

Legend had it that the founding patriarch of the Divine Blade Sect, Master Solarius, was one of the earliest experts on the Azure Origin Continent to step into the Saint Realm.

Before ascending to the Immortal Realm, Master Solarius discarded his mortal body and heart, leaving only his soul to ascend and join the ranks of the immortals.

His body was torn apart by fierce winds. Transforming into a rain of blood that fell from the sky.

Wherever the raindrops fell, dragon scales sprouted all over the ground, forming what later became known as the Azure Dragon Valley.

And Master Solarius’s heart transformed into the Vault of Divine Blade Heart, hiding deep within the clouds.

Legend foretold that three thousand years later, when the stars overlapped and ten thousand swords returned to their ancestor, the Vault of Divine Blade Heart would appear in the world.
