Chapter 114

Furthermore, there were two people staring right at his back. He absolutely couldn’t blow his cover the moment he entered the sect gates.

However, as Alex walked, he constantly felt bursts of cold, piercing killing intent from behind him.

Aya, following about fifty meters back, was constantly glaring at him with murderous aura. Not looking away for a single second.

This girl and I have never met before. Why does she harbor such massive hostility? Alex pondered secretly.

The aristocratic disciples of the Hundred Thousand Great Mountains are truly incredibly bizarre.

The ins and outs of this situation were unknown even to the original Taryn Lark. Therefore, Alex, having only read Taryn’s memories, naturally didn’t know either.

The Cale and Lark families were blood enemies for generations. However, their foundations and power were evenly matched. After years of both open strife and veiled struggles, neither could wipe the other out.

Aya’s grandfather, the former head of the Cale family, had been ambushed by a Lark family expert and died from his severe injuries.

Just now, upon seeing the word Lark in the Mountain-Visiting Token, Aya and the old woman concluded that although this youth looked gentle, elegant, handsome, and fair, he was definitely the favored genius of the Lark family’s younger generation.

The head’s only son, Taryn Lark.

They had long heard that this person was frivolous and domineering. An absolute playboy young master.

Aya originally wanted to draw her blade right then and there to avenge her grandfather. But she was stopped by the old woman.

The Divine Blade Sect had numerous rules, and its strict constraints on disciples were universally known.

Anyone holding a Mountain-Visiting Token was already considered an Outer Sect disciple. They had to strictly abide by sect rules, cast aside personal grudges, and above all, avoid turning weapons against fellow disciples.

After weighing the pros and cons, Aya temporarily suppressed her hatred and forcefully swallowed her fury.

Once she cultivated diligently, established many merits, and gained a firm foothold in the Divine Blade Sect, then she would find a way to take her revenge and make the Lark family pay the price.

...

Just as Aya was secretly holding back her vengeful fury, a smiling male voice came from behind her.

"You must be the Eldest Miss Aya, right? You truly are as beautiful as the rumors say!"

She turned to look. It was the blue-robed youth approaching her.

"I am the eldest son of the Thorne family, Lirin Thorne. Paying my respects!"

"Is there something you need?" Aya asked icily. Right now, her mind was entirely focused on revenge. She had zero interest in other trivial matters.

"Hehe, I have long admired Miss Aya’s great name. I never expected to have the opportunity to become fellow sect disciples!" Lirin smiled obsequiously and took a porcelain bottle out of his storage bag.

"This bottle of Grade One Qi-Gathering Pills can serve as a meeting gift. Please accept it, Junior Sister Aya!"

"I hope that after entering the Divine Blade Sect, we can become good friends who share honor and disgrace, crossing the same boat together!"

"Senior Brother Lirin, you truly are generous." However, Aya did not reach out to take it. She sneered.

"As fellow members of the Five Great Families of the Mountains, does my Cale family lack a mere bottle of Qi-Gathering Pills from your Thorne family?"

"Uh..." Failing to curry favor and getting rebuffed instead, Lirin instantly felt he couldn’t maintain his face. He swallowed his embarrassment and laughed awkwardly. "Junior Sister Aya, what are you saying?"

"Our Five Great Families share the same breath and branches. We naturally should help and support each other."

"Besides, this bottle of Qi-Gathering Pills is a gift I am giving to Junior Sister Aya in my personal capacity. It has nothing to do with our two families."

"Young Master Lirin need not be so polite." Aya continued walking forward without changing her expression.

"Isn’t it a bit too early to be addressing each other as Senior Brother and Junior Sister?"

"You and I are currently just Outer Sect candidates. It hasn’t even been confirmed if we can enter the sect."

"It won’t be too late to try and get close after the dust settles."

With that, Aya strode forward swiftly. Her expression unchanging.

Lirin’s face burned with humiliation, he gritted his teeth in anger.

He had deliberated for a long time before finally hardening his heart to take out a bottle of Qi-Gathering Pills to curry favor. These were good stuff he usually couldn’t even bear to eat himself.

But who could have expected that his flattery would end up hitting himself?

Not only did he fail to give away the pills, but he was thoroughly ridiculed by Aya. Losing all his face.

Seeing Aya hurrying in Alex’s direction, Lirin felt unwilling to give up and called out again. "Miss Aya, please wait a moment!"

"Is there something else?" Aya asked impatiently.

"Miss Aya, look." Lirin narrowed his eyes and lowered his voice.

"If I guess correctly, that guy over there is the eldest son of the Lark family, Taryn Lark!"

"The Lark family and your Cale family have been blood enemies for generations."

"This Taryn is an even more unforgivably wicked playboy who frequently plays with the feelings of young ladies from other aristocratic families."

"Isn’t it a disgrace for you and me to enter the Divine Blade Sect and associate with such a person?"

Aya frowned. "Then what do you mean?"

"Once we get up the mountain, you and I should join forces to ostracize him and drive him back down!" Lirin whispered.

"I heard that the Lark family recently suffered a massive disaster, and the entire clan relocated to the White Feather Dynasty to avoid trouble."

"Only this Taryn was left behind to guard their old nest. That’s why he came to the Divine Blade Sect, seeking their protection."

"After we get up the mountain, during the sect entry and master acknowledgement ceremony, the two of us will launch an attack together and force the elders to kick him down the mountain."

"Right now, without the Lark family behind him, it is absolutely impossible for the elders to offend our two major families just for his sake!"

Seeing Lirin’s confident smile, acting as if he already had a well thought out plan, Aya’s face sank.
