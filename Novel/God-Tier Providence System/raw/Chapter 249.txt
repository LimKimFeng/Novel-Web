Chapter 249

"If that Albert truly were Aurelia’s biological younger brother, I naturally would have nothing to say."

"But what if he is a wild bastard?"

"In order to prevent the imperial bloodline of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty from being tainted, what is wrong with me killing him?"

This single sentence was like a massive boulder thrown into a calm sea. It instantly set off a massive uproar across the entire venue.

"A bastard?"

"In front of the Three Ancestors, Alex actually dares to speak like this?"

"Why is he so absolutely confident? Could it be..."

Nova could no longer remain calm. As if her tail had been stepped on, she shrieked, "Alex, you’re spouting bullshit!"

"Albert is my biological son. Everyone high and low in the entire Heavenly Flame Dynasty knows this!"

"You wicked traitor, facing imminent death, you still dare to slander my Heavenly Flame imperial heir!"

"I beg the Three Ancestors to strike at once and execute this nonsense-spouting thief!"

Alex smiled faintly and calmly, while Nova was completely incoherent. Who was panicked and who was composed was clear at a glance.

"This is a capital offense punishable by the extermination of three generations. You wicked woman will definitely not admit it."

"But it doesn’t matter. I have long prepared for you."

Alex waved his hand and said indifferently. "Bring it!"

Two Hale family disciples immediately stepped forward, holding a golden basin filled with half a basin of clear, pure water.

Alex then faced mid-air, looked toward the heavens, and shouted loudly. "Theron, over the years you showered Aurelia with every kind of favor and treated her as your own. As a man, I admire you very much."

"Now, do you dare, in front of the entire Divine Dynasty, to perform a blood test to recognize family relations right here?"

"Let’s see if this Albert is truly your descendant, or a bastard of questionable origin!"

This speech by Alex was not only a brilliant counterattack. It also angered Nova to the point her face turned ashen, frozen in place at a loss for what to do.

Alistair and the others to the side forcefully suppressed their smiles, while in their hearts they were laughing so wildly they almost fainted.

Well said! No wonder when he had previously seen through this secret and told Alex about it, Alex acted like it didn’t matter.

It turned out he had long planned to save it for today, using it as a sharp arrow that completely destroys their character and murders their hearts.

Whether that late Emperor Theron was dead or alive today, from now on he would eternally bear the joke of being a cuckold. Thinking of this, Alistair suddenly felt he had vented his foul anger.

"Enough!" The Crane Ancestor couldn’t stand watching any longer, abruptly waving his hand. He directly shattered the golden basin into fine powder across the air, sending the clear water splashing everywhere.

Looking at Nova’s reaction just now, it proved that Alex’s words were likely 90 percent true.

For the Emberhal family to produce such a massive scandal could be called the greatest humiliation since the establishment of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty.

They absolutely could not let this farce continue any further.

Otherwise, if Alex shook out even more secrets one after another, the entire Emberhal family, along with their three great ancestors, would be forever nailed to the pillar of shame.

"Insolent brat, I want you dead!" The Tiger Ancestor had the most fiery temper. His sword-like eyebrows shot up, his beard and hair standing on end.

In an instant, he teleported from mid-air to right in front of Alex, blasting out a palm brimming with murderous aura.

A palm strike from a peak Supreme Realm powerhouse, with power piercing through bluestone, enough to split mountains and crack the earth.

However, Alex just stood there with his arms crossed expressionlessly, as if he fundamentally disdained to even block it.

Originally, everyone thought Alex had given up resisting and would undoubtedly die. But the next second, they all widened their eyes in disbelief.

Three streams of wind surged out of thin air, carrying a chilling aura capable of annihilating all things.

Alex’s expression remained unchanged, completely unharmed.

Yet the Tiger Ancestor was blasted flying nearly a hundred meters away, a streak of scarlet blood spilling from the corner of his mouth before he instantly fainted dead away.

"This, how is this possible?!" Nova widened her eyes in disbelief, thick terror filling her gaze.

"How could he possess strength even stronger than the Ancestor?!"

The next second, winds gathered and cyclones flowed in reverse. The spiritual energy in the air became so dense it actually materialized. Three sword-wielding giants, their entire bodies composed of golden light, appeared behind Alex.

Each of these three giants was thirty meters tall, clad in golden armor and wielding giant swords, their bodies permeated with a chillingly strict sword intent.

Moreover, the cultivation of these three great giants had all reached the peak of the Supreme Realm, half-step Saints, not the slightest bit weaker than the Three Great Ancestors of the Heavenly Flame.

Nova and Albert’s faces were full of panic.

They couldn’t help but retreat continuously, not daring to leave the Ancestors’ sides for even a single step.

If even half a foot stepped outside the Ancestors’ range of protection, they would likely be directly torn into fine powder by the sword intent.

Feeling the coercive pressure radiating from the three giants, the surrounding Heavenly Flame experts all felt waves of suffocation, finding it incomparably difficult to even breathe.

Meanwhile, Alistair, Malakai, and the others excitedly clenched their fists.

"My heavens, I didn’t expect the Hale family to actually have the existence of such experts!"

"Not only did Brother Alex himself break through to the peak of the Supreme Realm, but he also found three peak Supreme Realm helpers with such terrifying strength."

"No wonder he actually had such courage, daring to crash into the Heavenly Flame Capital to raid the execution ground relying merely on the strength of three worldly clans."

The Blackwood family’s Great Elder, Lazlo, and the Sol family’s Great Elder, Byron Sol, looked at the Hale family’s Great Elder, Cedric, complaining reproachfully. "Cedric, you’re not acting like a brother."

"Your Hale Family Saint Lord cultivated three experts with such formidable strength, yet you didn’t tell us the slightest bit about it?"
