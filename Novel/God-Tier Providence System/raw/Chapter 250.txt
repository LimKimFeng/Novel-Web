Chapter 250

"Exactly, our three families are allies right now. Is it interesting to hide and tuck things away?"

"You wouldn’t be intentionally concealing this, wanting to use these three powerhouses to deal with us later, right?"

Facing the questioning of Lazlo and Byron Sol, Cedric couldn’t help but look like he wanted to cry but had no tears.

Two brothers, not only were you kept in the dark, but we were also completely ignorant! Heaven knows when and from where the Saint Lord found three unfamiliar experts of unknown origin.

Moreover, the strength of all three was formidable to such an extent, yet he, the First Elder, knew absolutely nothing about this.

Wasn’t the Saint Lord being a bit too dismissive of him?

Tobias and Lara to the side both revealed looks of joyful surprise. Counting the Saint Lord, their Hale family now possessed four peak Supreme Realm powerhouses.

Not to mention having absolutely no rivals among the Saint Lineage families, even looking across the entire Heavenly Flame Dynasty, they were second-to-none outstanding figures.

Lily, Selene, and the trusted aides loyal to Aurelia all jumped for joy.

Originally, when the Three Great Ancestors appeared just now, despair had already filled their hearts.

As high-ranking officials of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty, they were naturally crystal clear on just how fierce the strength of the Dragon, Tiger, and Crane Ancestors was.

Relying merely on Alex and the three great Saint Lineages was fundamentally insufficient to contend against them.

But they didn’t expect Alex to actually have a back-up plan, preparing these three mysterious powerhouses as reinforcements.

It seemed today, Her Majesty the Empress was saved.

Everyone was in shock, secretly guessing exactly where Alex had found three such terrifying experts.

A moment later, the Crane Ancestor, who was the most knowledgeable of ancient and modern times, was the first to see the clues.

"No, that’s not right. These three guys are not humans!"

At this very moment, Alex stood with his arms crossed, towering in mid-air.

The Vault of Divine Mirror floated above his head, radiating a dazzling golden light.

The Vault of Divine Magatama floated beside him, permeating a boundless sword intent.

And the long Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword stood vertically before him.

From the blade of the sword, three slender golden threads floated out, connecting to the chests of the three great giants behind him.

"The Vault of Divine Mirror, the Vault of Divine Magatama, and the Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword..." The Crane Ancestor was muttering to himself when he suddenly realized something, his eyes revealing a look of thick panic.

"I understand! Legend has it that the Divine Blade Saint, the Evergreat Sword Ancestor, wielded the three great divine artifacts of the Vault of Divine Holy Mansion. He was the absolute pinnacle of his era, the supreme entity of the Sword Dao."

"And when the power of the three great divine artifacts merged into one, he could summon the Vault of Divine Three Firmaments Supreme Beings for his own use!"

"These three guys are the legendary Vault of Divine Three Firmaments Supreme Beings capable of annihilating worldly demons and heretics: Wind, Cloud, and Moon!"

"What?!" The expressions of the Tiger Ancestor and Wolf Ancestor both abruptly changed.

"This, how is this possible?!" The Tiger Ancestor said in horror.

"The so-called Vault of Divine Three Firmaments is nothing but a legend! They have never truly descended upon the mortal realm!"

"Even if the Vault of Divine Three Firmaments truly exist, they should be controlled by the Evergreat Sword Ancestor. How could they possibly be summoned by the Patriarch of a later-generation Saint Lineage family?!"

Before the Tiger Ancestor could question further, a chilling smile curled at the corners of Alex’s mouth.

"Since the Tiger Ancestor does not believe it, then I will let you experience the power of the Vault of Divine Three Firmaments."

"Wind Stills!"

Alex casually waved his hand, pointing his index and middle fingers to the left.

The Firmament of Wind Supreme Being instantly took action, fiercely swinging down the sword in its hand.

After this sword strike was swung, heaven and earth fell into a deathly silence.

The next second, a fierce chilling wind howled into existence, causing the colors of heaven and earth to change, sending sand flying and rocks rolling.

Ten thousand streams of fierce gales enveloped the Tiger Ancestor’s body within them. Stream after stream of wind currents, as sharp as daggers, left bloody wound after bloody wound upon his body.

The Tiger Ancestor clenched his jaw tightly, using all his strength trying to break free, yet he was fundamentally unable to escape the cage formed by the gales.

"Cloud Rises!" Alex pressed his hands together and let out another thunderous shout.

The Firmament of Cloud Supreme Being similarly pressed its palms together, the sword in its palm pointing straight toward the clouds.

Dark clouds surged above, condensing into two streams of cloud mist that crashed down with a boom, striking directly from front and back, blasting the Tiger Ancestor’s chest and back, causing the tiger-faced battle armor on his body to violently explode.

The Tiger Ancestor let out a miserable, shrill scream that shook heaven and earth, echoing heavily.

Seeing this scene, the Crane Ancestor and Wolf Ancestor were instantly shocked to the core.

However, the power of wind and cloud, approaching the Saint level, unleashed a fierce indiscriminate bombardment upon the Tiger Ancestor.

Just the shockwaves rippling out alone made them unable to approach even half a step. They could only watch helplessly from the sidelines.

"Moon Silences!" Alex narrowed his eyes slightly, finally pointing to the Firmament of Moon Supreme Being on the right.

The Firmament of Moon Supreme Being drew its sword, swinging the blade in slow, sluggish motions, as if dancing gracefully.

Under its dance steps, the dense dark clouds in mid-air actually slowly dissipated.

But what was astonishing was that just moments ago, when the dark clouds covered the sky, it was still the afterglow of the setting sun.

Now that the clouds parted and the mist scattered, the evening glow on the horizon had vanished without a trace, completely turning into a profound blue veil of night.

And right in the center of the night sky hung a bright, white crescent moon, radiating a brilliant white light.

Bathed in the moonlight, everyone present actually felt a chilling sensation, as if a sharp sword was hovering right above their heads!
