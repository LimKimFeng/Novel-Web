Chapter 252

Under this terrifying bombardment that ignored the difference between friend and foe, the entire venue fell into complete chaos.

Even if 99% of the power of the Crane and Wolf Ancestors’ combined attack was shouldered by Alex, just the remaining 1% alone caused hundreds of innocent people to lose their lives.

Sensing that Alex’s life force within the sphere was gradually growing faint, his aura masked by the smell of scorched earth, two streaks of clear tears flowed down Lily’s eyes. She collapsed to her knees with a thud.

"It’s over... it’s all over..."

"Princess Lily, what did you say?" Tobias, standing to the side, shouted coldly.

"The Saint Lord might not necessarily be unable to resist an attack of this level!"

"Exactly!" Lara also said indignantly.

"The Saint Lord is someone who triumphs over heaven. How could he possibly die here? He just easily annihilated the Heavenly Flame’s Tiger Ancestor with a flick of his finger moments ago. Facing the attacks of this Crane Ancestor and Wolf Ancestor, he definitely has a way to resolve them as well!"

"Princess Lily, why must you boost the morale of others and extinguish our own prestige?"

"No, it’s impossible..." Yet Lily still shook her head, her eyes rimmed red as she said hoarsely.

"Two Elders, please stop deceiving yourselves."

"The terrifying might of the Crane and Wolf Ancestors’ combined attack is enough to destroy the entire Heavenly Flame Dynasty. An attack of this magnitude, even a Saint Realm mighty figure would be unable to resist or parry it."

"No matter how strong Saint Lord Alex is, it’s absolutely impossible for him to withstand such a terrifying attack alone and remain completely unscathed."

"We’re finished..."

Lily’s words were like a sharp sword piercing into everyone’s hearts.

Among them, Cedric, Lara, and the rest of the Hale family were naturally worried sick for Alex. While Alistair, Malakai, and the others were more worried about themselves and their own clansmen.

Today’s matter had escalated to this point.

It was already spilt water that could not be gathered back. Even one of the Three Great Ancestors of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty had already died, which meant it was absolutely impossible for this to end hastily.

Right now, the only one who could contend with the Three Heavenly Flame Ancestors was Alex.

If Alex died, then this vigorous and massive royal rescue would be over. They who participated, as well as their clansmen, would definitely be slaughtered to the last person.

A moment later, the tens of thousands of demonic wolves and feathers had all poured out completely.

After the spiritual energy explosions finished, heaven and earth fell into a desolate and solemn silence.

The dark clouds above had long been blasted away, leaving a dazzling red sun hanging in mid-air. Beneath the red sun was precisely that lifeless Taiji spherical orb.

"Did it succeed?" The Crane Ancestor wiped the cold sweat from his forehead, muttering to himself, his voice trembling slightly from excitement.

The Wolf Ancestor revealed a look of wild ecstasy, throwing his head back and laughing loudly. "Hahahaha, the so-called Vault of Divine Three Firmaments are nothing more than this!"

"No matter how arrogant you are, Alex, you are nothing but a tiny ant of later generations! Even if you obtained the Three Treasures of the Vault of Divine, so what? Complete annihilation of body and spirit, your soul flying away and scattering, that is your end!"

"And you all, you rebellious and chaotic thieves, today you will all undoubtedly die!"

While looking down disdainfully at the crowd from the three great worldly families, the Wolf Ancestor teleported to the front of the Yin-Yang spherical orb. He intended to inspect whether those three ultimate treasures of the Vault of Divine had been destroyed in the explosion.

Seeing this scene, the crowd of the three great worldly families all revealed mournful expressions.

Even Lara and the others couldn’t hold back their sorrow and despair, shedding tears. Some felt insurmountable sorrow for Alex’s heroic sacrifice. Others wept because they knew the butcher’s knife of death was about to descend upon their own heads.

However, just as the people of the three great worldly families were enveloped in thick despair, a resonant loud laugh suddenly echoed from mid-air.

"Hahahahaha!"

Hearing this vigorous and powerful laugh, sounding like a great yellow bell, Tobias and the others all revealed expressions of excitement and wild ecstasy.

They couldn’t be more familiar with this laugh, full of absolute confidence and a domineering aura that looked down upon all living beings.

The Wolf Ancestor’s pupils shrank abruptly, his face revealing a look of terror. "This... how is this possible..."

The next second, the Yin-Yang spherical orb shattered open a gap with a loud bang. A massive hand condensed from golden light abruptly reached out from the fissure. Directly, like pinching a mouse, it tightly squeezed the Wolf Ancestor’s massive body in the palm of its hand.

"This... how is this possi..." The Wolf Ancestor’s face was full of horror and panic. He used all his strength trying to struggle, yet couldn’t break free in the slightest.

The next second, the massive hand showed no mercy, closing its grip with majestic force.

The Wolf Ancestor’s body was directly crushed into fine powder. Blood-red dust flowed down through the gaps between the giant fingers, while his peak Supreme Realm cultivation transformed into golden light and drifted toward the horizon.

The Wolf Ancestor was dead.

Originally, Albert and the others all thought the Ancestors had destroyed Alex, every single one ecstatic with joy. This sudden, massive twist made everyone fall silent once again.

The next second, cracks spread all over the Yin-Yang spherical orb, and then it completely shattered with a bang.

A golden-light giant, a thousand meters tall, stood towering between the vault of heaven and the earth.

The golden-light giant was clad in long robes, its expression majestic, with three light swords suspended behind its back. The power radiating from its body was shockingly Saint Realm divine might.

A casual glance could slaughter an entire city.

The golden-light giant’s feet almost completely filled the entire Profound Phoenix Square, causing the crowd of the three great worldly clans to continuously retreat to prevent being accidentally trampled and injured.

The Three Firmaments Supreme Beings from just now were only 100 meters tall, yet they had already occupied half the Vault of Divine, full of oppressive pressure.
