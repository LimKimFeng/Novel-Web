Chapter 106

"Excellent!" Alex stood with his hands behind his back, nodding with a satisfied smile.

"Tobias, I truly never expected that you could achieve such results. It truly brings me boundless gratification."

"To be able to break through five minor realms in the Mahayana Realm in just a short month... looking across the entire annals of the Hale family’s history, such a miracle has never occurred."

"It seems the champion of this second Examination is absolutely none other than you!"

Receiving Alex’s unsparing praise, Tobias couldn’t help but beam with joy.

"Many thanks to the Saint Lord!"

"If not for the Saint Lord’s attentive encouragement and painstaking cultivation, it would have been absolutely impossible for this poor Daoist to achieve such proud results!"

"For this poor Daoist to break through so smoothly, all the credit should belong entirely to the Saint Lord..."

Just as Tobias was acting completely smug after getting an advantage, having already begun delivering his award acceptance speech...

An earth-shattering, heaven-shaking boom echoed from behind them.

Following that, bursts of crimson radiant clouds burst forth. Bathing everyone within them.

"This... whose power is this?" The crowd looked at each other in dismay, then involuntarily raised their heads to look closely.

At this very moment, it was clearly the early hours of the morning. Just a second ago, the recently risen sun was still hanging in the eastern sky.

The heavens blue and cloudless for ten thousand miles.

Yet at this exact moment, the boundless clear sky of sunrise had directly transformed into the twilight of sunset.

Heaven and earth were bathed in a dim yellow glow.

A deep crimson red plummeted to the ground in the west.

Every single ray of crimson light that cast down exuded a majestic, kingly aura that commanded awe without anger.

"The colors of heaven and earth change, day and night are inverted..." Even Tobias, who had just broken through to the peak of Mahayana, couldn’t help but experience a slight change in expression. His entire body trembling uncontrollably.

"A Supreme Realm mighty powerhouse has been born?!"

Under the illumination of this sunset-like rosy light, all the young disciples of the Hale family were intimidated into wooden figures. Unable to move a single inch.

Those with stronger cultivation bases, like Julian and Lucas, only felt waves of coercive pressure.

But the majority of the disciples at Qi Gathering or those who had just entered Foundation Establishment were so shocked that fine beads of cold sweat broke out on their foreheads. Drawing a single breath became incredibly difficult.

Under everyone’s disbelieving gazes, a sphere of light was cast down from the setting sun at the horizon.

The light sphere descended from the sky. Slowly merging into the ground right in front of the crowd.

The next second, the hard bluestone ground outside the Saint Lord’s Hall shattered. Cracking open into hideous, jagged fissures.

A crimson, tender sprout slowly emerged from the cracks. Then it rapidly grew wildly at a speed visible to the naked eye.

In an instant, it transformed into a massive, thirty-foot-tall giant tree, as crimson as fire.

The giant tree burst forth with crimson brilliance, causing the surrounding temperature to rise sharply, unbearably scorching.

The crowd, each with faces full of astonishment, held their breath and concentrated. Rubbing their eyes in utter disbelief.

Alex, however, had a gleam of light in his eyes. A satisfied smile curling at the corners of his mouth.

Under the eager gazes of the crowd, a crimson fruit formed on the tips of the giant tree’s branches. It rapidly expanded at a visible speed, instantly growing to the size of a full-grown adult.

Then it drooped down from the branch, slowly landing on the ground. Its outer shell covered in cracks.

Crack... Crack...

A moment later, the hard outer shell of the crimson fruit shattered with a resounding bang.

An elegant, holy, and graceful figure clad in red slowly stepped out from the fruit.

It was none other than Lara, bathed in the dual energies of ice and fire. Her expression proud and solemn.

"F... Fourth Elder?!"

"Holy crap!"

Lara’s wildly arrogant, cool, and dazzling grand entrance shocked absolutely everyone present. This included Tobias, whose face was currently full of bitterness.

He originally thought that his entrance just now, holding a whisk and arriving on a flying sword, was already dazzling enough. But looking at it now, compared to Sister Lara, he was simply overwhelmingly outclassed.

Sister Lara directly used her own abilities to birth a towering fire tree.

Then urged the fierce energy to condense into a fruit. And emerged from within the fruit itself.

Even those Saints and Great Emperors throughout ancient and modern history might not have had an entrance as incredibly beautiful as this.

Tsk tsk, sister Lara usually looked so gentle and obedient. Who would have thought she was so deeply versed in the Dao of Aura Farming?

The disciples couldn’t help but whisper to each other, bursting into fervent discussion.

"How could the aura on the Fourth Elder’s body become so terrifying..."

"Idiot, haven’t you seen it yet?! The Fourth Elder has already broken through to the Supreme Realm!"

"Wh-what the heck?! Supreme Realm?!"

"The Fourth Elder’s cultivation was clearly the weakest among the four elders. How is it possible for her to be the first to break through to Supreme?!"

"Who knows? But either way, she just did it..."

Amidst the continuous discussions of the crowd, Lara walked gracefully in front of Alex. She nodded slightly and respectfully curtsied. "Paying respects to the Saint Lord!"

"Lara, dispense with the formalities." Alex nodded with a satisfied smile.

"It seems you truly haven’t let down my expectations."

"The Saint Lord gave the order, commanding me to break through to Supreme within a 50 days. How could this little sister dare to disobey?" Lara smiled enchantingly. Taking out a thumb-thick jade bottle from her robes, she respectfully presented it to Alex.

Alex looked curious. "This is...?"

"Saint Lord, this is the Unregretful Pill refined from the Heavenly Heart Unregretful Orchid you gave me previously," Lara said excitedly.

"I have refined pills since childhood, and the pills I’ve refined in my life number in the thousands, if not tens of thousands."

"However, this single Unregretful Pill is the absolute finest masterpiece I am most satisfied with among all the pills I’ve ever refined in my life!"

"After the Saint Lord consumes this pill, it will inevitably stabilize your Dao heart and greatly increase your cultivation."
