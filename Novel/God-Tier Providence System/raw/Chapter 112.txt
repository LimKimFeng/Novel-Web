Chapter 112

Without the burden of Blaze, Alex moved alone. His speed as fast as lightning and rushing wind.

In the blink of an eye, he crossed several hundred miles and arrived at the foot of Feather Transformation Peak.

The area before him was planted full of exotic flowers and rare grasses. Looking as if they were fiercely competing in beauty.

But upon closer inspection, every single petal and every single blade of grass was engraved with fine, dense formation patterns.

"What an exquisite peak-protecting grand formation." Alex pinched a petal with his index and middle fingers, instantly feeling a needle-like prick of spiritual energy pouring into his fingertips.

If the one touching it wasn’t him, but a cultivator below the Enlightenment Realm, they would probably have half their life claimed in an instant.

"Although the Hundred Thousand Great Mountains are remote and impoverished, there is truly no lack of strange talents and extraordinary people." Alex muttered to himself.

"Such a clever formation must certainly be the work of Aerin."

Aerin was one of the Ten Elders of the Divine Blade Sect. His sword techniques were not considered outstanding, but he was solely dedicated to studying formations. Reaching a state of profound perfection and becoming obsessed to the point of madness.

His most outstanding masterpiece was the only Saint-tier sword formation in the current world: The Primordial Antiquity Sword-Annihilation Killing Formation.

In the original novel, under the influence of the Protagonist Halo, this formation naturally fell into Knox’s possession.

But after being casually used to slay a minor boss and push the plot forward, it became nothing more than the security system outside the protagonist’s base camp. Never showing any outstanding performance again.

What a waste! What extravagance! A reckless waste of heavenly opportunity!

Alex narrowed his eyes. Secretly making up his mind.

Once he got into the Divine Blade Sect, he absolutely had to build a good relationship with Aerin. Under the premise of obtaining the Vault of Divine Blade Heart, he also had to find a way to get his hands on this Primordial Antiquity Sword-Annihilation Killing Formation.

...

As Alex was silently calculating, he suddenly sensed two unfamiliar gazes from behind.

He didn’t turn his head. Merely releasing a sliver of mental energy to investigate. He saw an old woman and a girl in red appearing about half a mile behind his position.

The girl in red looked to be in her early twenties, about the same age as Elara and Joanna.

Her skin was fair, her features exquisite, and her silky black hair flowed elegantly down to her waist.

Every frown and smile fully displayed the demeanor of a young lady from a prestigious family. However, her cultivation was far too weak, merely at Qi Gathering.

The old woman beside her had an elegant expression and a steady temperament.

One glance was enough to tell she was a ruthless character not easily provoked. Although her cultivation was stronger than the girl’s, it was still merely at the early stage of Core Formation.

The exact same level as Julian.

Facing two ants with such low strength, Alex had no interest in understanding their temperaments. He concealed his cultivation, acted as if he hadn’t seen them at all, and minded his own business playing with the floral formation on the ground.

"Aunt Mira, look." The girl noticed Alex in the distance and asked doubtfully.

"Who is he, and why has he also come to the foot of Feather Transformation Peak?"

"To answer the Young Miss, you are not the only one entering the sect today." The old woman answered respectfully.

"This person is presumably a young master from another aristocratic family."

"Hmm... then why don’t I recognize him?" The girl muttered.

"Do the aristocratic families of our Southwestern region have such a handsome and composed young master?"

"Aunt, could I trouble you to go and ask which family’s young master he is?"

"This... isn’t quite appropriate, is it?" The old woman looked troubled, feeling it was somewhat improper.

But seeing the girl’s eager, pleading gaze, she could only nod helplessly. "This servant obeys."

Immediately, the old woman walked gracefully until she stood before Alex. She bowed and said respectfully, "Greetings to this young master!"

"This servant is Mira, paying my respects."

Alex turned his head and looked at the old woman’s deeply wrinkled face.

He remained silent for a long, long time before faintly spitting out three words.

"Who asked?"

"This..." Mira’s expression changed several times. Left completely speechless by Alex’s attitude that jabbed straight into her lungs.

After pondering for a moment, she barely managed to squeeze out a smile. "Who this old servant is naturally isn’t important."

"That young lady over there, however, is the precious daughter raised by the Third Madam of our Cale family, Aya Cale."

Aya Cale.

Alex wasn’t unfamiliar with this name. According to Taryn’s memories, Aya Cale was the Third Miss of the Cale family.

One of the five most powerful families in the Hundred Thousand Great Mountains.

However, what Alex didn’t expect was that the Third Miss of the Cale family actually had so little prestige.

He had subconsciously assumed that a family ranking in the top five of the Southwestern region, even if not as overwhelmingly powerful as the Hale family, would at least be at the level of a second-tier Saint lineage.

As it turned out, the dignified Third Miss of a major family only brought a single old woman at Core Formation as her guard when traveling.

Tsk, tsk, tsk. No wonder, with just the Divine Blade Sect’s level of strength, they were able to act as absolute kings and tyrants of the Southwestern region for so many years.

Seeing Mira looking at him with a smiling face, Alex remained silent before saying faintly, "And so?"

"Seeing the little friend’s imposing appearance and extraordinary looks, you must also be holding a Mountain-Visiting Token to enter the sect, correct?" The old woman said with a smile.

"From now on, young master, you and my family’s young miss will be fellow disciples."

"Once you enter the Divine Blade Sect in the future, I hope you will look out for each other."
