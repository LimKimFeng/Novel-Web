Chapter 253

At this very moment, the appearance of this thousand meter giant was like the descent of the apocalypse, making everyone find it difficult to breathe.

The Crane Ancestor, standing thirty meters tall with his immortal demeanor, was already a massive figure.

But compared to this thousand meter giant, he was like a tiny mouse, making people unable to see even the slightest bit of hope.

Even the massive Azure Phoenix Palace behind him appeared as shabby as a doghouse before this giant.

Cedric and the others were dumbfounded, quiet out of fear like winter cicadas.

After a long while, they finally noticed that within the giant’s entirely golden body, there was a tiny black dot.

It was precisely Alex, standing with his arms crossed, floating at the heart of the giant.

"This..." Nova stared in a daze, muttering to herself.

"Is this the power of a god?"

The Crane Ancestor’s face was ashen like dead ashes. He said hoarsely, "Legend has it that a person bearing a heavenly destiny can cause the Vault of Divine Three Firmaments to merge into one. And when the Three Firmaments unite, it is the moment a calamity descends..."

"Correct." Alex stood with his arms crossed and smiled.

"As expected of the profoundly learned Crane Ancestor, you are indeed knowledgeable of both ancient and modern times."

"To use the Calamity Supreme Being to send you back to your death can be considered me paying you the highest respect."

What is a calamity?

A formidable attack capable of instantly slaughtering the living beings of an entire nation, and an absolute defense capable of resisting any divine ability. Power infinitely approaching conceptual power, capable of forming rules and order through martial force.

It is because of possessing these that the Calamity Supreme Being is called the Calamity Supreme Being.

Back then, Alex did not hesitate at the cost of offending a Saint to snatch away the Vault of Divine Magatama and the Vault of Divine Mirror precisely for this.

In front of the Calamity Supreme Being, anyone below a Saint is but an ant.

Now that he had mastered this divine ability, before ascending to the Immortal Realm, he had absolutely no rival in this world.

Alex stood expressionlessly with his arms crossed, merely closing his eyes and shifting his thoughts.

The Calamity Supreme Being slowly raised its hand, drawing one of the golden swords from behind its back. Separated by dozens of miles, it slightly swung a sword strike out.

The next second, the Crane Ancestor’s body began to collapse and decompose, turning into dust.

Compared to the Tiger and Wolf Ancestors, who screamed in terror and lost all demeanor before being annihilated, the Crane Ancestor appeared extremely calm, constantly wearing a smile, calmly facing the descent of death.

To personally witness the power of the Calamity Supreme Being before his death, he already had no regrets.

To die under its sword strike could also be considered his special honor.

The exact moment the Crane Ancestor was annihilated, a palace behind him collapsed with a resounding bang.

Albert turned to look, instantly feeling a cold shiver run through his body.

This palace was named the Blessing Shelter Palace, the place where the Heavenly Flame Imperial Family burned incense to pray and seek the ancestors’ protection whenever disaster descended.

Now the three great ancestors had all lost their lives, and this Blessing Shelter Palace was also destroyed within the calamity.

After annihilating the Crane Ancestor, the Calamity Supreme Being that occupied the entire Profound Phoenix Square instantly vanished, turning into golden light splashing everywhere.

Alex slowly descended from mid-air. Expressionlessly looking down at the crowd, he shouted indifferently.

"My name is Alex, the Saint Lord of the Hale family and the strongest person in the current era of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty."

"Learning that the King of the South, Albert, in collusion with Former Empress Nova, intended to usurp the imperial throne and commit treason, I specially called upon the Saint Lords of the Blackwood and Sol families to lead their clan elites here to purge the rebels and rescue the dynasty."

"Anyone willing to surrender, I shall spare. If anyone dares to remain obstinately blind to reason, they will be killed without pardon!"

These words were vigorous and powerful as a great yellow bell, echoing through every corner of the venue.

After witnessing the terrifying power of the Calamity Supreme Being and Alex single-handedly annihilating the Three Great Ancestors, the Mahayana Realm experts who had defected to Albert’s side all switched sides once again.

One after another, they dropped their weapons and spiritual artifacts, lowered their heads, raised their hands, and silently walked over to the three great clans.

In an instant, the subordinates around Albert and Nova had all surrendered, leaving only the mother and son standing in opposition to the tens of thousands present.

Alex stood with his hands behind his back. Looking at Albert and Nova, he said faintly. "Do you two have anything left to say?"

Nova gave a tragic, self-mocking smile and said hoarsely. "Alex, I truly never expected this. Albert and I schemed and endured for so many years. We finally encountered this god-given opportunity of Aurelia’s severe illness to make our move and seize the throne."

"I didn’t expect that, just as this throne was within our grasp, our entire plan would be ruined by you, the lord of a mere Saint Lineage family."

"Alex, I must admit, your strength is indeed fully worthy of the title of the strongest person in the current era of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty."

"The Emberhal family has occupied this realm for nearly ten thousand years. Now, it seems it is ultimately going to change its name and switch masters."

With that said, spiritual energy burst from Nova’s palm, summoning a mass of dazzling golden light that radiated seething murderous intent.

Cedric and the others felt their hearts tighten, shouting anxiously. "Saint Lord, watch out!"

However, Alex’s expression remained unchanged, standing in place with his hands behind his back.

"I, hate..."

"Hate the heavens!!"

Nova’s eyes glared wide, letting out a sharp, furious roar that echoed through heaven and earth.

The next second, the pure spiritual energy gathered to the extreme in her palm ruthlessly blasted into her own chest!
