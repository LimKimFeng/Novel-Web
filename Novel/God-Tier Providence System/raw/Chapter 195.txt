Chapter 195

"Hmm?" Kray frowned slightly, a trace of doubt arising in his heart.

"According to records in ancient texts, these three Evil Buddhas are the dark, evil thoughts the Ancient Phoenix King discarded in the mortal realm. Thus their appearances are extremely ugly."

"Among them, the Greed Buddha has three thousand demonic eyes, the Anger Buddha has three thousand bloody mouths, and the Delusion Buddha has three thousand grotesque appendages."

"How come these three guys are somewhat different from what is recorded in the ancient texts?"

"Could it be... the ancient texts made a mistake?"

Kray did not know that the records in the ancient texts had not made a mistake.

The true three Evil Buddhas had already been silently annihilated by Alex using the Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword late last night.

And at this very moment, the three giant Buddhas of Greed, Anger, and Delusion appearing before the crowd were exactly the Heaven and Earth Dharma Idols summoned by Alex, Alistair, and Malakai.

This was precisely the cooperative plan Alex had discussed with the other two Saint Lords last night.

"Such powerful auras..." Feeling the fierce killing intent radiating from the bodies of the trio, while the Saint Lords trembled in shock, a trace of suspicion also arose in their hearts.

"However... why do these three Evil Buddhas look somewhat familiar?"

"It feels like... we’ve seen them somewhere before."

Knox, however, didn’t sense anything amiss at all. His face full of excitement.

"These three Evil Buddhas have finally revealed themselves!"

"Everyone, do not panic. I will deal with them!"

Saying this, Knox directly brought out that Evil-Repelling Gourd from his storage ring. With a casual wave, he tossed the gourd into mid-air.

"Divine Spirits Repel Evil!"

A burst of dazzling green light burst from the mouth of the gourd. Completely enveloping the massive bodies of the three Evil Buddhas within it.

According to what Leona had said, this Evil-Repelling Gourd was the natural enemy of the three Evil Buddhas.

As long as they were illuminated by the evil-repelling green light, the strength of the three Evil Buddhas would be massively damaged. They might even directly melt into bloody water and their souls scattering and flying away.

Originally, after bringing out the Evil-Repelling Gourd, Knox felt he already had a solid grasp of the situation, wearing a smile as if he already had a well-thought-out plan.

But the next second, his smile directly froze.

Under the illumination of the green light, the three major Evil Buddhas were not affected in the slightest.

Instead, they looked at each other and threw their heads back, laughing loudly and endlessly. Their smiles full of contempt and disdain.

"Hahaha, you brat are so naive."

"Using this kind of toy to give us a sunbath?"

"...Hmm???" Seeing that these three Evil Buddhas were actually completely unaffected by the Evil-Repelling Gourd, and were instead directly sunbathing in its light, Knox couldn’t help but look completely bewildered, momentarily at a loss.

"No, this Evil-Repelling Gourd definitely has a suppressing effect on these three Evil Buddhas."

"It’s just that my cultivation is too low to unleash its true power." Knox gritted his teeth.

Immediately poured all his spiritual energy into it, and focused his mental energy to the absolute limit.

He directly drove the Evil-Repelling Gourd with all his might, making the green light as dazzling as the sun.

"Tsk, why is it so blindingly bright?" Alistair shielded his face with his hand, curling his lips in slight annoyance. Watching the Evil-Repelling Gourd slowly float in front of him, he directly raised his hand and pinched the gourd between his index and middle fingers.

Then tossed it straight into his mouth, chewed it to pieces with a loud crunch, crunch.

"Wh... What?!" Seeing this scene, the Saint Lords of the major Saint Lineages were all completely dumbfounded.

"This guy actually directly... ate a spiritual treasure?!"

Many who originally had the desire to make a move were directly dissuaded by this scene. Quietly tucking their own spiritual artifacts deeper into their robes.

Knox’s face turned ashen green as he looked at Leona.

"This... this is impossible." Leona desperately shook her head.

"The ancient texts clearly record that the Evil-Repelling Gourd can disperse all evil energy in the world. It is exactly the natural enemy and nemesis of the three great Evil Buddhas."

"How could it... how could it not have the slightest effect?"

Alex secretly sneered in his heart. This Knox’s Protagonist Halo was truly heaven-defying.

He hadn’t found this Evil-Repelling Gourd during his thorough sweep last night. Who would have expected that it would actually be found by these three, and even brought here to deal with him.

But fortunately, at this very moment, what you are facing is not some Greed, Anger, and Delusion.

But the strongest Saint Lords of the three most formidable Saint Lineages in the entire Heavenly Flame Dynasty.

"Everyone, do not panic!" Leona said resoundingly.

"These three guys are nothing more than avatars transformed from the dark, evil thoughts the Ancient Phoenix King left behind in the mortal realm."

"Even if they possess Supreme Realm cultivation, they are merely evil obstacles without any sapience!"

"With so many of us, as long as we join forces, we can definitely defeat them!"

"Moreover, as long as we decapitate the three of them, we can obtain the fortuitous encounter of the Buddhist Saint Heavenly True One!"

Rallied by Leona, the Saint Lords of the major Saint Lineages once again revealed their battle intent.

Kray was the first to step forward to support his daughter. Waving his hand, he materialized a folding fan. With a snap, he opened the fan ribs and shouted resoundingly. "Absolute Immortal Fan, move!"

Streaks of ink-colored light beams burst from the fan ribs, transforming into a sky full of light bullets.

The crowd of Vire family disciples behind him also successively brought out their respective strongest divine abilities, launching a fierce offensive.

Alistair frowned, just about to make a move to block.

Malakai smiled faintly and said contemptuously. "I’ll do it!"

Malakai clasped his palms together, and summoned an entirely deep red gourd and tossed it into mid-air.

This gourd was actually Malakai’s signature spiritual artifact, the Heaven and Earth Gourd.

But at this very moment, having been disguised and packaged by Alex using the power of the Buddhist Dao, it looked like a genuine, authentic Buddhist spiritual artifact; fundamentally no one recognized it.
