Chapter 151

"An ant of the current world. So weak it cannot stand the wind. This old man can annihilate him with a casual wave of a hand."

"Today, this old man’s soul descended to the world precisely to cleanse the sect."

"You, must die!"

Feeling the solemn killing intent radiating from the Evergreat Sword Ancestor, Arcan’s entire body trembled.

He unleashed the strongest evasion technique of his life. Using all his strength wanting to escape and survive.

But the Evergreat Sword Ancestor did not grab him back through the void like before. Instead, he cupped his hands as if holding a ball. Narrowed his eyes. Let out a stern, thundering shout.

"Vault of Divine Mirror!"

Spiritual energy flowed and flashed. Transforming into a radiantly brilliant small mirror that appeared in his palm.

The mirror’s surface refracted golden light. Shining upon Arcan’s body.

Arcan had been desperately fleeing. But the moment the beam of light shot over him, he was instantly nailed dead in place.

Completely unable to move an inch.

"Master, you... you are actually using these Saint Artifacts to deal with me?!" Arcan widened his eyes, face full of unbelievable terror.

The Evergreat Sword Ancestor did not change his expression.

He took out two curved, hook-shaped magatama from his robes. Held them in his palms. Closed his eyes to focus his mind.

The golden light around him flowed rapidly. Condensing into dazzling stars.

In an instant, thousands of golden stars were formed. Crowding around the Evergreat Sword Ancestor like a myriad of stars revolving around the moon.

"Vault of Divine Magatama!"

A thundering shout. The magatama in his palms merged into a single jade pendant, bursting forth with sharp, piercing sword intent.

The next second, jade swords blasted out like a violent storm. A golden downpour descending from the heavens.

Over a thousand jade swords shuttled straight through Arcan’s body like beams of light.

After a round of indiscriminate bombardment, his entire body was riddled with countless hideous, bloody holes.

His body trembled slightly. With a heavy thud, he fell to one knee in mid-air. Scarlet blood continuously flowing from the corners of his mouth and his wounds.

Trembling, he raised his head. Focused his gaze.

The Evergreat Sword Ancestor was already grasping a giant golden sword. Radiating an awe-inspiring, chilling aura of clear killing intent.

"Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword!"

Feeling the terrifying spiritual energy on this sword, looking at the Evergreat Sword Ancestor’s majestic figure under the illumination of the golden light, Alex stood behind him. His eyes revealing fanatical excitement.

"So these are the legendary Three Great Vault of Divine Saint Artifacts of the Evergreat Sword Ancestor?"

"Excellent. They are truly even more powerful than I imagined."

In legends, the three great Saint Artifacts created by the Evergreat Sword Ancestor were all named after the Vault of Divine. The Vault of Divine Mirror. The Vault of Divine Magatama. The Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword.

Among them, the Vault of Divine Mirror was connected to all realms. Capable of casually traversing space and time. Ascending to the heavens or descending into the earth.

The Vault of Divine Magatama possessed earth-shattering power. Able to slaughter an entire city in a single instant.

And the Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword was the Evergreat Sword Ancestor’s strongest weapon.

It had once suppressed and killed three thousand chaotic demons with a single strike at the peak of the Demon Slaughter in the Immortal Realm.

Alex didn’t expect he would actually have such an honor today. To witness the power of all three artifacts with his own eyes.

Of course, for him, just seeing them was enough.

Arcan’s face was pale. Ashen as death, absolute despair. Facing the terrifying divine might of the Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword, he fundamentally had no thoughts of fleeing or resisting.

"Master, I beg you to spare..."

His final plea could not be spoken. A single strike from the Evergreat Sword Ancestor fell. Directly annihilating him into fine powder, turning him to ash and smoke that vanished completely.

The crowd of Sunblaze Peak below were all stunned watching this.

A mighty figure at the peak of the Supreme Realm, eradicated so easily.

It seemed that this one was the true incarnation of the Evergreat Sword Ancestor.

Although they couldn’t find any proof, they all unanimously thought this way. Because if this person was not the true Evergreat Sword Ancestor, then their Divine Blade Sect would be facing an imminent catastrophe.

After suppressing and killing Arcan, the Evergreat Sword Ancestor unhurriedly turned around to look at Alex.

"You little brat who intended to wash the Divine Blade Sect in blood and use the Soul-Devouring Demon Swords to increase your cultivation. This old man will now personally..."

He raised the Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword in his hand.

However, before he could finish his words, his eyes were stung by a sudden burst of golden light.

"Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword, is it? Truly a good treasure." Enveloped within the golden light, mockery revealed itself in Alex’s eyes.

"Now, it’s time for me to properly experience the feeling of controlling such a artifact."

At this very moment, Alex and the Evergreat Sword Ancestor each grasped an identical giant golden sword in their hands.

Whether it was the radiating aura, the oppressive might, or the dazzling intensity of the golden light. Almost completely identical.

Everyone hanging from the divine tree widened their eyes in absolute disbelief.

"This... how is this possible?!" Vincent said in terror.

"The reason Sunblaze Peak has two Swords of the Martial Ancestor is because a single sword could not bear the Ancestor’s formidable cultivation and sword intent."

"However, the Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword is the Ancestor’s personal Artifact. How could there possibly be two?"

"And the other one... is actually in Alex’s hands?!"

The Evergreat Sword Ancestor’s expression was similarly solemn. Finally revealing a trace of gravity in his eyes.

"Little brat, are you a believer of this old man?"

"Otherwise, why would you forge a counterfeit completely identical to this old man’s weapon?"

Alex froze for a moment, unable to help but scoff. "Old geezer, aren’t you being a bit too narcissistic?"

"I am the Saint Lord of the Hale family, Alex."

"As for saying the Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword in my hand is a counterfeit, I’m afraid it’s too early for that."
