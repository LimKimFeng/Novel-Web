Chapter 76

Just trying to balance the malicious aura and bloodlust in his body from now on would be enough for Knox to suffer immensely.

Watching this scene, Knox was so enraged his mouth twitched violently. He almost couldn’t hold back from vomiting blood.

Mine. It’s all mine. All those treasures were originally supposed to belong to me.

Yet now, Alex had profited from every single one of them. This old shit truly deserved to die.

But at this moment, having his acupoints sealed by Aurelia, he was completely unable to express the slightest bit of emotion.

After a long time, Aurelia finally spoke to state her position.

"Since I imparted the Heavenly Flame Bone-Shattering Palm to Knox, he is equivalent to half a disciple of mine."

"Regardless of anything else, Lady Joanna was injured by someone from the Heavenly Flame Dynasty. I naturally must give Saint Lord Alex a proper explanation."

Alex instantly rejoiced in his heart. But he remained perfectly calm on the surface.

"I wonder how Her Majesty the Empress intends to give me this explanation?"

"Two hundred miles north of your Hale family’s holy land lies a blessed land called the Void Domain Cave Heaven." Aurelia said solemnly.

"Within it lies the karmic blessing left behind by ancient Saints."

"The speed of comprehension and cultivation within this blessed land is many times faster than in the outside world."

"Furthermore, surrounding the Void Domain Cave Heaven is a Heaven and Earth-tier formation personally established by me."

"If Saint Lord Alex does not disdain it, this blessed land shall be yielded to your Hale family as compensation."

Lily, Selene, and Lyra were utterly shocked.

They knew perfectly well what kind of place the Void Domain Cave Heaven was.

A cultivation sanctuary that the Empress had expended immense spiritual energy to open.

It was originally intended to select the most outstanding juniors within the Heavenly Flame Dynasty, place them inside, and personally train them into the future backbone of the dynasty.

Yet now, she was actually ceding this blessed land to the Hale family. Even as an apology, wasn’t this far too sincere?

Alex was practically ecstatic inside. On the surface, however, he pretended to be completely indifferent, clasping his fists.

"In that case, I must thank Her Majesty the Empress."

"From now on, I will definitely urge my family’s juniors to cultivate diligently."

"I will strive for them to grow up as quickly as possible so they may become the backbone of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty!"

Fearing that Aurelia might regret her decision, Alex immediately turned around and left.

Seeing Alex leave, Leo also hurriedly flashed an awkward smile, bid his farewells, and scurried away. He had only been able to stand here swaggering because he was riding on Alex’s coattails.

With Alex gone, if he, a heavily wanted criminal, stayed behind, wouldn’t he just be throwing himself into the net?

Lyra took a deep breath and glared at Knox. Her beautiful eyes filled with profound coldness and intense hatred.

Lily said, "Sister Lyra, what did I tell you?"

"I told you to stay far away from that Knox and not help him with anything. Otherwise it would definitely bring misfortune upon yourself."

"Now, my words have hit the mark, haven’t they?"

"To seek his revenge, Knox colluded with Leo and stole your Profound Ice Blue Moon Hairpin."

"If it weren’t for him, how could you have lost this supreme treasure?"

Selene also said coldly. "This Knox is a heartless, unjust, cold-blooded, and ruthless beast."

"Keeping someone like him with us will only ruin the reputation of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty."

"Because of him, we have lost far too much."

"My Heavenly Heart Unregretful Orchid. Sister Lyra’s Heavenly Spirit Flame Lotus. And now Sister Lyra’s Profound Ice Blue Moon Hairpin."

"Even the Void Domain Cave Heaven that Her Majesty opened for the future of the dynasty has been used to make a wedding dress for someone else!"

"Your Majesty, in my opinion, we should expel Knox from the capital immediately, before he causes an even greater disaster."

"Otherwise, when he inevitably brings about a world-shattering calamity in the future, it will be too late."

Aurelia remained silent for a moment before sighing. "I will consider your suggestions."

"For now, return and rest. Allow me to think this over carefully before making a decision."

Although Lily and Selene were filled with boundless resentment, they had no choice but to turn and leave.

Aurelia sighed once more. "Lyra, you are dismissed as well."

"...Yes." Lyra nodded and glared coldly at Knox one last time. Her beautiful eyes looked as if they were about to spew millennium-old ice.

After a long while, unable to suppress the raging anger in her heart, she waved her jade-like hand through the void.

Slap.

A crisp palm print covered in ice shards landed squarely on Knox’s face.

Only then did she flick her sleeves, turn around, and leave.

Once only the two of them remained in the entirety of Azure Phoenix Hall, Aurelia waved her hand, releasing the acupoint sealing technique.

Regaining his mobility, Knox instantly slumped onto the floor with a thud. Clutching his frozen left cheek. Lips bruised purple. Face pale as paper. Teeth chattering uncontrollably.

"Young Master Knox, I am deeply grateful for your life-saving grace in the past and have always treated you as an honored state guest of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty." Aurelia said faintly.

"But your performance today has truly disappointed me far too much."

"Y-Your Majesty, what did I do wrong?!" Knox’s face was full of grievance. Speaking in a crying tone.

"I just wanted to exact my revenge and wipe away my humiliation. Is that wrong?!"

"Besides, you were the one who gave me the idea earlier, telling me to seek Princess Moon’s help!"

"Heading to the Five Poisons Valley and asking Leo to take action, that was exactly the idea Princess Moon gave me!"

"And that Profound Ice Blue Moon Hairpin, she clearly gave it to me of her own volition! How did it turn into me stealing it?!"

"Why do none of you believe me, and instead choose to believe Alex’s one-sided story?!"
