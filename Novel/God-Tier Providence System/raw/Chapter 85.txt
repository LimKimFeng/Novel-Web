Chapter 85

This Purple Cloud Fruit was an incredibly rare tonic for the demon race. But for plant-based demons like flower demons and tree demons, it was a miraculous elixir surpassing even dragon liver or phoenix marrow.

The power contained within this single fruit alone would be enough to help Helen break through to the Mahayana Realm.

In a month’s time, let alone a measly minor realm, even breaking through an entire major realm wouldn’t be difficult at all.

"Saint Lord, Helen’s roots are planted here, so I cannot easily move in the short term, lest I severely damage my origin energy." Helen said respectfully.

"Once my great revenge is achieved, I will migrate my roots to the Hale family. From then on, I swear to serve the Saint Lord with absolute loyalty until death!"

"Excellent!" Alex nodded, laughing with satisfaction.

"Lady Helen, seeing you possess such resolve truly fills me with boundless joy."

"Not just because the Hale family has gained a massive pillar of support..."

"But even more so because you have finally broken free from the torment of that rotten peach blossom, shedding your mortal body and gaining a new life!"

Alex leaped up, landing back onto the floating cloud in mid-air. With an elegant wave of his hand, he steered the cloud and drifted away.

Joanna’s face was full of sheer adoration. "Saint Lord, you are truly too amazing!"

"Such a formidable Millennium Flower Demon, unkillable and indestructible, practically invincible..."

"Yet with just a few words, you transformed her into a disciple of our Hale family!"

"From now on, with that flower demon guarding this place, this Void Domain Cave Heaven will definitely become an absolute paradise for our juniors!"

Alex nodded and smiled faintly. "That is what I was thinking as well."

"However, to build a true paradise for the clan’s juniors, Helen’s strength alone is not enough."

"We must leave one more person here."

Joanna was bewildered. "Saint Lord, who are you speaking of?"

"You." Alex smiled. "Joanna, are you willing to remain in the Void Domain Cave Heaven?"

"Ah? Me?" Joanna pointed at herself, instantly looking completely lost.

"If the Saint Lord commands it, it is naturally Joanna’s unshirkable duty."

"But Joanna doesn’t understand, why me?"

"Because there is no one more suitable than you." Alex said sternly.

"Right now, in the entire clan, you are the most outstanding among the younger generation."

"Aside from the Four Great Elders and the branch heads, you are the only clan disciple to have broken through to the Core Formation Realm."

"Other than you, the clan cannot find a second junior capable of taking charge and standing on their own."

Being praised like this, Joanna felt a wave of secret delight in her heart.

Alex continued. "Secondly, now that you possess the Innate Poison King Saint Body, you must urgently cultivate poison manipulation arts. Otherwise, it would be a reckless waste of heavenly treasures."

"I happen to have an excellent poison manipulation art, but it is my personal creation and not stored in the clan’s scripture pavilion."

"If you returned to the holy land to cultivate this art, it might invite gossip and criticism. It could also pose a fatal threat to the weaker juniors."

"Keeping you in the Void Domain Cave Heaven serves two purposes. One is to guard this place. The other is for you to enter secluded cultivation here."

"Once you cultivate to the Nascent Soul Realm, the Void Amalgamation Realm, or even the Mahayana Realm, you can become my right-hand woman, conquering the heavens by my side!"

Alex continuously tossed out these massive, heavily-painted flatbreads of hype, completely dizzying Joanna with grand promises.

She pondered for a moment. "Saint Lord, do you mean that as long as I stay here, you will gift me a cultivation art you personally created?"

"Correct." Alex nodded and smiled faintly.

"Aside from the cultivation art, I will also gift you a spiritual artifact."

"This artifact will be more than sufficient to last you until the Mahayana Realm without ever holding you back."

Joanna no longer hesitated, nodding excitedly. "Saint Lord, I am willing!"

"From this day forward, this Void Domain Cave Heaven is my home! Every blade of grass and every tree here is like my parents!"

"Before breaking through to the Mahayana Realm, I will absolutely not take a single step out of this place!"

"I will work together with Lady Helen to fiercely guard this cultivation blessed land for the future generations of our Hale family!"

"Good, what great ambition!" Alex said loudly.

"Then this heavy responsibility is entrusted to you!"

While praising Joanna, he casually browsed the Providence Mall. After scrolling for quite a while, he finally settled on a cultivation method.

[Heavenly Thoroughfare Vicious Poison Art, Grade: Lower-Grade Supreme-tier.]

[Providence Points required for redemption: 30,000 points.]

[The Heavenly Thoroughfare Vicious Poison Art is a cultivation method created by a Master of poisons, the number one poison master of the Heavenly Thoroughfare Holy Land.]

[Upon reaching major completion, the physical body can be forged into a vessel to cultivate ancient venomous insects, allowing the user to manipulate and command them at will.]

Alex stroked his chin, nodding with satisfaction.

This cultivation method wasn’t bad at all. Using the body as a vessel to cultivate ancient venomous insects long since lost to the current era.

In a sense, a low-budget version of his Animal Path.

Moreover, since Joanna possessed the Innate Poison King Saint Body, it would perfectly prevent the toxins from burdening her body during the vessel-forging process.

The only drawback.

The price tag of 30,000 Providence Points was a tiny bit expensive. However, utilizing the system’s convenient installment plan feature, it wouldn’t hurt the wallet quite as much.

With a thought, Alex spent 5,000 Providence Points to redeem the first three Chapters of the Heavenly Thoroughfare Vicious Poison Art.

He stored the contents into a jade slip and handed it to Joanna.

"Joanna, the cultivation method I am going to impart to you is called the Heavenly Thoroughfare Vicious Poison Art." Alex said profoundly.

"Inside this jade slip is the first half of the content."

"Once you train this first half to major completion, I will hand the second half over to you."
