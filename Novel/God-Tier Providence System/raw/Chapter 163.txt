"The one who killed Cyrus was not the First Elder."

"This brat. I truly underestimated him."

Originally, Alex gave Blaze that Supreme-Destroying Knife merely because he was worried about an accident occurring on the way back, strictly as a just-in-case measure.

He never expected that Blaze would actually strive for excellence to such a degree. Using the Supreme-Destroying Flying Knife to kill Cyrus and helping the Holy Land defend against the Noir Family’s invasion.

These Providence Points of mine were spent far too worthily.

"However, this Cyrus is truly interesting." Alex stroked his chin.

"Previously, when slaughtering Venomscale Peak, I annihilated thirty of his family’s elites. Already heavily damaging his Noir Family’s power."

"Yet I didn’t expect this old geezer to actually dare to proactively provoke a war and invade my Hale family Holy Land."

At noon. The sun high in the sky.

Knox utilized sword flight and entered the territory of the Hundred Thousand Great Mountains. His heart filled with excitement as he arrived at the foot of Sunblaze Peak.

It took him half a month to travel from the Heavenly Flame Capital to the Divine Blade Sect.

Over this half-month, Knox had befriended many prominent figures with illustrious reputations. Obtained many fortuitous encounters.

These immensely meaningful harvests allowed Knox to completely regain the self-confidence that had been repeatedly crushed by Alex.

At the same time, it made him firmly believe once again that he was the Heaven-Chosen Son. Blessed by the heavens. Shrouded in Providence.

I came to the Divine Blade Sect to study, and before even arriving at the destination, I have already broken through an entire major realm.

Once I enter the Divine Blade Sect and receive Master Vincent’s true inheritance, won’t I be able to directly step into the Enlightenment Realm, Void Amalgamation Realm, or even the Mahayana Realm?!

Before coming here, the Supreme Realm that Aurelia promised him was practically right around the corner!

Knox could already hardly wait. Looking forward to the scene where, after breaking through to the Supreme Realm, he would trample Alex underfoot, exhale the foul breath in his chest, and wash away his previous humiliation.

Just as his face was full of an idiotic smile and his imagination was running wild, a sharp sword suddenly descended from the sky!

The sword brushed right past the tip of his nose, plunged deeply into the ground. Nearly piercing his skull.

Knox was so frightened his hair stood on end. He slumped onto the ground with a thud.

The next second, a female disciple revealed herself. Pulled the sword from the ground. Her voice cold.

"Who are you?"

"This Senior Sister, you must be a disciple of the Divine Blade Sect, right?" Knox squeezed out an ugly smile.

"My name is Knox. I specially came here from the Heavenly Flame Capital to formally acknowledge a master."

"The Divine Blade Sect is currently sealing the mountain and closing the sect. We are not accepting any new disciples." The female sword cultivator said faintly.

"Please go back. Do not pester us here."

"What?" Knox froze. Hurriedly adding.

"Senior Sister, I am here on the orders of the Heavenly Flame Empress, Aurelia, to pay my respects to Master Vincent!"

"The Heavenly Flame Empress and Master Vincent have been close friends for many years."

"I beg Senior Sister to make an introduction and take me to see Master Vincent!"

Knox had originally thought that by bringing up the Heavenly Flame Empress, coupled with her relationship with Master Vincent, the female disciple would instantly change her expression and take him into the sect with the utmost flattery and fawning.

But unexpectedly, the female sword cultivator’s attitude did not change in the slightest. She said coldly, "You came too late."
