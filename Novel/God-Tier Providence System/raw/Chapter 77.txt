Chapter 77

Seeing Knox’s grief and indignation, Aurelia couldn’t help but feel a massive headache coming on.

This brat. He truly doesn’t understand a single thing, does he?

"Knox, I truly thought you were a smart person, how could you be so foolish today?" Aurelia’s face was full of disappointment.

"Today’s matter originally wasn’t that thorny of a problem."

"Even if you had truly colluded with Leo, Alex would have to consider my face and wouldn’t dare do anything to you."

"But of all the things you absolutely should not have done, you shouldn’t have been slightly baited by him into confessing Lyra’s involvement!"

"Lyra is my sworn sister, her actions represent my will."

"If I didn’t give Alex a satisfactory explanation, he would have broadcasted this matter to the entire world, completely sweeping my reputation into the dirt."

"If you hadn’t fallen for Alex’s trap, how could I have ended up in such a passive position?!"

Hearing Aurelia’s words, Knox instantly felt a surge of bitter remorse in his heart.

Compared to an old fox like Alex, he was ultimately still far too green.

The Millennium Blood Gathering Ginseng, the Profound Ice Blue Moon Hairpin and the Void Domain Cave Heaven, which Aurelia had expended a massive amount of spiritual energy to open up.

Thinking about how Alex had obtained so many incredible resources today, and that these resources were originally all supposed to belong to him, Knox ground his teeth in pure rage. The killing intent in his chest almost burst out uncontrollably.

He fell to his knees once more with a thud, face full of sincerity, eyes revealing a pleading look, Voice carrying a crying tone.

"Your Majesty, I beg you to help me! Help me exact my revenge and wipe away this grudge!"

"If I cannot personally slay that old fox Alex, I will never be able to eat or sleep in peace, and I will die with my eyes unclosed!"

However, Aurelia wasn’t like she was in the beginning, filled with righteous indignation and immediately agreeing on the spot. Instead, her entire person appeared extremely weathered and haggard.

With a complicated expression, she remained silent for a long time before letting out a heavy sigh.

"You keep saying, letting me help you exact your revenge."

"But has the help I gave you really been small?"

"Lily, Selene, and Lyra have already lost their strongest spiritual artifacts, all to help you."

"Tell me, how else do you expect me to help you?"

Knox’s expression was vicious and ruthless. He opened his mouth wanting to speak, but ultimately hesitated and stopped.

He had always thought that his revenge wasn’t actually that troublesome at all.

As long as Aurelia took action, she could strike Alex down in a single blow. Then dispatch the powerhouses of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty to wash the entire Hale family in blood.

Wouldn’t his great revenge be achieved just like that?

However, with Aurelia’s overly cautious, image-conscious personality, unless there was a perfectly justifiable and righteous reason, she would absolutely never rashly take action. She had to prevent ruining her own prestige and the reputation of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty.

Therefore, Knox did not voice his true thoughts aloud. He merely looked at Aurelia pitifully, eyes shining with tears, afraid that saying too much would further ruin his image in this woman’s heart.

"Fine. So be it." Aurelia sighed helplessly.

"It seems that only she can help you now."

"She?" Knox froze slightly, then hurriedly asked with excitement, "Your Majesty, you... you are speaking of Princess Flower?!"

"Speaking of which, ever since I arrived at the Heavenly Flame Dynasty, I have already become well acquainted with Princess Moon, Princess Jade, and Princess Orchid."

"But I have never seen Princess Flower, nor have I ever heard any of you mention her."

"I wonder... for what reason is that?"

Aurelia smiled bitterly. "Ever since the Sixty-Year Demon Purge thirty years ago, her mental state has been somewhat abnormal."

"But right now, for your revenge, she is the only one who can help you achieve it."

Having said this, the space ring on Aurelia’s finger flashed with a dark green light. It immediately transformed into a half-human-tall gourd, standing firmly in the front of the hall.

The entire body of the gourd was a dark greenish-black. Covered all over with dense, fine sacred patterns. Knox recognized that many of these were powerful sealing talismans reaching the Heaven and Earth-tier. Even the Supreme-tier.

Suddenly, a cyan-green gardenia flower appeared in mid-air. It slowly drifted down, landing perfectly on the top of the gourd.

The very instant it landed, all the sealing formations were violently broken. The gourd was covered with hideous, jagged cracks. Terrifying spiritual energy and blinding green light burst forth.

Under Knox’s shocked gaze, the green light permeated every single corner of the grand hall.

A vigorous, agile figure suddenly emerged from the light.

A woman with unkempt hair, a dirty face, and a twisted expression.

Long, dark green hair hung messily down her back, fair skin stained all over with a bizarre, eerie green sap. Although her facial features were exquisite and beautiful, her eyes were currently glaring wide and the corners of her mouth were split open. Revealing a twisted, sickly smile.

Before Knox could even react, the woman pounced on him directly like a wild beast.

She pinned him firmly underneath her, razor-sharp nails piercing through his flesh, crystal-clear drool continuously oozing from the corners of her mouth.

While letting out beast-like whimpers, she also emitted bursts of low growls.

"Purge the demons!"

"Purge the demons!"

Knox’s face was deathly pale. Even using every last ounce of his strength, he couldn’t parry the woman’s terrifying power.

Just as he watched her open her bloody maw, about to take a bite right into his carotid artery...

Aurelia, expressionless, shouted calmly. "Cease your insolence!"

The woman finally seemed to regain a sliver of rationality. Her two sharp claws released Knox. She obediently scurried over to Aurelia.

Like a docile wild beast, she meekly lowered her head.

Knox, still trembling in shock, asked with a quivering voice. "She... she is..."

"The daughter of the Grand General. Nyx." Aurelia stroked the woman’s hair, her eyes filled with heartache.
