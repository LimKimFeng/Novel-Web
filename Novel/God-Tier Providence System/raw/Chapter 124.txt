Chapter 124

"At best, he’ll scold me a few times. At worst, he’ll directly cripple my cultivation."

"Risking such a massive price to help you is a bit too unworthy, isn’t it?"

Lirin hurriedly pleaded. "Senior Sister Fiora, but I can only beg you!"

"I’ve long heard that in the entire Heaven-Turning Peak, Senior Sister Fiora is the most loyal. Always standing up for justice for us junior brothers and sisters!"

"Furthermore, the person I want you to kill isn’t from Heaven-Turning Peak."

"It’s a brat who joined the Divine Blade Sect with me a few days ago. Currently over at Broken Sword Peak."

"Broken Sword Peak?" Fiora revealed a look of confusion.

"Broken Sword Peak has long been abandoned. It hasn’t accepted disciples for decades and only serves as a forbidden area to punish offending disciples."

"Since he just joined the sect, how could he be at Broken Sword Peak?"

"Senior Sister Fiora, you don’t know the whole story." Lirin hurriedly explained.

"That idiot doesn’t understand the rules. On the very day he entered the sect, he completely offended Elder Dariel..."

Immediately, Lirin recounted the entire incident of how Alex had offended Dariel upon entering the sect. Detailing everything from beginning to end.

After listening, a mocking sneer curled at the corners of Fiora’s mouth. "How can this guy be stupid to this degree?"

"Master Dariel is universally recognized as the most narrow-minded among the Ten Elders. Always seeking revenge for the smallest of grievances."

"Of all the people that brat could have offended, he just had to offend him."

Fiora continued with deep interest. "Since that Taryn has already been banished to the Hidden Broken Sword Cave, what is there left for you to worry about?"

"Ever since the Hidden Broken Sword Cave became a forbidden area, not a single disciple locked up there has come out alive."

"In a month or two at the shortest, or half a year at the longest, that brat is dead without a doubt."

"Just hold your patience and wait for the good news. Isn’t that enough?"

"Senior Sister Fiora, I truly cannot endure it!" Lirin said with a bitter face.

"You don’t know this, but the woman I like just happens to have taken a fancy to that Taryn! Today, she even went to deliver wine and food to him."

"If I wait for half a year, the nights are long and dreams are many."

"Furthermore, the assessment in a month is still a massive variable."

"What if that brat performs outstandingly and joins another peak to acknowledge a master? If I try to kill him then, wouldn’t it be harder than ascending to the heavens?"

Lirin gritted his teeth and took a porcelain bottle from his robes. Hardening his heart as he handed it to Fiora.

"As long as Senior Sister Fiora is willing to go to Broken Sword Peak and kill Taryn..."

"This bottle of Jade Dew Pills will be my filial respect to you, Senior Sister!"

"Oh?" Fiora’s eyes lit up. She took the porcelain bottle, opened it to sniff, and an excited smile appeared on her face.

"These Jade Dew Pills are rare treasures! Worth thirty thousand spirit stones apiece!"

"You brat, you’re actually willing to part with an entire bottle to give to me?"

"Truly worthy of being the young master of the Thorne family. How incredibly generous!"

Fiora tucked the Jade Dew Pills into her robes.

A greedy, ferocious sneer curling on her lips.

"Excellent! Rest assured, Junior Brother Lirin!"

"I, Fiora, have always been the most loyal. Junior Brother, your business is my business!"

"At Heaven-Turning Peak, right under Master Rorin’s nose, I wouldn’t dare kill anyone."

"But going to that desolate, remote ghost town of Broken Sword Peak to kill a reckless fool who offended Master Dariel? That isn’t a problem at all."

"Before dawn tomorrow, I will have you looking at that brat’s severed head!"

Late at night, Alex returned to his cave dwelling and lay down on the bed to rest.

After confirming that the elder outside had already fallen asleep, he casually waved a surge of spiritual energy to seal the cave door.

He took out his communication jade slip and contacted Adrian.

"Saint Lord, you’ve finally contacted me!" Adrian said excitedly.

"How is the situation on your end? Is everything going smoothly?"

"Mm... you could say it’s going smoothly." Alex nodded. "Has Knox arrived at the Divine Blade Sect?"

"Not yet. Knox arrived at Nether Fate Territory tonight and just checked into an inn to rest." Adrian replied.

"Based on his speed, it is estimated he will arrive at the Divine Blade Sect in three days."

"Three days..." Alex narrowed his eyes slightly. A trace of urgency in his gaze.

"Alright, I understand."

"Within these three days, I will absolutely uncover the intelligence regarding the Vault of Divine Blade Heart and snatch this treasure into my pocket one step ahead of that brat."

"Right, Tobias and Lara... have they set off to participate in the Dao Vindication Assembly?"

"They have already set off." Adrian replied.

"Tobias and Lara selected Julian, Lucas, and thirty other disciples with the highest cultivation from the clan and headed to the Heavenly Flame Capital."

"But for some reason, the Heavenly Flame Empress said we do not need to participate in the arena battles. We only need to arrive in time for the Secret Realm Treasure Hunt."

"With nothing to do, Tobias and Lara have been taking the clan disciples to sightsee and play around the outskirts of the Heavenly Flame Capital every day to kill time."

"Very good." Alex smiled and nodded.

"Pass my message to Tobias: tell them not to slack off too much."

"The other families have to bitterly fight in the arena and exhaust their strength, while we can use this time to nourish our spirits and store up energy."

"When the time comes to enter the secret realm, every single one of you must risk your lives to perform well and bring the absolute best treasures back to this seat, understood?"

"Understood!"

Having been incredibly bored these past few days, Alex simply dragged Adrian along to chat idly with him.

And while they were chatting, Fiora, dressed entirely in black, had already stealthily infiltrated the Hidden Broken Sword Cave.
