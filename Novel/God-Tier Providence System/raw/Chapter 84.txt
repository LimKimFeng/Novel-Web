Chapter 84

"Beast..."

"Well said." Alex narrowed his eyes and said coldly.

"This brat is nothing more than a beast."

"To think that back then, he deceived and manipulated a woman of my Hale family, bewitching her into severing all ties with our clan."

"Later, when his master rushed over to back him up..."

Immediately, Alex began to recount Knox’s various glorious deeds in explicit detail, holding absolutely nothing back.

The more Helen listened, the uglier her expression became.

By the end of it, her face was completely filled with disgust.

She never expected that the husband she had followed for three lifetimes was actually this kind of despicable, petty villain.

Selfish, cold-blooded, ruthless.

He would not hesitate to sacrifice anyone and everyone for the sake of his own benefits.

A profound sense of grievance welled up in Helen’s heart. She felt a deep, piercing worthlessness regarding her own foolish actions across her past two lives, where she had desperately fought to the death to support Knox.

[Ding! The host has successfully made a female supporting character recognize the true face of the Chosen One, causing her heart to completely die toward him!]

[The Chosen One’s luck value has decreased by 5,000 points. The host receives 10,000 Providence Points!]

Alex narrowed his eyes slightly. A smile curled on his lips.

Good, very good.

It truly wasn’t a waste of all the massive effort he had expended. Between the torturous physical education, the martial suppression, playing the emotional card, and laying out the cold, hard facts, he had finally managed to make this hopelessly incurable simp recognize Knox’s true face.

She no longer had to repeat the disastrous mistakes of her past two lives. Throwing away a millennium of cultivation and fortune all for a single Knox.

His actions weren’t just about plundering luck and earning Providence Points.

At the same time, this was a massive favor to Helen herself.

In his eyes, persuading a simp to turn back was an act of far greater boundless merit than persuading an old prostitute to turn over a new leaf.

Alex calculated silently. If he could just persuade Julian to give up on Isolde, that would be even better.

Probably impossible.

Julian’s sickness was far, far more severe than Helen’s. Helen, at best, was just blindly infatuated. But that Julian was a pure, unadulterated, irredeemably cheap, bottom feeding lick dog.

"Lady Helen, the reason I told you all this is with the hope that you can let this go." Alex said with bitter earnestness.

"As a Millennium Flower Demon, you should possess a boundless future."

"Even becoming a regional Lord is not out of the question."

"Why must you ruin your life and future over petty, romantic entanglements?"

"Let alone the fact that the man you did it for is this kind of beastly scum."

"If you are willing, from this day forward, you might as well pledge your loyalty to me."

"I guarantee to lead you to sweep across the world and become the absolute sovereign of this heaven and earth!"

Faced with the olive branch Alex extended, Helen lowered her head and remained silent.

After a long while, she slowly raised her head. "Saint Lord Alex, I am willing to pledge my loyalty to you."

"But before that, there is one thing I must do."

"Oh?" Alex asked with deep interest.

"What is it?"

"Let me kill Knox with my own hands!" Helen’s expression was cold as frost. Her eyes practically spewing fire as she gritted her teeth.

"That hypocrite who hides behind a facade of morality used his despicable disguise to deceive me for three lifetimes."

"If I do not avenge this grudge, I will not eat or sleep in peace, and I will die with my eyes unclosed!"

"I ask Saint Lord Alex to permit me to personally rip out Knox’s tendons, peel off his skin, and slice him into a thousand pieces!"

Alex couldn’t help but secretly click his tongue.

Just a few minutes ago, she loved Knox so much she was willing to die for him.

Yet a few minutes later, she wanted to rip out his tendons and slice him into a thousand pieces. Truly, the deeper the love, the more profound the hate.

"Alright, no problem!" Alex nodded, agreeing readily.

"This karma, entangled over three lifetimes, should indeed be severed by you personally."

"I will help you with this!"

"Rest assured, it won’t be long before Knox absolutely comes to this Void Domain Cave Heaven."

"What you need to do is seize this time to properly and diligently cultivate."

"When you see Knox again, you can return all the grievances of these three lifetimes back to him in full!"

Helen clenched her fists and nodded resolutely.

Alex quietly opened the Destiny Mall. After browsing for a moment, he selected a heavenly treasure and spent two thousand Providence Points to redeem it.

Spiritual mist swirled in his palm. He summoned a deep purple fruit exuding dense, rolling vapors, and casually tossed it to Helen.

"This Purple Cloud Roland Fruit should be quite helpful to your cultivation."

"You have already wasted many years. Take it and accelerate your cultivation. Do not idle your time away any longer."

Holding the fruit and feeling the incredibly dense spiritual energy radiating from it, Helen was utterly astounded.

"This... is this a Heaven and Earth-tier spirit fruit?"

"Saint Lord, this is our first time meeting, and we were just in conflict. How could I possibly accept such a precious gift?"

Alex smiled faintly. "You have already agreed to pledge your loyalty to me. From this day forward, you are a person of the Hale family."

"Isn’t bestowing resources upon a junior of one’s own clan exactly what a Saint Lord ought to do?"

"Of course, our Hale family also has its own rules."

"Since you have taken my resources, you must produce some results to show this seat."

"Within one month, you must break through at least one minor realm."

"Otherwise, I will punish you."

"No problem!" Helen nodded, smiling confidently.

"If I cannot break through even a single minor realm in a month’s time, I will commit suicide before the Saint Lord to atone for my failure!"
