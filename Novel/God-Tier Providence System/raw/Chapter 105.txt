Chapter 105

Kneeling at the very front, in the most conspicuous position, was exactly Julian.

His face was full of uncontrollable excitement and joy as he shouted loudly, "We pay our respects to the Saint Lord!"

"Mm, all of you, rise." Alex nodded slightly, his gaze sweeping over the crowd as he smiled faintly.

"The hour has not yet arrived, yet you have all gathered early. It seems you are incredibly impatient for the Examination Assembly."

"It appears everyone has achieved no small amount of breakthroughs and is entirely focused on coming to claim their rewards."

The crowd laughed awkwardly, scratching their heads with some embarrassment.

Only Julian confessed without holding back, saying excitedly, "Saint Lord, but of course!"

"For this second Examination Assembly, I have exhausted my heart and soul. I haven’t even slept much!"

"Oh?" Alex said with deep interest.

"In just a short month, you have already broken through to the fifth level of Core Formation?"

"Your progress speed, brat, is even faster than I imagined."

"Heheh, but of course!" Julian stood with his hands on his hips. His face full of pride and arrogance.

"Saint Lord, as far as I know, Lady Elara asked for a leave of absence from you a few days ago and went to the Southwestern region to visit her relatives."

"And Lady Joanna is also staying outside and won’t be returning to the holy land anytime soon."

"Aside from the two of them who cannot participate, among everyone present, no one has achieved more outstanding results than me!"

"The first place in this second Examination Assembly should absolutely belong to me, shouldn’t it?"

Looking at Julian’s ecstatic and incredibly smug appearance, the crowd behind him couldn’t help but show complicated expressions.

Julian’s natural talent and strength naturally needed no questioning. Breaking through from Body Tempering to Core Formation in just two short months was enough to show he was an extraordinary genius.

The problem was this genius’s brain was truly a bit abnormal.

Back then, relying on the Saint Lord’s favor, Isolde had acted tyrannically within the holy land. Bullying and oppressing them in various ways. Recently, she finally managed to infuriate the Saint Lord and was mercilessly expelled from the family.

Yet this Julian actually wanted to welcome her back.

Furthermore, he wanted to gift the Seven Orifices Divine Heart back to her again.

This guy’s brain was absolutely, without a doubt, filled with some abnormality.

However, although everyone was unwilling for Isolde to return, the breakthroughs they had achieved were far inferior to Julian’s. They naturally couldn’t say much.

Alex stood with his hands behind his back, smiling cheerfully. "Julian, it seems that among all the family’s juniors, there is indeed no one who can surpass you."

"Therefore, the champion of this second Examination Assembly..."

Seeing that Alex was about to announce it, Julian was so excited his entire body trembled uncontrollably.

But the very next second, to the north behind the crowd, a blinding pillar of light roared into the sky.

Everyone turned their heads in unison.

The pillar of light was entirely converged from white light. Its top pierced high into the clouds, radiating a majestic, boundless Daoist true essence.

"This... this is..." Tobias’s two disciples, Erik and Sable, were the first to react, saying excitedly, "This is our master’s power!"

The next second, a figure emerged from within the pillar of light. Flying on a sword, he sped rapidly toward them.

It was none other than Tobias.

Draped in a Daoist robe, stepping on a flying sword, holding a whisk in his hands. His entire body emanated the transcendent aura of a Daoist immortal.

"What is the Third Elder doing?" The crowd couldn’t help but be thoroughly confused.

"Everything was perfectly fine, why make such a massive commotion?"

"No, wait..."

"The Third Elder broke through?!"

It was unknown who spotted it first, but everyone widened their eyes in disbelief.

Tobias had shockingly skyrocketed from his original cultivation at the fifth level of Mahayana, breaking through five minor realms straight to the peak of the Mahayana Realm.

The entire venue erupted in an uproar.

After all, Tobias was already several hundreds of years old. His cultivation base had basically completely solidified.

Without the assistance of Heaven and Earth treasures, he would essentially remain at this realm for the rest of his life.

The fact that Tobias could break through was simply more astonishing than if he had suddenly grown two centimeters taller.

But who could have imagined that this old man, would actually break through a full five minor realms out of thin air?

These weren’t the minor realms of Body Tempering or Qi Gathering. These were the minor realms of the Mahayana Realm. Any single minor realm at this stage was enough to bring about a heaven-to-earth difference.

"Third Uncle, how... how did you do it?" Lucas couldn’t help but say in astonishment.

"In just a short month, breaking through five minor realms in the Mahayana Realm..."

"This isn’t something that can be achieved purely through natural talent or hard work!"

Beside him, Julian was somewhat displeased, muttering in a low voice, "What’s the big deal?"

"He only broke through five minor realms, but I broke through twelve minor realms!"

"Even if we compare, I should still be the winner, right?!"

"What a joke!" Someone beside him immediately poured a basin of cold water on his head.

"Do you know what kind of concept it is to still be able to break through upon reaching the Mahayana Realm?"

"Breaking through a single minor realm in the Mahayana Realm completely surpasses twenty of your Core Formation Realm minor realms!"

"Furthermore, this means the Third Elder’s natural talent is not yet exhausted. He even possesses the possibility of charging into the Supreme Realm!"

"Mighty is the Third Elder!"

Listening to the crowd’s excited cheers, Tobias descended from the sky. He arrived before Alex, nodded, and said, "This poor Daoist pays respects to the Saint Lord."

"After the first Examination concluded last time, the Saint Lord commanded this poor Daoist to also accelerate my cultivation and prepare to participate in the second Examination."

"Fortunately, this poor Daoist has finally not disgraced the mission, completing the breakthrough just in time for the day of the Examination."
