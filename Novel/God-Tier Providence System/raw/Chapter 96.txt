Chapter 96

"Very well. Since that is the case, I will trouble my two strong and sturdy virtuous brothers to carry this female demon to the Saint Lord to claim the reward."

Martin took a sip from his wine gourd. Unhurriedly patted his donkey’s rump.

Turned the beast around and prepared to leave gracefully.

However, he hadn’t even walked two or three hundred meters away.

Nyx, lying paralyzed behind them, shot two beams of crimson blood-light from her eyes.

"Purge the demons!"

She opened her mouth. Her originally delicate cherry lips instantly transformed into a terrifying, bloody maw.

From the depths of her throat, she spat out a barrage of blood-red, razor-sharp crystals.

They pierced completely through the white donkey beneath Martin, causing it to explode in a shower of blood and die instantly.

"What?!"

"She’s already completely suppressed by this old man’s Date Pit Nails, yet she can still use such a ferocious cultivation technique?!"

"Just what kind of monster... is this guy?!"

Martin Hale’s eyes widened. His face filled with sheer horror, but the very next second, even more blood-red crystals surged toward his face like a torrential downpour.

He was forced to roll frantically to the side to dodge. Covering his entire body in dirt and dust.

Oscar and Peter were also completely shocked by Nyx’s sudden explosion of ferocity. Their faces full of terror.

Entirely at a loss.

...

Just as these three grown men were about to be completely stripped of their courage by Nyx’s rampage... a pleasant, melodious female voice suddenly echoed from the ground beneath their feet.

"You three, dodge quickly!"

They recognized this voice. Although displeased in their hearts, they had no choice but to retreat.

Having lost the suppressive weight of those three, Nyx, dragging a body completely laden with spiritual artifacts, actually managed to slowly stand back up.

"Purge the demons!"

"Purge the demons!"

With red, bloodshot eyes, she looked around wildly before locking her gaze onto Peter. His massive frame made him the hardest to hide.

Grinding her teeth so hard they emitted a loud creak, she slowly took a step, about to launch herself at him like an arrow.

Suddenly, with a sharp crack, the ground beneath her feet violently burst open. Revealing hideous, jagged fissures.

A gigantic hound, pitch-black as ink and as massive as a small hill, roared and burst forth from the earth.

It directly opened its bloody maw and swallowed Nyx entirely whole.

...

Only then did the three men’s hearts finally settle. They let out a long breath of relief.

Oscar grumbled with dissatisfaction.

"Sister Alice, if you knew it would come to this, why didn’t you just use your Black Fiend to subdue this monster right from the start?!"

"You just had to hide and hold back, nearly causing us to suffer a massive loss!"

Alice looked down at the three of them imperiously and sneered.

"Wasn’t it because you three wanted to hog the reward for yourselves? Hiding it and refusing to tell me?"

"Fortunately, I anticipated this long ago. With just you three pieces of trash, it was fundamentally impossible to catch this female monster."

"Let’s go, Black Fiend! To the Saint Lord’s Hall!"

Alice gave a soft shout. The massive black hound beneath her instantly let out an awoo, spreading its paws and carrying her straight toward the Saint Lord’s Hall.

Oscar, Peter, and Martin looked at each other in dismay. Instantly panicking.

"Sister Alice, wait for me!"

"This female demon was caught by the combined efforts of all four of us! Don’t even think about claiming all the credit for yourself!"

"My Date Pit Nails are still stuck all over her body!"

...

A short while later, the four branch family heads arrived clamorously at the Saint Lord’s Hall.

Inside the hall, Alex was sitting cross-legged on a low couch. Burning incense and resting with his eyes closed.

Alice was the first to rush in, carrying Nyx. She grinned and laughed.

"Saint Lord, I have completed the mission!"

"Oh?" Alex opened his eyes.

A look of deep interest on his face. "Sister Alice, how incredibly heroic."

But before he could offer further praise, Oscar rushed in closely behind her.

"Saint Lord, I have a share in this too!" Oscar Hale said excitedly.

"I was the one who first discovered this female demon, and with a single strike of my trident, I pinned her to the ground, rendering her unable to move!"

"If you don’t believe me, Saint Lord, look! The wound on the back of her neck was caused by my strike!"

Alex lowered his head to glance at it. His face displaying deep interest.

"It truly is a wound caused by your trident. Brother Oscar, how incredibly heroic."

"And me!"

Peter also rushed in right on his heels.

"Saint Lord, that Oscar’s skills were lacking, and he nearly let this female demon escape!"

"It was entirely because I used my Divine Scorpion Soul-Fixing Needles that we managed to capture this demoness!"

"If you don’t believe me, look! Both of this demoness’s calves were pierced completely through by my Divine Scorpion Soul-Fixing Needles!"

Alex looked at Nyx’s calves. Sure enough, two shocking, bloody holes. He couldn’t help but tsk-tsk and shake his head.

"Brother Peter, what a ruthless strike. Absolutely no pity."

"Hmph hmph, but of course."

Peter stood with his arms crossed, just about to properly boast about how heroic he was...

When Martin completely broke the doors open. Panting heavily as he yelled.

"You pack of bastards! Relying on your youth to leave this old man far behind, aren’t you?!"

"Saint Lord, if we’re talking about capturing this female demon, the absolute top credit belongs to me!"

"Look at her! Every single black thread on her body is one of my Immortal-Binding Date Pit Nails!"

"If this old man hadn’t arrived in time, this female demon would have escaped long ago!"

Alex pinched one of the black threads with his fingers. Unable to hold back a chuckle.

"Over four hundred black threads. Over four hundred Date Pit Nails?"

"Brother Martin Hale... if we’re talking about being ruthless and poisonous, you truly take the crown."

Seeing these three men all running in to steal her spotlight, Alice’s face showed deep displeasure.

"So what?!"

"No matter how heroic your performances were, wasn’t this person ultimately captured by me?!"

"You three grown men couldn’t even catch a single little female demon."
