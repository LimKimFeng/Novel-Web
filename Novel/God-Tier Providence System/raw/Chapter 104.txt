Chapter 104

Therefore, even for Alex, who possessed a god’s-eye view of the plot, Elara’s background was a complete mystery. He couldn’t guarantee that going to the Southwestern region would actually lead her to her biological parents.

"Very well." Alex smiled faintly.

"Since you are driven by filial piety, I will naturally not stop you."

"There is no need to wait until the Examination Assembly concludes."

"Go to the First Elder right now. Collect a Ten-Thousand-Mile Cloud and withdraw two thousand spirit stones for travel expenses."

"Take advantage of the fact that it is still early today and set off immediately."

Elara froze.

Then she fell to her knees before Alex with a thud. A layer of mist clouded her eyes.

"Saint Lord, Elara... Elara truly doesn’t know how to repay your immense grace and profound virtue."

"Please rest assured, Saint Lord! Even if Elara truly finds her biological parents, I will definitely return to the Saint Lord’s side and continue to serve you!"

"Silly girl, what kind of words are those?" Alex patted Elara’s head and smiled.

"If you truly manage to find your biological parents, you don’t need to come back."

"After all, a place with family is the only place that can truly be called home, isn’t it?"

"If your biological parents are capable and skilled people, just send me a letter saying you are doing well."

"If they do not have much capability, then bring them back to the holy land."

"I can guarantee you all a lifetime of peace, with no worries about food or clothing."

Elara’s delicate body trembled non-stop. She couldn’t stop shedding scalding, clear tears.

She didn’t understand why the Saint Lord was treating her so incredibly well. At the same time, she didn’t know exactly how she could ever repay the Saint Lord’s grace.

...

In the blink of an eye, three to five days passed.

Without Elara’s meticulous and attentive service, Alex felt somewhat unaccustomed to it. He promoted a little girl from the clan named Cynthia to be his personal maidservant.

Cynthia couldn’t compare to Elara’s obedience and sweetness. But her advantage lay in being clever and quick-witted.

Sometimes she would play clever little tricks, bringing Alex some unexpected pleasant surprises. Other times, she would outsmart herself and mess things up entirely, leaving Alex not knowing whether to laugh or cry.

After a few days of interaction, he accepted the existence of this new maidservant.

...

A few days later, the day of the second Examination Assembly arrived.

The disciples gathered outside the Saint Lord’s Hall early on. Every single one rubbing their fists and wiping their palms, brimming with soaring ambition.

After a month of painstaking cultivation, almost all of them had broken through two or more minor realms.

Their mastery over the cultivation methods they had initially chosen had also reached a realm of profound perfection.

Among them, the most eye-catching focal point was none other than Julian.

Last time, Julian used merely a short month to break through from the seventh level of Body Tempering to the third level of Foundation Establishment.

It was only because Elara appeared out of nowhere, proudly looking down on the crowd with her monstrous achievement of breaking through to Core Formation, that his brilliance was overshadowed.

After another month of painstaking cultivation day and night, Julian had already broken through from the third level of Foundation Establishment to the fifth level of Core Formation.

An arrogant achievement of twelve minor realms.

It could truly be called leaving everyone in the dust. Even those with similarly superior natural talent, like Lucas, Erik, and the others, were left far behind.

"Brother Julian, how did you manage to break through so much in just one month?" A group of young women from the family crowded around him, asking questions endlessly.

"That’s right! Obviously the amount of resources everyone used was about the same."

"Why were you able to break through to Core Formation in just a short month?"

"I expended so much effort just to barely step into Foundation Establishment."

"Could it be that you chose a Heaven-tier cultivation method back then?"

Being surrounded by a group of women with faces full of adoration, Julian felt incredibly validated. He narrowed his eyes, crossed his arms, and said with an air of profound seriousness, "As for my success, of course it is not unrelated to the cultivation method."

"But more importantly... it is the power of love!"

"...What?"

The women looked at each other in dismay. Every single one of them completely bewildered.

Julian kept a straight face, utterly earnest.

"Did you all not know?"

"Whoever obtains first place in this Examination Assembly, the Saint Lord will not only bestow the Seven Orifices Divine Heart upon him, but will also grant him a single wish."

"Therefore, I am completely fueled by this! It is motivating me to constantly break through!"

"As long as I get first place this time, I will request the Saint Lord to accept Isolde back into the family!"

"Then, I will completely give the Seven Orifices Divine Heart back to her."

"Isolde has suffered so much hardship on the outside. If she returns to the family and receives such warm treatment, she will definitely be so grateful that she’ll pledge her life to me!"

"From then on, Isolde and I will become an immortal couple whose beautiful name will spread throughout the entire Heavenly Flame Dynasty!"

The women: "..."

"Tsk, how boring."

"Let’s disperse, let’s disperse."

This round of self-moving, simp remarks from Julian directly repelled every last fangirl who worshipped him. But he himself remained completely oblivious. Still immersed in his blissful fantasies.

"Isolde, are you cold right now? Are you hungry? Is anyone bullying you?"

"Even if someone is bullying you, it’s okay. I will soon get first place in the Examination Assembly."

"Then I will personally go and bring you back to the Hale family. Once we return, we can stay together every single day..."

...

The crowd gathered in groups of three or five, talking and laughing cheerfully.

A moment later, Alex emerged from within the Saint Lord’s Hall and walked before the crowd.

Everyone instantly stopped their clamor, uniformly dropping to one knee in absolute reverence, and shouted loudly, "We pay our respects to the Saint Lord!"
