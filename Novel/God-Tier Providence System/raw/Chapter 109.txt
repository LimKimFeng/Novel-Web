Chapter 109

It would seek out the successor predestined by Master Solarius in the unseen world, recognizing them as its brand-new master.

The Vault of Divine Blade Heart was a fortuitous encounter left behind by an Saint Realm mighty figure.

The sword intent and power it harbored naturally needed no questioning.

As long as someone obtained it, regardless of whether their cultivation was high or low, or their abilities strong or weak, even a three-year-old toddler who had never touched a sword could instantly become the premier Sword Dao Grandmaster on the Azure Origin Continent.

"Saint Lord, this is absolutely an earth-shattering major event for the entire Heavenly Flame Dynasty!" Tobias said excitedly.

"With the Vault of Divine Blade Heart appearing, all the sword cultivators in the Dynasty will inevitably drool over it and fight for it."

"Our Hale family absolutely cannot be willing to fall behind others!"

However, after his brief initial surprise, Alex appeared to have calmed down completely. He smiled faintly. "Tobias, do not be so vulgar."

"Since everyone else will go and fight over this Vault of Divine Blade Heart, why must our Hale family also go and fight them until our heads are broken and bleeding?"

"This..." Tobias looked doubtful.

"Could it be that the Saint Lord is willing to just watch helplessly as this Vault of Divine Blade Heart is handed over to others?"

"No." Alex said profoundly.

"What I fight for, others will definitely fight for. If everyone fights, one might not obtain it."

"What I yield, others will definitely yield. If everyone yields, one might not lose it."

"If we absolutely insist on risking our lives to fight for it, there will inevitably be casualties. We will sour our relationships with other factions, yet still might not even obtain it in the end."

"Rather than doing that, it is better to step out of the affair. Sit back and watch their success or failure. We will see the result of their struggle before making our move."

Hearing these words, everyone present couldn’t help but nod in deep agreement.

The Saint Lord is truly worthy of being the Saint Lord! Even upon hearing such an earth-shattering major event, he can remain so calm, collected, and unhurried.

This kind of mentality, this kind of shrewd depth... they fear they wouldn’t be able to learn even a fraction of it in their entire lifetimes.

Alex smiled faintly. "Adrian, if my prediction is correct, the Heavenly Flame Empress’s side should have already made a move, right?"

"Uh... there haven’t been any major moves, actually." Adrian replied.

"It’s just that the Heavenly Flame Empress sent that Knox to the Divine Blade Sect to pratice."

"Furthermore, she personally wrote a handwritten letter, instructing Knox to hand it to Master Vincent after arriving at the Divine Blade Sect."

"She also said that she and Master Vincent once had a friendship. As long as he delivers this letter, Master Vincent will absolutely accept Knox as his personal disciple."

"...What the hell did you just say?" Alex was stunned.

"You’re saying... Knox went to the Divine Blade Sect?!"

"Yeah." Adrian nodded.

"When did he go?!"

"He just set off this morning..."

"Fuck me!" Alex’s expression changed drastically, and he cried out in alarm.

"Why the fuck didn’t you say so earlier?!"

"That brat didn’t obediently stay in the Heavenly Flame Capital and actually went to the Divine Blade Sect?! This is an absolute disaster!"

"Tobias, Lara! For the Dao Vindication Assembly in a few days, the two of you will lead the clan’s young disciples to the Heavenly Flame Capital to participate."

"I am going to the Divine Blade Sect right now! If I do not snatch the Vault of Divine Blade Heart into my hands, I swear I will not return!"

Saying this, Alex directly summoned his auspicious cloud and flew off. Not lingering for a single moment.

Leaving Adrian, Tobias, Lara, and the crowd of juniors completely dumbfounded.

Wait... Saint Lord, what happened to your restraint and demeanor from just a moment ago?!

What happened to What I fight for, others will fight... What I yield, others will yield?!

With this frantic, fire-burning-his-ass posture, anyone who didn’t know better would think he was rushing to the cafeteria before the food ran out...

"Speaking of which... exactly what kind of deep grudge is there between the Saint Lord and that Knox?" Lucas couldn’t help but mutter softly.

"Just now, upon hearing the Vault of Divine Blade Heart was appearing, the Saint Lord was so calm and collected."

"Why is it that the moment he heard the name Knox, he completely lost his composure and headed straight for the Divine Blade Sect?!"

The crowd all shook their heads. Completely in the dark.

...

Naturally, they couldn’t possibly know what kind of turbulent, roller-coaster psychological activity Alex’s heart had experienced after hearing Adrian’s intelligence.

At first, upon hearing the Vault of Divine Blade Heart was about to appear, Alex was extremely delighted.

In the original novel, because of his master Iris, Knox practiced the sword at the Divine Blade Sect for half a year. It was exactly during this half-year that the Vault appeared. And under the absolute power of the Protagonist Halo, it selected him as its master.

It allowed him to obtain the Mystic Yellow Nine Swords Bloodline. Master the Emperor-tier sword art Nine Stars Divine Sword. And become the unparalleled Number One Sword Cultivator of the current era.

But now, thanks to his own hard work, Knox and Iris’s relationship was already ruined. Zero master-disciple affection.

Knox also wouldn’t be practicing the sword at the Divine Blade Sect. Therefore, this Vault of Divine Blade Heart definitely shouldn’t have half a cent of a relationship with him.

But who could have imagined that this brat’s Protagonist Halo was ridiculous to this degree?

He had exhausted his mind and thoroughly ruined his relationship with Iris. As a result, Aurelia actually wrote a damn letter to let Knox take Vincent as his master.

Vincent was the head of the Ten Elders of the Divine Blade Sect. His status vastly surpassed that of a mere Saintess like Iris.

If Knox truly took Vincent as his master, the consequences would be absolutely fucking unimaginable.

Regarding the sheer power of his Protagonist Halo, Alex never harbored the slightest doubt. His Protagonist Halo could allow him to lose one master, only to gain a completely new, even better master.
