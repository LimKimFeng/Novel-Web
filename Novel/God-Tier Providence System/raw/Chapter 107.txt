Chapter 107

"Especially if consumed at the peak of the Supreme Realm, it is highly likely to shatter the realm barrier and directly step into the Saint Realm!"

Saint.

Hearing these words, all the disciples instantly revealed faces full of intense yearning.

In the current world, news of a Saint hadn’t been heard for hundreds of years.

The Heavenly Flame Empress, at the peak of the Supreme Realm, was already the supreme powerhouse of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty.

If their Hale family could break the status quo where Supreme ruled the world, and take the lead in having a Saint to hold down the fort... they would inevitably become the absolute premier Saint Lineage in the entire Heavenly Flame Dynasty.

Even Aurelia would have to give them a significant amount of face.

"Lara, you’ve worked hard." Alex accepted the Unregretful Pill and said loudly, "My junior disciples, regarding today’s Examination Assembly, there shouldn’t be any doubts left, should there?"

"At this time last month, the Fourth Elder Lara’s cultivation was merely at the fifth level of the Mahayana Realm."

"In the short span of a single month, she crossed the realm barrier in one fell swoop. Breaking through to the second level of the Supreme Realm. Becoming the second Supreme powerhouse of the Main Family besides me."

"The champion of this Examination Assembly absolutely belongs to none other than Lara!"

The crowd nodded heavily in succession. Completely convinced.

Including Tobias, who didn’t have the slightest bit of dissatisfaction right now. He could only smile bitterly and applaud.

He originally thought that his breakthrough to the peak of Mahayana could already be called a miracle and securely bag the champion title. But who could have imagined that Sister Lara would completely out-compete him?

However, as the Third Elder of the Hale family, Tobias valued the overall situation of the family far more than his own personal glory.

The Main Family gaining a second Supreme Realm powerhouse to hold down the fort was a profoundly significant and excellent thing.

Therefore, compared to regret, Tobias felt far more joy.

The disciples were also full of admiration, giving Lara the most enthusiastic applause.

Within the crowd, only Julian remained with a bitter face. Doubting his entire life.

Why is my fate so bitter?!

Last time, he exhausted his mind and body to finally break through from Body Tempering to Foundation Establishment. Geniuses like Lucas and Joanna were left far behind by him.

In the end, Elara appeared out of nowhere, breaking through from Body Tempering to Core Formation in a single day.

With a crushing posture, she mercilessly snatched away his champion title.

This time, he used everything to break through from Foundation Establishment to Core Formation. It happened to coincide with his two formidable rivals, Elara and Joanna, being absent.

The absolute perfect opportunity.

But who could have expected that first the Third Elder would crash the party halfway, and then the Fourth Elder would descend from the heavens?

One breaking through to the peak of Mahayana was already enough to stun the entire audience.

The other actually directly stepping into the Supreme Realm was enough to shock the entire family.

You two highly respected senior elders, bullying a junior like me... is that interesting to you?!

...

At this moment, Alex unhurriedly waved his hand, taking out a wooden box from his space ring.

"Lara, in this second Examination Assembly, your performance was the most outstanding." Alex smiled faintly.

"According to the prior agreement, this Seven Orifices Divine Heart absolutely belongs to you!"

Hearing these words, the crowd’s faces instantly revealed shock and envy.

The Seven Orifices Divine Heart was the most renowned treasure in the entire Hale family. Its power could rival a Supreme-tier supreme treasure.

That was why last month, when Alex announced the reward for the second Examination Assembly was the Seven Orifices Divine Heart, it caused a massive sensation. It drove the disciples to cultivate painstakingly day and night, forgetting to eat and sleep for an entire month.

Although the Fourth Elder had already reached the Supreme Realm and could no longer directly use the power of the Seven Orifices Divine Heart to elevate her realm, possessing it would absolutely have an incomparably massive impact on her cultivation and comprehension.

As the youngest of the Four Great Elders, Lara was the first to break through to the Supreme Realm.

Now that she had obtained the Seven Orifices Divine Heart, it was like adding wings to a tiger. Her future attainments would simply be immeasurable.

"Saint Lord, I do not want it." Lara shook her head and said earnestly.

"That I could break through to the Supreme Realm is entirely thanks to the Saint Lord’s encouragement and guidance."

"Furthermore, I only wish to refine pills well for the Saint Lord and the family. What use do I have for this treasure?"

"In this subordinate’s opinion, this Seven Orifices Divine Heart provides a much greater benefit to the juniors with superior natural talent. It should be left for them."

"Lara, those words are incorrect." Alex kept a straight face and said sternly.

"A word once spoken cannot be chased down."

"Since I announced back then that the Seven Orifices Divine Heart would be gifted to the champion of this second Examination Assembly, I must absolutely do as I said!"

"Right now, you have seized the crown in one fell swoop with the outstanding achievement of breaking through to the Supreme Realm. You fully deserve it, yet you refuse to accept this reward."

"Could it be... you want me to be ridiculed by the family juniors and become a faithless, unjust person?"

"This subordinate wouldn’t dare." Lara’s expression changed. She hurriedly accepted the wooden box with both hands, saying solemnly, "Since that is the case, I shall temporarily accept this Seven Orifices Divine Heart and keep it safe on your behalf."

"If the Saint Lord wishes to take back this treasure in the future, this subordinate will return it at any time!"

"Heh, good, good." Alex nodded cheerfully and helped Lara up.

In his heart, he naturally had his own calculations.

The Seven Orifices Divine Heart could allow a cultivator below the Nascent Soul Realm to directly elevate by three major realms.

Therefore, giving it to a junior at the Nascent Soul Realm offered the highest cost-to-performance ratio. Leaving it in the hands of Lara, a Supreme Realm expert, somewhat failed to make the best use of it.
