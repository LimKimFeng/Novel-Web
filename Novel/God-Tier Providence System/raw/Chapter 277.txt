Chapter 277

The Four-Phased Demonic Wolf let out a howl.

Within its cry, one could actually clearly hear the four emotions coexisting. Alex was secretly amazed in his heart.

Any rare and exotic beast could be found and summoned from the Animal Path.

This Four-Phased Demonic Wolf was a Supreme Realm Eighth Level Soul Beast recorded in the Animal Path.

Although its disposition was rather evil, it wouldn’t bite its master.

Normally, it frequently used its four bloody maws to devour people with different emotions.

For example, the Joy-Devouring Demonic Wolf specialized in devouring those who had encountered happy events and were in high spirits.

The Anger-Devouring Demonic Wolf specialized in devouring those cowardly people who suffered beatings but didn’t dare to fight back.

At this very moment, using it to deal with emotionless Giant Demons was slightly inappropriate. However, as a Soul Beast that Alex had only recently learned to summon, he naturally couldn’t wait to bring it out and show it off a bit.

The Four-Phased Demonic Wolf stood towering behind Alex, making him appear incredibly petite in comparison.

Watching the Giant Demons slowly approaching with cold, apathetic strides, Alex leapt up from the wolf’s back, vaulting into mid-air with a resounding shout.

"Four-Phased Wolf God Lock!"

The wolf’s four heads, and the corresponding necks for each head, instantly elongated and stretched out several meters long.

Watching the Four-Phased Demonic Wolf involuntarily stretch itself directly into a "Four-Phased Giraffe," Alex couldn’t help but laugh involuntarily.

It was clearly such an imposing and domineering wild beast, yet casting a technique made it look so comical.

Although the power of this move could be considered quite extraordinary, this pre-cast animation was truly a bit too abstract.

Each of the wolf’s heads opened their bloody maws and viciously bit down on the head of one of the Giant Demons.

After biting the Giant Demons’ heads, the wolf heads didn’t directly exert force to tear them off. Because even if they were torn off, the Giant Demons would immediately resurrect and grow new ones.

Therefore, the Four-Phased Demonic Wolf’s four bloody maws merely clamped down on the four Giant Demons’ heads.

Subsequently, from their blood-red eyes, seething blood mist emerged, condensing into blood-red locks in the shape of wolf heads.

The Giant Demons used their cushion-sized claws to tear desperately, wanting to throw off the blood locks on their heads.

But under the shackles of the Four-Phased Wolf God Lock, they were all frozen in place, completely unable to advance a single inch.

Just as Alex held his breath and concentrated, circulating his spiritual energy to fully confine the movements of the four Giant Demons, the remaining fifth Giant Demon let out a hiss and roared, pouncing toward him with menacing and aggressive momentum.

Seeing that Alex couldn’t extricate himself to deal with this Giant Demon, Pearl on his shoulder resolutely stepped forward.

It directly leaped up, giantifying in mid-air and transforming into a massive white cat several meters long.

A fierce yet undeniably cute meow-meow punch directly staggered the Giant Demon, seemingly leaving it somewhat dazed.

Although Pearl’s physical size was on par with the Giant Demon, it ultimately only had cultivation at the Qi gathering realm.

To use such meager cultivation to resist a Supreme Realm Giant Demon at this moment, this courage was truly admirable. It also fully proved just how loyal Pearl was to Alex.

"Pearl, good job!"

Under the power of the Four-Phased Wolf God Lock, combined with Pearl’s heroic performance of desperately clashing head-on with one Giant Demon, all five Giant Demons were simultaneously restricted.

Alex extricated himself, summoned the Rakshasa Demon Umbrella from his storage ring, and teleported, charging toward the Nether Earth Ghost King.

The Six Paths Wheel of Destruction Technique, as a divine-tier cultivation method, was almost perfect.

But its only weakness was that while executing one path, it was impossible to simultaneously use another path.

Right now, using the Animal Path to summon the Four-Phased Demonic Wolf meant he could no longer use the Asura Path to summon the Vault of Divine World-Destroying Sword to deal with the Nether Earth Ghost King.

However, this minor flaw didn’t obscure the jade’s brilliance, he had plenty of trump cards regardless.

A look of horror flowed through the Nether Earth Ghost King’s eyes. His fingers trembled slightly, desperately chanting incantations to command the Giant Demons to break free and protect him.

But in just a single instant, Alex had already teleported right in front of him.

The Rakshasa Demon Umbrella completely opened, and from the exact center of the umbrella ribs, a profound purple dot of light condensed.

"Rakshasa Soul Break!"

Accompanied by a massive boom, the dot of light rapidly transformed into a light beam and shot out, piercing straight through the Nether Earth Ghost King’s chest, subsequently triggering a violent explosion.

Majestic purple mist howled and surged, simultaneously carrying the miserable shrill screams and furious roars of the Nether Earth Ghost King.

The Profound Nether Cave, which had just returned to silence, trembled violently once again.

The purple mist gradually scattered.

On the Nether Earth Ghost King’s chest, there was shockingly a hollow, empty bloody hole.

Within this Profound Nether Cave, no matter what injuries the Nether Earth Ghost King suffered, he could always heal and replenish himself using the yin qi in the cave.

But at this very moment, the bloody hole in his chest continuously poured out black mist, yet was already unable to self-heal.

Because just now, the Nether Earth Ghost King was forced to consume all the ghost qi within the Profound Nether Cave to defend against Alex’s Vault of Divine Annihilation.

Right now, he had already lost his few remaining geographical advantages.

"I truly didn’t expect that after tens of thousands of years without meeting a rival, this King would actually be forced into a desperate situation by a human brat today." The Nether Earth Ghost King panted heavily and weakly, saying hoarsely.

"Saint Lord of the Hale family, Alex, this King has remembered your name."

"Next time, I will definitely make foolproof preparations and, at all costs, fight you to the death, never resting until one of us is dead!"
