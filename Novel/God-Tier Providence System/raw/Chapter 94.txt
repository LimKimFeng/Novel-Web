Chapter 94

The crowd was stunned hearing this.

A Great Demon at the fifth level of the Supreme Realm was truly extremely rare.

Oscar asked cautiously. "Saint Lord, dare I ask what this Great Demon looks like?"

"This Great Demon looks exactly like a human. Furthermore, she takes the form of a peerless beauty, as gorgeous as a flower."

Alex said this with a completely straight face.

"However, although she can disguise her outward appearance, she cannot hide her vicious, brutal true nature."

"This Great Demon will most likely appear at night. Her eyes are blood-red, and her mind is deranged."

"You can tell at a single glance that she is abnormal."

"Most importantly, she constantly mutters to herself, endlessly repeating the words Purge the demons. This is her greatest defining feature."

The four of them nodded slightly. Deep in thought. They all secretly memorized the monster’s characteristics, preparing to conduct a thorough search.

"My four virtuous younger brothers and sister, I entrust the mission of capturing this Great Demon to you." Alex sighed and clasped his fists.

"Whoever can help me resolve this matter, I will absolutely never forget their merit."

"Very well! Saint Lord, set your mind at ease. Leave it to us."

Martin patted his chest, fully agreeing with a face full of confidence. Then, suddenly thinking of something, he asked cautiously.

"Right, Saint Lord, there is one more thing. Regarding this year’s Dao Vindication Assembly, what are your thoughts..."

"There’s no rush for that."

Alex waved his hand and smiled faintly.

"As for who is the most fair and reasonable choice to attend the Dao Vindication Assembly, I am still deliberating and considering."

"How about this. Once you help me capture that Great Demon and bring her back, I will naturally give you an answer."

...

Hearing these words, the four of them pondered carefully. Finally, a clear idea formed in their hearts.

The Dao Vindication Assembly was one of the most important grand events for Saint lineages like theirs. Every year, during the depths of winter, the major Saint lineages would gather in the Heavenly Flame Capital to spar, compete, and determine who was the strongest.

The Hale family’s rule was that the Main Family would lead one of the branch families, selecting the most outstanding young talents to attend together.

Originally, for this kind of thankless, exhausting chore, the branch families basically pushed it away whenever they could. Acting very unenthusiastically.

However, this year was uniquely different. Rumors had long been spreading that the Heavenly Flame Empress, Aurelia, had prepared a special gift for the number one family of this year’s Dao Vindication Assembly.

Whoever obtained this special gift would be able to grasp a formidable power capable of determining the very destiny and luck of the Heavenly Flame Dynasty.

The Hale family, as the premier Saint lineage, was one of the strongest candidates to seize the crown. Naturally, the major branch families all wanted to fight for the chance to go. Hoping to shamelessly grab a piece of the pie after the Main Family obtained that special gift.

Now, since the Saint Lord put it this way... his meaning was clear. Whoever catches the Great Demon gets to attend the Dao Vindication Assembly.

Truly fair and reasonable.

The four branch family heads were all fired up. Secretly rubbing their fists and wiping their palms.

They bid farewells to Alex one after another and immediately returned to prepare for demon hunting.

...

After all four had left, Cedric said admiringly.

"Saint Lord, this subordinate truly admires your wisdom to the point of throwing myself on the ground in worship!"

"In just a few short words, not only did you refute the unreasonable demands of the four major branch families, but you completely changed the long-standing rules. Ensuring they can never suck our Main Family’s blood again."

"From this day forward, without these blood-sucking parasites, we can loosen our bindings and go all out to do something great!"

Alex nodded and smiled faintly.

"That is natural."

"Not only will I prevent them from sucking our blood in the future..."

"I will also make them compensate and bleed back all the blood they have sucked from us over the years."

Cedric nodded heavily. No matter what Alex said, he firmly believed it without a shadow of doubt.

Off to the side, Adrian asked weakly.

"But, Saint Lord..."

"When did a blood-drinking, flesh-eating monster disguised as a beauty infiltrate our Main Family?"

"And killing dozens of young disciples... that clearly never happened."

"No. If I say it happened, it naturally happened."

Alex stroked his chin, smiling confidently.

"It’s just that right now, she is still on her way and hasn’t arrived yet."

"Tonight, I believe she will definitely arrive."

...

Late at night. The bright moon hung high in the sky.

Two agile figures sneaked into the Hale family’s holy land.

Knox and Nyx.

Knox had been to the Hale family’s holy land many times. He knew the terrain like the back of his hand, moving with the ease of a veteran driving a familiar cart.

He led Nyx down the most secretive hidden path. Bypassing the eyes of all the guards.

They successfully infiltrated the holy land and stood hidden within a patch of shrubbery.

"Phew, we finally managed to sneak in."

Knox pulled down the mask on his face and whispered.

"Princess Flower, you’re good, right?"

"The Hale family has numerous experts, including the Four Great Elders who are all at the Mahayana Realm."

"Although you possess profound cultivation, Princess Flower, dealing with their entire family will ultimately be somewhat difficult."

"Therefore, we must find Alex directly. Finish him off and retreat immediately. We absolutely must not linger and get bogged down in a drawn-out battle. Understand?"

Clearly, Nyx couldn’t understand a single word Knox was saying.

Her nose twitched a few times. As if she had caught the scent of demonic arts.

Her eyes instantly burst with a blood-red light. A drop of crystal-clear saliva slipped from the corner of her mouth.

She broke free from Knox’s restraint and charged out recklessly.

Sprinting at full speed with her legs pumping wildly, she let out an excited roar. Charging into the depths of the night directly toward Alex’s Saint Lord’s Hall.

"Purge the demons!"

"Purge the demons!"

Watching Nyx’s figure rush out like an evil spirit, a cold, ferocious sneer curled at the corners of Knox’s mouth.
