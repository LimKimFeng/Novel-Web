Chapter 56

Their faces glowed with immense pride and joy. Then, under the envious gazes of the crowd, they stepped back and returned to their ranks.

Next, Alex called forward three more groups of juniors who had overachieved to varying degrees. He offered words of praise for their achievements to show his approval.

Every single one of the over a thousand Hale family disciples had broken through at least one minor realm.

The entire assembly was filled with a joyous, celebratory atmosphere. Not a single person was punished.

When it was Oria and Qira’s turn, they had originally wanted to bring up yesterday’s bet. However, seeing that the Saint Lord had no intention of mentioning it, and noticing that Elara was nowhere to be found, they didn’t dare speak up in public.

They planned to wait until the assembly concluded to remind the Saint Lord.

After all, the temptation of a full year’s worth of resources was incredibly strong.

Once all the disciples had been inspected, Alex nodded in satisfaction.

"I truly did not expect that all of you juniors would achieve such brilliant results."

"The heavens bless the everlasting foundation of our Hale family. The descendants and future generations are all dragons and phoenixes!"

"According to our previous agreement, I have prepared generous gifts for every single one of you."

"Among you, for the junior who achieved the absolute greatest results..."

"I have decided to grant him one request."

"No matter what the request is, as long as I can accomplish it, I will definitely help him realize it!"

"In addition to this, I have also prepared an Upper-Grade Heaven-tier spiritual artifact for him."

"Upper-Grade Heaven-tier!"

The entire crowd was utterly shocked.

They all instinctively looked toward the youth standing at the very front. Their faces displayed deep envy.

For juniors like them, possessing even a Profound-tier spiritual artifact was considered incredibly rare. An Upper-Grade Heaven-tier spiritual artifact? They wouldn’t even dare to dream of such a thing.

Not to mention the unconditional request.

As a mighty figure in the Supreme Realm, the Saint Lord was practically omnipotent in their eyes. The weight and value of a promise from him went without saying.

Standing at the very front, Lucas’s face lit up with absolute joy. His month of blood, sweat, and tears had finally paid off.

As the son of First Elder Cedric, Lucas’s foresight far exceeded that of ordinary people.

He understood perfectly well that what he was gaining was far more than just a Heaven-tier spiritual artifact.

He was gaining the absolute trust and high regard of the Saint Lord.

From this day forward, he would be treated as the most outstanding junior in the Hale family. He might even completely replace Isolde’s former position, receiving a massive influx of grooming and resources.

"Thank you, Saint Lord!" Lucas’s smile was overflowing as he excitedly stepped forward.

However, just as he was about to reach Alex, a burst of hearty, uninhibited laughter suddenly echoed from the distance.

"Hahahaha! Brother Lucas, step back, step back!"

"The most outstanding junior isn’t you!"

The crowd instinctively raised their heads, looking around in confusion.

The next second, they saw it.

A giant Mystic Owl constructed entirely of flowing light. Flapping massive wings that blotted out the sun and clouds, it soared in from the distance. Standing proudly on its back was a youth dressed in white.

Julian.

"That..."

"That’s Julian?!"

"My god!"

The Hale family crowd froze. Jaws dropped in dumbfounded shock. Upon recognizing the figure in mid-air, every single one of them felt a wave of unbelievable astonishment.

"What... what a terrifying aura!"

"But Julian was clearly only at the seventh level of the Body Tempering Realm!"

"It’s only been a short month, how is it possible..."

Lucas could also clearly sense that the aura radiating from Julian was vastly stronger than his own. His expression turned incredibly complicated, though unwilling to accept it, he was completely helpless to deny it.

Flying directly above the Saint Lord’s manor, Julian dismissed the giant Mystic Owl construct and descended from the sky. He landed firmly in front of Alex and declared in a resonant voice:

"Greetings, Saint Lord!"

"This junior Julian arrived a step late. I ask the Saint Lord to forgive my offense!"

"Mm, I have been waiting for you for a long time." Alex nodded and smiled faintly.

"Julian, show everyone. Over this past month, what are your results?"

"Yes!" Julian turned to face the crowd and declared loudly.

"Brothers and sisters, forgive my presumption!"

"Over this past month, I, Julian, have broken through from the seventh level of the Body Tempering Realm all the way to the fourth level of the Foundation Establishment Realm!"

"I have cultivated the Treatise of the Mystic Owl and Fallen Phoenix to the initiate stage and can now manifest the feral energy to summon the Mystic Owl."

"Today, I shall gladly accept the title of the junior with the greatest achievements! Hahahaha!"

Listening to Julian’s incredibly smug laughter, the crowd felt a bit displeased.

But even if they were displeased, they couldn’t say anything.

Julian’s outstanding performance truly left everyone convinced and utterly speechless.

A month ago, this brat was merely at the seventh level of the Body Tempering Realm. Among all the young disciples in the entire Hale family, he was part of the absolute weakest batch.

Yet in just a short month’s time, he had broken through a staggering two major realms.

A cultivation base at the fourth level of the Foundation Establishment Realm was enough to proudly look down upon everyone present.

One had to remember, back when he chose a Heaven-tier cultivation method with his frail cultivation base, who among the crowd hadn’t mocked him? Arrogant, biting off more than he could chew, a snake trying to swallow an elephant.

Yet in the end, Julian had actually pulled it off.

He used a single month to prove his aptitude and strength.

He proved that he possessed the qualifications to become the heaven-chosen genius the Hale family would pour its utmost resources into grooming.

"Saint Lord, may I ask what generous gift you have prepared for the junior with the greatest achievements?" Julian asked excitedly.

"This junior can hardly wait to see it with his own eyes!"
