Chapter 218

It wasn’t in vain that Pearl had eaten so much along the way, devouring the corpses and blood essence of over thirty fierce beasts.

To think it actually smoothly broke through to the Qi Gathering Realm at this critical, life-and-death moment. Simply timely rain in a drought.

The Sword God Restriction of the Azure Dragon Valley only targeted humans.

It was completely ineffective against spirit beasts. Pearl, having broken through to the Qi Gathering Realm, was enough to carry him and sweep through the entire Azure Dragon Valley, remaining invincible throughout.

Blair opposite them clearly hadn’t realized the severity of the situation, still wearing a face full of contempt, he sneered coldly.

"Heh, little kitten, do you still not know pain?"

"Then I will cripple you with a single sword strike and let you know my power!"

Blair leaped up, striding forward and hacking the Thundering Sword straight at Pearl.

Alex’s mind moved, he shouted deeply. "Pearl, Gigantification!"

"Meow!" Pearl was enveloped in a dazzling white light, bursting forth with pure spiritual energy.

Blair had just rushed close when he was blasted in the face by this spiritual energy, directly knocked flying several meters away.

"Bastard beast, I’ll butcher you!" Blair flew into a rage out of humiliation, jumping up with his sword in hand.

However, as soon as he got up and looked, he was instantly dumbfounded.

In the short span of two or three seconds, the white light dissipated completely.

Pearl’s size skyrocketed hundreds of times from its original petite, exquisite form.

It directly transformed into a giant white cat, filling the entire deep pit to the brim.

The corners of its mouth curled up slightly, eevealing fangs as long and sharp as elephant tusks.

The oppressive aura radiating from this giant white cat made Blair feel bursts of suffocation, almost making him piss his pants.

Alex unhurriedly leaped up onto Pearl’s back.

"Weren’t you looking down on my Pearl just now?"

"Now, what do you have to say?"

"...Heh, just because it got a little bigger, does that mean it’s definitely formidable?" Blair still had a stiff mouth, gritting his teeth.

"I don’t care what kind of monster you are, eat my sword first!"

"Die!"

Facing Blair’s murderous, fully-charged strike... Alex merely smiled faintly, not having the slightest worry for Pearl.

The Phantom Heaven Nine-Lives Cat’s nine divine abilities were randomly awakened from exotic beasts throughout history.

Gigantification was a divine ability unique to the Evil Spider Clan among the ancient demon races.

The Evil Spider Clan’s Gigantification wasn’t as simple as merely making one’s body larger. While body size skyrocketed, attack power, defense, and destructive power would all skyrocket exponentially.

Therefore, although Pearl’s cultivation had just broken through to the Qi Gathering Realm... its physical brute force had already reached the level of a Foundation Establishment Realm beast.

Dealing with a mere Ninth Level of Body Tempering was as easy as flipping a hand.

Blair roared angrily, forcefully blasting out a sword glare flashing with lightning.

But his all-out attack hit Pearl’s fur and was like a drop of water entering the sea, failing to cause even the slightest damage.

"What?!" Blair’s face abruptly changed, gulping down a mouthful of saliva.

Pearl let out a massive roar, directly raised its front paw and slammed it down.

This paw strike was like Mount Tai pressing down upon one’s head, full of oppressive force, making it fundamentally impossible to dodge.

Blair was completely powerless to parry, like a tiny mouse, he was viciously swatted flat onto the ground by Pearl.

"Ah!" Accompanied by a shrill, miserable scream, the sound passed through the Divine Secret Sphere and reached the ears of the elders.

Torin’s face turned ashen green, he recognized this familiar voice.

The next second, they saw Pearl leap up, carrying Alex out of the deep pit, strutting away leisurely into the distance.

Torin, Lorin, Blake, and Nerin all fell into deathly silence.

Only Sylra couldn’t help but clap his hands and laugh loudly, dancing with joy.

"Hahaha, brat, well done, exceptionally well done!"

"This old man knew it, you brat absolutely wouldn’t have a problem!"

"You were just acting like snobbish dogs looking down on people. Now you know how formidable he is, right?"

"Five Heaven and Earth-tier weapons and ten Heaven and Earth-tier pills from each of you. Not a single one can be missing!"

The several of them looked at each other, instantly feeling despair. Their faces looking like they wanted to cry but had no tears.

However, no matter how unwilling they were, they had nothing to say.

There was no helping it. Who could have expected that a mere First Level of Body Tempering would actually defeat a Ninth Level?

"Wait..." Torin’s face suddenly changed.

"Why hasn’t Blair come out yet?"

Aerin frowned slightly and gave an instruction into his communication jade slip.

A few minutes later, two disciples responsible for supervising order appeared on the light curtain.

They jumped into the dirt pit. After half a minute, they supported Blair and brought him out.

No, to be precise, they carried him out.

How could a single word like miserable suffice to describe Blair’s appearance? He was dripping with fresh blood from head to toe. Who knows how many bones had been shattered, looking just like a puddle of mud.

The two disciples had to use a large wooden board, placing him on it to barely manage to carry him out. Whether he could even survive was a question.

As for his entire body’s cultivation, it was definitely completely crippled.

Torin was so angry his face was gloomy as still water, his entire body trembling violently.

He immediately strode over to Aerin, gritting his teeth.

"Senior Brother Aerin!"

"That Taryn struck so viciously and ruthlessly, beating my disciple Blair to the point of severe injury!"

Without waiting for Aerin to speak, Sylra stepped forward and sneered coldly.

"Torin, do not be so overly protective of your dog."

"Besides, you can’t entirely blame my disciple for this."

"It was your disciple Blair who proactively dug and set up a trap, causing my disciple to fall into it and delaying his journey."
