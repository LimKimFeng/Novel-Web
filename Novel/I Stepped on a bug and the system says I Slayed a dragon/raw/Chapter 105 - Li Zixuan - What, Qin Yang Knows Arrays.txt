Chapter 105: Li Zixuan: What, Qin Yang Knows Arrays?

Jianghai Library, Area 1.

Several teams of fully-equipped security guards walked past the bookshelves, checking every corner of the library along their established patrol routes.

Qin Yang pushed a book cart, leisurely passing behind the security team. He watched their movements, feeling a bit helpless.

After the commotion Li Qinghe caused last night, the entire library was on high alert.

Even his usual spots for slacking off had been visited by the Steward several times.

’Looks like this level of mobilization won’t die down for at least a week.’

’I guess I’ll have to use my backup spot for now...’

Qin Yang sighed, pushed his cart to the library’s smoking area, and skillfully slipped inside, ready to start slacking off.

But the smoking area was too common a slacking spot. Its location wasn’t advantageous, making it a key place for the Steward to check.

’I’ll only have a few minutes at most.’

With this in mind, Qin Yang considered the time he had and decided to seize the opportunity, starting with the Big Dipper Array Map.

The sound of running water came from outside the stall, and people occasionally passed by. Qin Yang lit a cigarette, keeping track of the time, then closed his eyes and sank into the Enlightenment Space.

The next moment, seven Morning Stars rose slowly in his mind.

The stars shifted and turned, simulating the changes of the sky from day to night. They traced splendid trajectories, forming various Magic Array Patterns and weaving together magnificent knowledge of Arrays.

The Big Dipper Array Map was unconventional and vastly different from the ordinary Array Dao.

It was magnificent and vast, using the stars in the sky as its Array lines.

Its grand scale alone was far beyond what arrays based on mountains, rivers, and feng shui could ever compare to.

’Not bad, not bad at all.’

In just a few short minutes, a vast sea of knowledge washed over him like a divine blessing, endlessly pouring into his mind and rapidly advancing his attainments in the Array Dao.

A moment later.

The cigarette burned down to the butt, and wisps of white smoke curled upward.

Qin Yang slowly opened his eyes, satisfied as he stopped comprehending the Array Map.

’A little bit of progress whenever I have a spare moment. That’s enough for today.’

Thinking this, Qin Yang crushed the cigarette butt and headed toward Area 1.

But as he passed through Area 2, he suddenly noticed the space between the bookshelves was empty. A certain someone was nowhere to be seen.

Li Qinghe was hiding somewhere.

’That’s strange...’

Seeing this, Qin Yang’s curiosity was piqued.

’Someone as hardworking as her is slacking off now?’

’Something’s up!’

With that thought, Qin Yang enveloped the floor with his Spiritual Power and instantly locked onto her position.

She was in a remote corner of Area 2, faintly radiating excitement and giggling to herself about something.

’Tsk, why have fun all by yourself?’

Seeing this, Qin Yang smiled.

He immediately started walking toward her.

...........

Jianghai Library, Area 2. A corner.

Hidden among the bookshelves, Li Qinghe held a Star Stone in one hand, using a Perception Array to keep watch. With her other hand, she excitedly studied the Ten Thousand Array Map.

"So that’s how it is," she murmured, her eyes fixed on the patterns on the map.

The Ten Thousand Array Map was filled with countless Array Patterns, containing multiple Fifth Level Arrays that were interwoven, overlapping, and compiled in a specially encrypted way.

One map contained a multitude of arrays.

Only by analyzing it with Spiritual Power could one hope to glimpse its secrets.

Now, Li Qinghe was relying on her own outstanding talent, immersing herself completely in the Array Map and benefiting greatly.

"An Illusion Array can actually be set up like this..."

Li Qinghe’s fingers gently brushed the surface of the map, her pupils trembling in astonishment.

Just as she was completely focused, a voice suddenly spoke from right behind her.

"Whatcha lookin’ at?"

Qin Yang had appeared, standing directly behind her.

The calm voice fell like a sudden clap of thunder.

"Ah!"

Li Qinghe shrieked on the spot, her heart nearly stopping. She quickly hid the Ten Thousand Array Map behind her back.

"Hiding something good?"

Qin Yang chuckled and took a step behind her. "Can I take a look?"

"Qin! None of your damn business!"

Li Qinghe glared at Qin Yang, circling around him as she quickly stored the Ten Thousand Array Map in her Star Ring. "How did you get over here?!" she demanded angrily.

"Duh, I walked, of course!"

Qin Yang shrugged and smiled nonchalantly. "Just came to catch you slacking off."

With that, seeing his objective of teasing her was accomplished, he turned and left crisply.

"You!"

Hearing this, Li Qinghe’s face flushed red. She watched Qin Yang’s retreating back, both anxious and angry, yet completely helpless against him.

’Who on earth is this guy?’

Li Qinghe wondered in shock, glancing down at the Star Stone in her hand.

’I had my Perception Array fully activated just now. Within a ten-meter radius, not even a fly could have escaped my notice.’

’But Qin Yang appeared beside me without a single sound, and I didn’t sense a thing...’

’That’s definitely not something an ordinary person could do!’

’I remember... Zixuan seems to know him?’

Li Qinghe frowned, searching her memory. She immediately took out her phone, opened her contacts, and scrolled to the family section to find a number.

’That Qin guy is too cautious, completely flawless. It seems the only way to get a lead is through Zixuan.’

...

「As evening approached.」

On the street, traffic flowed endlessly. A light drizzle fell from the sky, and the afterglow of the sunset gradually faded on the horizon.

Inside a corner café, Li Zixuan sat by the window.

She was wearing a floral dress and light makeup. Her ponytail was let down, her black hair cascading like a waterfall, giving her a serene and beautiful appearance.

Across the table, Li Qinghe had also dispelled her concealment Array, revealing her true appearance.

With messy, short hair, a sharp nose, and thin lips, she exuded a crisp, deadly air. Combined with her form-fitting white shirt, she had the distinct look of a tomboy.

"Sister Qinghe, how did you find the time to ask me out?"

Li Zixuan picked up her coffee, took a small sip, and looked at Li Qinghe. "I remember you’re usually very busy."

"What? Can’t I just come see my dear cousin?!"

Li Qinghe reached out and gently pinched Li Zixuan’s cheek. "I haven’t seen you since the last family gathering. Your cheeks have gotten even smoother."

"Stop it, Sister Qinghe."

Li Zixuan pushed her hand away and looked down, stirring her coffee. "There are a lot of people here," she said, her voice as quiet as a mosquito’s buzz. "This isn’t home..."

"Waaah, my little sister Zixuan is famous now, so she doesn’t need her big sister anymore."

Li Qinghe pulled back her hand, rubbing her eyes with her fists and pretending to cry. "Big sister is sad now."

"Stop teasing, Sister Qinghe. I’ll always care about you," Li Zixuan said. "I’ll help you with anything."

"I knew it, my little sister Zixuan is the best! You’re the one who said it."

Seeing this, Li Qinghe’s face broke into a smile. "Actually, I wanted to ask you something about a person named Qin Yang."

"Qin Yang? You mean the librarian at the Jianghai Library?"

Hearing this, Li Zixuan froze for a moment, then shook her head. "I don’t know him very well."

"Oh, is that so?"

Li Qinghe teased, "Really? I saw you go looking for him yourself. A man that our Jianghai’s goddess would personally seek out must be extraordinary, right?"

Li Zixuan thought of the character for "Qin" on the stone and shook her head. "He’s just an ordinary librarian."

"Ordinary?"

Hearing this, Li Qinghe let out a laugh and shook her head. "He’s anything but ordinary. He effortlessly broke the Array I set up. Among the younger generation in Jianghai, I’ve never met anyone like him."

"An Array?"

At these words, Li Zixuan’s pupils instantly dilated. She slammed her coffee cup down with a THUD, and the rich liquid splashed over the rim, spilling onto the table.

"You’re saying Qin Yang knows about Arrays? And he broke your Array?!"
