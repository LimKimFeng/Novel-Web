Chapter 33: Slaying the Blood Demon with One Sword! Host, This System Kneels to You

A Ziwei glow appeared on the horizon, overpowering the midday sun. The entire world dimmed, as if twilight had descended.

Grim. Deathly silent.

The Bloodthirsty Leech shuddered, its bloated, black body recoiling.

Its massive, mountain-sized body trembled uncontrollably!

’I’m going to die!’

’I’m really going to die!’

With its nascent intelligence, it could already discern various dangers and immediately perceived the terrifying nature of this aura.

It wanted to escape Li Shui Lake.

But the boundless killing intent washed over it, sealing off every escape route.

It was as if some kind of homing beacon had been planted on it.

BOOM! BOOM!

Then, a terrifying sonic boom came from the southwest, like countless lightning bolts striking at the same time.

The majestic Sword Intent whipped up the air currents, creating a furious gale and forming a tornado of astonishing power.

On the city streets, parked cars began to blare their alarms, creating a cacophony of mournful wails.

"Look! Over there! Something’s flying this way!"

The numerous Star Martial Artists on Li Shui Lake all turned and pointed to the southwest.

Hearing the shouts, Elder Li, the Commander, and the others followed their gazes.

Soon, everyone saw it clearly...

It was a longsword of exquisite design, soaring over the city. Its hilt was entwined with dragons and phoenixes, and it was cloaked in the vast radiance of the sky, as if pulling the great vault of the heavens in its wake.

In an instant, all the trees in its path were shattered, and the very air parted before it like the sea!

It streaked across the surface of Li Shui Lake, causing the water to surge backward. Terrifying waves erupted on both sides, revealing the lakebed with stark clarity.

The terrifying Sword Intent washed over them, making all the Star Martial Artists present feel as if they were suffocating.

Even though they knew it was aimed at the Bloodthirsty Leech...

Even though they knew it was an ally...

Even though they knew...

...this was the same Sword Intent that had been displayed in Sword Valley!

But the Star Martial Artists couldn’t suppress their terror!

It was an instinctual awe for a supreme expert.

"A miracle..."

Elder Li was so profoundly shaken by the sight before him that he couldn’t form a complete sentence. His aged eyes glinted with fervent reverence.

But he eventually suppressed his excitement, controlled his descent to land on the shore, and moved out of the Sword Intent’s range.

At this moment, the killing intent mixed with Sword Qi was several times more potent than the Bloodthirsty Leech’s own attacks; anyone who remained nearby risked being caught in the aftermath.

Seeing this, the other Star Martial Artists followed Elder Li’s lead. They moved away from the path between the Ziwei Divine Sword and the Blood Demon, landing on the shore to brace against the Sword Qi.

Safely out of the danger zone, Li Zixuan stood on the lakeshore and looked up at the approaching Ziwei Divine Sword.

Her eyes were sparkling with adoration.

’I can finally see my master in action with my own eyes.’

The sheer magnificence of the display was hundreds of times more dazzling than she had ever imagined.

A single sword, altering the very heavens as it tore through the sky, all just to slay a single insect.

Locked on by the overwhelming killing intent, the Bloodthirsty Leech dragged its clumsy body, struggling to dive to the lake bottom.

A flash of human-like fear flickered in its eyes.

It was as if it were saying...

’My lord, I’m just a little leech...’

’What have I possibly done to deserve you making a move against me?!’

SQUELCH!

The Ziwei Divine Sword arrived in an instant, plunging directly into the Blood Demon’s head. The aftershocks sent ripples spreading across its wrinkled hide.

In that single moment, every meridian in the Blood Demon’s body was severed. All of its tiny leech Clones were caught in the chain reaction and disintegrated completely.

The leech’s main body instantly shattered.

But even so, its powerful Life Force still gave it a chance to recover.

’It’s still not dead?’

On a distant skyscraper, Qin Yang stood on the rooftop, sensing the Blood Demon’s physical state. Calculating the right moment, he formed a hand seal and silently uttered the word, "Rise."

He began to deliver the finishing blow.

The Divine Sword possessed a spirit of its own. As his word fell, the violet light upon the Ziwei Divine Sword grew even more brilliant.

VMMMM!

The Ziwei Sword, embedded in the Bloodthirsty Leech’s flesh, let out a hum. It echoed in response, singing in harmony with the radiant glow that filled the sky.

The next moment.

The sky darkened as if it were midnight, and countless stars seemed to fall from the heavens. They surrounded the Blood Demon, forming phantoms of sharp swords that sealed it in from every direction.

Elder Li gazed up at the magnificent sight and murmured:

"A Sword Array! The Senior is forming an array to execute the Bloodthirsty Demon Insect!"

Many years ago, he had been fortunate enough to witness a Grandmaster-Level Sword Cultivator forming an Array.

But that one was composed of only a few short swords. In terms of sheer power and scale, it couldn’t even begin to compare to the Ziwei Sword Array before his eyes.

Hearing this, the other Star Martial Artists stared at the Ziwei Sword Array, utterly stunned.

They had never seen such a spectacular sight and were all rooted to the spot in shock.

SLICE! SLICE!

Inside the Sword Array, the Blood Demon had nowhere to flee. It was subjected to the torture of death by a thousand cuts as the immense Sword Qi tore its body apart, dicing its flesh into mincemeat.

The Ziwei Divine Sword erupted with intense heat, instantly vaporizing its blood and the surrounding lake water into white steam. The once-unbeatable Blood Demon was now nothing more than fish on a chopping block, being mercilessly butchered by the Sword Array.

Seeing this, everyone present erupted into a fervent frenzy.

"As expected of a Sword Dao master!!"

"One Sword! It was really just One Sword! These are truly the methods of an Immortal!"

"That must be the Divine Sword that emerged from the valley that day! It’s so beautiful!"

Nearby, Li Zixuan listened to it all in silence, her head held high with pride.

’Hmph! Impressive, right?’

’That’s my master!’

If she hadn’t sworn an oath to Qin Yang on the day she became his disciple, Li Zixuan would have probably blurted out her connection to him by now.

The Chief Commander watched the scene, filled with emotion. "This senior’s strength is astonishing. He’s practically a one-man army."

"Commander, that’s an understatement."

Elder Li shook his head, gazing at the Sword Array with reverence. "That one sword strike could hold off an army of one hundred thousand."

It was a magnificent, world-shaking miracle.

In the livestream chat, thousands of netizens who witnessed the scene left a flood of shocked comments.

"So damn cool! ’His Sword Qi spans thirty thousand miles; one slash of his blade chills nineteen provinces!’"

"Insane! It’s already f*cking over!? Where’s the Immortal? We only see the sword, not the man?!"

"The Blood Demon was strong enough to fight three Innate Realm masters, and it couldn’t even make this expert show his face?"

"The GOAT has spoken! One-shotted it with a single sword! What more is there to say, bros!"

. . . . . .

「Meanwhile.」

Qin Yang stood on the rooftop, his senses extended to Li Shui Lake. Only after confirming that not a single trace of the Bloodthirsty Leech’s life force remained did he let out a long sigh of relief.

’Done and dusted!’

’Finally wiped out the predecessor to the Blood Demon.’

’Pull it out by the roots to prevent future problems.’

’This was the first time I’ve ever encountered such a powerful enemy.’

’It actually took One Sword to eliminate it completely.’

’Well, it’s about time.’

Qin Yang stretched, sat down on the edge of the rooftop, and while recalling the Ziwei, he called out to the system in his mind.

"System, talk to me!"

After a long moment, the system’s terrified voice arrived as if on cue.

"Oh my god, Host, you actually killed it with One Sword—"

"This system bows down to you..."

"Cut the damn crap!" Qin Yang rolled his eyes and cut it off.

"Just hurry up and give me my damn reward!"
