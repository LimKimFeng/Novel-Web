Chapter 199 - 198: The Long-Awaited System Prompt, An Emperor Venerable?

The Jianghai border wall.

Qin Yang floated in mid-air, clad in a black robe.

The Ziwei Divine Sword circled around him, giving him the bearing of an Immortal. The bloody rain parted around him, and a tsunami of cheers roared in his ears.

So strong!

Such sacrifices naturally deserved a reward!

’Jianghai has a thousand-year-old legacy. Heaven helps those who help themselves.’

BOOM—

A gentle spring rain began to fall. A curtain of water spread along the city wall, descending gently upon it.

The curtain of rain, stretching to the heavens, washed away the stench of blood and gore.

The needle-fine spring rain descended slowly, healing the foundations of the thousands of defending soldiers, nourishing everyone’s bodies, and granting all a new lease on life.

But that was not all!

Fortune, it seemed, was born from this disaster...

Bathed in this spring rain,

the lightly injured were completely healed, while the severely wounded gained an opportunity for Body Tempering. Their sundered meridians were rebuilt, stronger and sturdier than before, and their bones and sinews grew tougher!

Lin Mofeng clutched his severed arm, feeling the Spiritual Charm within the spring rain. He was inwardly astounded; as a Grandmaster, he understood what this implied better than anyone.

After all,

the Celestial Dao restores balance by taking from the surplus to mend the deficit.

Now, thanks to this spring rain, everyone on the city wall could not only heal their injuries but also enjoy Body Tempering by the Laws.

After the ordeal of the Beast Tide, everyone was already battered and bruised, in a state of natural repair. For the Sword God to send this spring rain now—wasn’t this the most optimal method for healing and enhancement?!

’What an incredible technique... and the timing is perfect. Did he calculate the exact moment to intervene?’

As this thought crossed his mind,

Lin Mofeng murmured to himself, his gaze turning to Qin Yang in the sky, his heart filled with utter shock.

The Wood Law was profound and extraordinary. When the Jianghai Sword God had his breakthrough, there had been a phenomenon of plants and trees flourishing throughout the city, but witnessing the Law’s healing power firsthand was another matter entirely!

In just a few moments, everyone on the border wall had been restored to their original condition.

Such a potent healing technique was simply too terrifying!

Just then,

an itching sensation spread from his wound.

He glanced to the side and saw new flesh budding from the stump. Torn muscles knitted together, shattered bone mended, and blood vessels regrew.

In less than a moment,

his severed arm grew back from the shoulder.

It was as smooth as jade and as tender as a newborn’s.

"Hmm..."

Lin Mofeng stretched his new arm, clenching his fist experimentally. He suddenly felt a stir in his heart.

It was as if he could faintly touch the Laws...

"Hm?"

’A blessing in disguise?!’
